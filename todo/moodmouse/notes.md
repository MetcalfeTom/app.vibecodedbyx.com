# MoodMouse App

## Log
- Created mood detection app that analyzes mouse movement patterns
- Tracks speed, chaos (angle changes), and energy levels
- 9 different moods: Energized, Anxious, Calm, Focused, Bored, Frustrated, Playful, Tired, Curious
- Custom cursor with trailing effects
- Real-time stats display

## Issues
- Custom cursor only works on desktop (disabled on mobile)
