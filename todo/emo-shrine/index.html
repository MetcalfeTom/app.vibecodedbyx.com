My Manifesto

This is my sanctuary. A place where the darkness isn't feared, but embraced. Where my studded belts clink in harmony with the chains hanging from my baggy jeans. Where my side-swept bangs cover one eye because the world only deserves to see half of my pain.

I'm not like other people. I feel things deeper. My soul is an ocean of sorrow, swimming in a sea of misunderstood poetry written in the margins of my geometry homework. My tribal arm tattoos tell stories that my words cannot.

Society wants me to conform. But I'll stay here, in my room, listening to Taking Back Sunday on repeat, wondering if anyone will ever truly understand the complex tapestry of emotions I experience while standing in the mall Hot Topic at 7 PM on a Thursday.
