# Boop Gallery

Click anthropomorphic animal noses to boop them before they disappear!

## log
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. 8 animals (Cat, Dog, Bunny, Fox, Bear, Frog, Pig, Owl), each hand-drawn with canvas (ears, eyes, nose, mouth, unique features). 30-second timer, combo scoring (10 × combo multiplier), accuracy tracking. Nose glow hint, pop-in/out animations, blink cycle, warning flash when about to vanish. Heart + star particle FX on boop, floating "BOOP!" text. Spawn rate accelerates over time. End screen with score, best combo, accuracy, missed count. Fredoka + Silkscreen typography, deep purple/pink neon aesthetic. Custom pink cursor.

## features
- 8 unique canvas-drawn anthropomorphic animals
- Click/tap nose to boop — hitbox around nose area
- 30-second timed rounds
- Combo multiplier (10pts × combo)
- Heart and star particle effects on successful boop
- Pop-in entrance animation, shrink exit
- Blink animation cycle
- Warning flash when critter is about to vanish
- Nose glow indicator pulsing around target
- Spawning speeds up as round progresses
- End screen with stats (score, combo, accuracy, misses)
- Mobile touch support
- Custom pink boop cursor
