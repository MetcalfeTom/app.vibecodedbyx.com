# Mutation Lab

Cybernetic eye tracking the mouse with floating DNA strands.

## log
- 2026-03-18: Initial build. Giant cybernetic eye in center tracks mouse — iris fibers, pupil contracts when mouse approaches, crosshair overlay, scanning ring, blink cycle. Circuit traces radiate from eye with nodes. DNA double helix strands scroll vertically with sinusoidal backbone, base pair rungs, nucleotide dots, mutated pairs glow red. Floating particles repelled by mouse cursor. Subtle grid overlay, corner brackets, status readout (subject position, DNA integrity, mutation count, threat level). Motion trail via transparent fill. Custom cursor. Rajdhani + Share Tech Mono typography, green-on-black biotech aesthetic.
