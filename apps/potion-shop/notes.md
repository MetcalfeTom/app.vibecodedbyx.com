# Potion Shop Tycoon

Brew magical potions and sell them to wizards, goblins, and dragons. Build your potion empire!

## log
- 2026-04-13: Initial build. 8 ingredients, 12 recipes across 3 tiers, 10 customer creature types. Ingredient selection fills cauldron slots (start with 2, upgradeable to 5). Animated cauldron with color-mixing liquid, bubbles, and steam. Customers arrive with patience timers, wanting specific potions at varied prices based on creature type. Selling earns gold + reputation, tips from generous creatures. 5 upgrade paths (cauldron size, quality, customer attract rate, haggle bonus, bulk discount). Reputation unlocks higher-tier creatures and recipes. Day cycle every 60s. Activity log. Cinzel + Crimson Pro typography, dark apothecary aesthetic.

## features
- 8 magical ingredients with costs
- 12 potion recipes (3 tiers of rarity)
- Animated cauldron with color-mixing, bubbles, steam
- 10 customer creature types (wizard, goblin, elf, dragon envoy, fairy queen, demon lord, etc.)
- Customer patience timers — serve fast or lose reputation
- Variable pricing based on creature type (goblins haggle, dragons pay well)
- Tip system based on creature generosity
- 5 upgrades: cauldron size, brew quality, customer attract, haggle, bulk discount
- Reputation system unlocking higher-tier customers
- Day/night cycle (60s days)
- Gold economy with ingredient costs and potion sales
- Inventory management
- Activity log
- Mobile responsive layout
