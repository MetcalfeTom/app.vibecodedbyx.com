# neon-derby

## log
- 2026-05-27: Initial build per thecrazygamer16's "actually make it 3D" pivot. **three.js r128** chase-cam horse race. 5 hand-built neon horses race down a 1200-unit grid track to a glowing finish-line gate; player picks one + bets $5-100 from a $100 bank that persists in localStorage. **Horse model** (~30 primitives per horse): rounded body (1.4×0.9×2.6 box, hue-emissive at 0.45 intensity), tilted neck box, head box, smaller darker muzzle cube, 2 cone ears, 2 spherical white eyes, 6-segment hue-shifted mane along the neck (each ridge a small box rotated -0.3 + t·-0.15 rad), thin tail box that animates, 4 leg boxes that rotate on X-axis during gallop, 4 white hoof spheres that lift on opposing diagonal phases (front-left + back-right vs front-right + back-left — true canter-gallop pattern), plus a large additive-blended emissive aura sphere that pulses opacity 0.08-0.20 with speed. **Track**: 80×1400 dark ground plane + GridHelper (400×400 with 80 divisions, magenta/cyan lines, 45% opacity) + 6 lane separator thin glowing planes between 5 lanes (4 units apart) + cone+halo markers every 50 units on both sides + finish-line strip + 2 magenta pillar gates + gold double-sided banner mesh at z=1200. **Skybox**: custom GLSL shader on a backface-rendered 400-unit sphere with three color stops (top void / mid violet / bottom magenta) blended by world Y via smoothstep. Distant gold sun at z=1480, soft `THREE.Fog` from 35 to 220 in violet for atmospheric depth. **Lighting**: ambient 0.5 + magenta directional key + cyan directional fill. **Camera**: chase 11 units behind, 5.5 up, eased toward `(laneX·0.4, 5.5, playerZ-11)` per frame, lookAt `(laneX, 1.8, playerZ+6)`. Adds random shake ±mashRate·0.04 when player is mashing fast (mash event timestamps tracked in a 700ms sliding window). **Race flow**: bet panel → 3-2-1 countdown with per-second beeps + color flashes + GO bell (5-note arpeggio + noise burst) → racing phase. Each horse has runtime state `{x, vel, stam, legPhase, finished, finishTime, laneX, burstCD}`. **Mash mechanic**: each Space (or tap on the GALLOP button) adds `accel·8·max(0.4, stam)` to player velocity (capped at `def.top`), depletes stam by 0.045. Stam regens 0.10/sec passively; if stam < 0.06, mash gives only +1.5 mini-impulse. Player velocity decays toward `def.base·0.85` at 0.9/sec when not mashing; if stam exhausted, velocity decays an extra ×0.25 per second. **AI horses**: drift toward `def.base + sin(raceTime·1.3 + laneX)·1.4` via `accel`-scaled lerp + small noise; have a `burstCD` random timer (1.5-4s) that triggers a +2-5 velocity boost; weak-stamina horses (low `def.stam`) have a per-frame `0.04·(1-stam)` probability per second of "tripping" (velocity ×0.85). **5 horses** with hand-balanced odds: Volt Runner (cyan, top 44, stam 0.36, 2:1), Magenta Bolt (magenta, top 41, stam 0.55, 3:1), Goldfire (gold, top 38, stam 0.80, 4:1 — the workhorse), Lime Streak (lime, top 48, stam 0.28, 5:1 — fast but trippy), Violet Velvet (violet, top 40, stam 0.65, 6:1 longshot). **Bet panel UI**: 5 horse cards in radiogroup (swatch + name + 5-star spd/stam rating + odds), bet slider $5-100 (clamped to bank), live payout calc (bet × odds), PLACE BET button. **Racing HUD**: bank / position (1-5) / time cards at top, stamina bar at bottom with green-to-red gradient and "low" class flip when stam<0.3, GALLOP touch button on mobile. **Result panel**: medal-ranked list of all 5 finishers with player row highlighted lime + their finish time, win payout in lime / loss in magenta, NEXT RACE button. **Audio** (Web Audio synth): hoof = 110→50Hz sine + 1200Hz bandpass noise burst; countdown beeps = triangle pitch tones; start bell = 4-note ascending triangle arpeggio + bandpass noise; win = 5-note C-major ascending; lose = saw + square dirge. **Defensive init**: matchMedia + canvas + every handler in try/catch, magenta error toast on window.error + on `THREE === undefined`. JSDOM smoke test correctly shows the error toast firing on WebGL-unavailable environments (expected — JSDOM has no WebGL). **Aesthetic**: Bowlby One SC cyan→magenta→gold gradient title, Audiowide pad/button labels, Major Mono Display tag, Share Tech Mono body, Rubik Mono One numerals. **WCAG basics**: rem units, semantic main/section/dialog, role=radiogroup on horse cards with role=radio + aria-checked + tabindex=0 + keyboard space/enter, aria-live on race prompt + result, aria-pressed on mute, focus-visible 3px outlines, ≥2.75rem touch targets, prefers-reduced-motion damps dt ×0.85, full keyboard support for Space mash + Tab navigation through bet UI.

- 2026-05-27 (later): Animation upgrade per chat ("option B"). Restructured `buildHorseModel` to nest upper-body parts in a `torso` group, with a `neckPivot` at the chest-neck joint and a `headPivot` at the top of the neck. Legs + hooves stay at the root `g` level so they remain grounded; everything from chest up to ears now rotates as a chain. **New per-frame motion** computed from `velRatio = clamp(vel/topSpeed)`: (1) **Forward lean** — `torso.rotation.x` eases toward `-velRatio·0.21` (~12° forward pitch at top speed) plus a tiny `sin(legPhase·4)·0.015·velRatio` stride bob mixed in; lateral bank `sin(legPhase·2)·0.04·velRatio` adds gallop sway via `torso.rotation.z`. (2) **Neck pump** — `neckPivot.rotation.x` = `-velRatio·0.18 + sin(legPhase·4 + π·0.3)·(0.10 + velRatio·0.10)` — neck stretches down-and-forward when sprinting and pumps in sync with the front-hoof plant. Tiny `sin(legPhase·2 + 0.7)·0.04·velRatio` lateral sway on `rotation.y`. (3) **Head wobble** — `headPivot` adds an extra micro-bob `sin(legPhase·4 + π·0.55)·(0.08 + velRatio·0.08)` slightly out of phase with the neck so the head reads as a separate floating mass (the classic horse head-wobble). Lateral `rotation.z` tilt scales with velocity too. (4) **Mane flutter** — each mane ridge stores its baseX and shifts sideways with `sin(legPhase·5 − mi·0.6)·0.07·velRatio` to produce a traveling wave from neck base to head. **Aura + tail still on torso** so they tilt with the body. Eases use `dt·5/8/10` lerp rates so motion is smooth without snapping at low frame rates. Net effect: at sprint speed each horse pumps its head forward and down with the stride, neck swings, mane flutters in the wind direction, body leans into the wind — reads as a real galloping silhouette without adding any geometry (still ~30 primitives per horse).

## issues
- Procedural horse model is chunky (cuboid limbs, not articulated joints with knees) — fits the neon arcade vibe but won't impress horse anatomy purists. Acceptable tradeoff for build time + load weight.
- All horses share the same `legPhase * 0.35` cycle scaling — at very high velocities the leg animation can look slightly out of sync with the speed. Acceptable.
- Three.js loaded from cdnjs CDN — first load needs network. Cached after.
- AI rubber-banding is intentional but subtle; if it feels too predictable, increase the `Math.random() - 0.5` noise range in updateRace.
