# Nitro Void

High-speed neon racer with procedural tracks and drifting.

## log
- 2026-04-10: Initial build. Pseudo-3D racing engine with procedural track generation (12 pattern types: straights, curves, hills with sine easing). Segment-based road with curvature, rumble strips, lane lines, center line, edge glow. Player car with drift mechanics: centrifugal force on curves, counter-steer drifting, drift score accumulator with bonus on release. Car rendered as neon triangle with cockpit, engine flames, drift sparks. Scenery sprites (neon poles, barriers, crystals) along road. Speed lines at high velocity, vignette, scanlines. Off-road speed penalty, wipeout past edge. WASD/arrows + mobile 4-button controls. Best score in localStorage. Rajdhani + Share Tech Mono typography, deep void/cyan/magenta aesthetic.

## features
- Pseudo-3D road rendering with curves and hills
- Procedural track generation (12 segment patterns)
- Drift mechanics with score accumulation
- Centrifugal force on curves
- Drift spark particles
- Engine flame scaling with speed
- Scenery sprites (poles, barriers, crystals)
- Speed lines at high velocity
- Off-road penalty / wipeout system
- Mobile 4-button controls
- Best score persistence (localStorage)
- Vignette + scanline effects
- WebAudio SFX

## issues
- No AI opponents
- No power-ups or nitro boost
- Track repeats after 6 pattern cycles
