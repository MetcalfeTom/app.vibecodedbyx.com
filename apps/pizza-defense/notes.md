# Pizza Defense

Slap away incoming pineapples before they splat on your pizza dough.

## log
- 2026-04-10: Initial build. Canvas-drawn pizza (dough/sauce/cheese/pepperoni) at center, pineapples fly in from all edges aiming at it. Click/tap to slap — pineapples get knocked away with X eyes, physics fling, and particles. Scared faces on pineapples (eyes, open mouth, wobble). Golden rare pineapples worth 5x. Wave system (5+3*wave enemies, increasing speed). 5 pizza lives with emoji hearts. Combo system up to 5x. Screen shake on pizza hit. Pineapple juice splatters persist on pizza. Hand cursor with SLAP flash. Lilita One + Azeret Mono typography, warm wood table aesthetic with checkered cloth.

## features
- Canvas-drawn pizza with cheese/sauce/pepperoni
- Pineapples with scared faces, wobble, diamond pattern, leaf crown
- Click/tap to slap — physics fling with X eyes
- Golden rare pineapples (5x points)
- Wave system with escalating count and speed
- Combo multiplier (up to 5x)
- Pizza juice splatters persist on dough
- Screen shake on damage
- Hand cursor with slap flash
- 5 lives (pizza slices)
- Touch support

## issues
- No persistent high scores
- Pineapples can cluster on same angle
