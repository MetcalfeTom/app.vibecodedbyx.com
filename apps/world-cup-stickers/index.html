How to use

  • Click a sticker to mark it owned · click again to unmark.
  • Shift+click adds a duplicate (for trading).
  • Alt+click (or right-click) removes one duplicate.
  • Use the filter chips to show only missing, dupes, or shiny foils.
  • Search by team name, player name, or sticker number.
  • / focuses the search bar · ↑/↓ cycle teams.
  • Your collection is saved locally — export the JSON to back it up.
