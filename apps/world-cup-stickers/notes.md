# Pitch Album '26 · World Cup Sticker Tracker

## log
- 2026-05-24: initial release — full Panini-style sticker album for the 2026 World Cup. 48 teams across all FIFA confederations (3 hosts + 6 CONMEBOL + 16 UEFA + 9 CAF + 8 AFC + 1 OFC + 5 inter-confederation playoff winners). 19 stickers per team (1 foil badge + captain + 17 numbered players based on POSITIONS array: 1 GK / 4 DEF / 4 MID / 3 FWD / 6 reserves). 15 tournament-specials extras (trophy + tournament logo as foils, 3 mascots, 10 stadiums spread across the 3 host nations). Total: 927 stickers, numbered sequentially #1..#927 (extras first, then teams in TEAMS array order). State persisted to `localStorage['world-cup-stickers-v1']` as `{owned: {[num]: bool}, dupes: {[num]: count}, currentTeam, filter, query}`. Click sticker toggles owned, shift+click adds duplicate (auto-marks owned if not already), alt/right-click decrements dupe. Filters: all / owned / missing / dupes / foils. Search hits team name, position, player slot, and #num. Left rail shows conference groupings with per-team progress (`X/19`); right sheet shows the selected team's 19-grid. Global progress card top-right shows `owned/927 + %` + dupes count + gold gradient bar. Export/import JSON for backup. Keyboard: `/` focuses search, `?` opens help dialog, `↑/↓` cycle teams (wraps), Enter/Space toggle on focused sticker. Aesthetic: Panini-album cream paper background (radial green pitch glow + gold corner glow + 2-layer paper fiber grain), pitch-green top stripe on sheet, gold-foil accent line under masthead, chunky black borders + 4px offset block shadows on cards. Foils animate via 4s `shimmer` keyframe over a 4-color background-position cycle (yellow→pink→cyan→mint), suppressed when missing or under `prefers-reduced-motion`. Fonts: Bungee for title + big numerals, Anton for headers + chips, IBM Plex Sans for body, IBM Plex Mono for stats, Silkscreen for sticker #s. WCAG 2.1 AA basics: rem-everywhere, `role="status" aria-live="polite"` on progress + sheet + toast, `aria-pressed` on filter chips + stickers, `aria-current` on selected team, ≥2.75rem (44px) touch targets, focus-visible 3px gold outline, prefers-reduced-motion kills shimmer + transitions, semantic `<main>`/`<header>`/`<nav>`/`<section>`/`<button type="button">`/`<dialog>`. Mobile collapses rail to 14rem max-height and stacks above sheet at ≤900px.

## issues
- No real player names used — only positional labels ("Captain", "#2 Defender", etc) to avoid licensing/naming issues. If users want real rosters, that would need a curated dataset per team.
- 48 teams is a guess at the 2026 lineup — actual qualified teams won't be finalized until 2026. Playoff slot teams (Chile/Peru/Costa Rica/Panama/Jamaica) are placeholders.
- Foil shimmer animates continuously on all visible foil stickers — could become busy on small screens with many foils visible. Reduced-motion kills it; might want a global "static foils" toggle later.
