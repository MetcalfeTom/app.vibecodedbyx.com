HOW TO PLAY

Stack blocks of 2 and 3 until the total equals the target number. Click a block on the stack to pop it off.

Target: 7
Stack: 2 + 2 + 3 = 7 ✓ (3 blocks)
Or: 3 + 2 + 2 = 7 ✓ (3 blocks)

Try to use the fewest blocks possible — that's the real challenge. Every number ≥ 2 can be made with just 2s and 3s!

Tip: 3s are more efficient. Use 2s to fill the gap. For any target, the optimal uses as many 3s as possible.
