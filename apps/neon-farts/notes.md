# neon-farts

## log
- 2026-05-27: Initial build. **Distinct from gas-giant** (the 9-preset fart soundboard built earlier this session) — neon-farts is fully **procedural**: every press generates a fresh, never-repeating fart by sampling parameters from the family's range. No two presses sound identical. **Six families**, each a parameter-space template: BRASS (saw through dual bandpass formants, 140-220Hz base + ±30Hz slide + 4-7 Q formants); WET (sawtooth 75-115Hz with heavy 12-22Hz wobble LFO 22-42 depth + 240-420Hz bandpass + crackle noise bus); SQUEAK (high square 320-540Hz with 18-32Hz vibrato + 600-900Hz bandpass); SUB (sine 50-75Hz + saw harmonic through lowpass 180-280Hz + low crackle transient); BUBBLE (5-9 random pitch-step sawtooth between 60-260Hz + 3-7 sine pop overlays at 200-600Hz); GHOST (bandpassed-noise sweep 1800-2600 → 300-500Hz + sub whisper tone). `sampleParams(ranges)` samples uniform random values within each range per press → genuinely unique waveform. **5-tier rarity system**: common 50% (silver, no modifier), uncommon 30% (lime, ×1.0-1.15 duration), rare 15% (magenta, ×1.1-1.35 dur + extraWobble modifier doubles LFO rate+depth on wet/squeak), epic 4% (violet, ×1.2-1.5 dur + tanh waveshaper distortion mix), legendary 1% (gold, ×1.4-1.8 dur + 2.4s convolution reverb send at 55% wet). Rolled with weighted random `rollRarity()`. Legendary pops a gold "⭐ LEGENDARY ⭐" banner with cubic-bezier overshoot pop; epic pops a violet "◆ EPIC ◆" banner. **HUD stats** persist in localStorage: total unique generated + rare count (rare+epic) + legendary count. **Last-fart card** in viz overlay shows family name, base frequency, duration, and rarity tag with color-matched glow. **Live waveform viz** at top: full-width canvas with 3-pass chromatic-aberration oscilloscope (pink/cyan/white at ±1px offsets in `lighter` blend mode) hooked to the master AnalyserNode (fftSize 2048, smoothing 0.5). **Mash mode**: 🌪 button toggles auto-fire loop with random family + random params at 180-360ms intervals; banner-mode animation pulses while active. **🎲 Roll Unique** = single random family + single random fart. **⭐ Force Legend** = guarantees a legendary roll on a random family (for chat to actually hear the reverbed monster without grinding). **Audio architecture**: 0.85 master gain → DynamicsCompressor (−10 dBFS, 4:1) → AnalyserNode → destination. Procedural reverb impulse: 2-channel 2.4s noise with `(1-i/len)^2.2` decay. **Defensive init**: every step in try/catch, audioCtx on-demand (autoplay policy), magenta error toast. **WCAG basics**: rem units, semantic main/h1, role=group on grids, aria-pressed on mash+mute, aria-label on every button, aria-live on legendary banner, focus-visible 3px outlines, ≥2.5rem touch targets, prefers-reduced-motion no-ops mash-button pulse. Keyboard: 1-6 fire family, R = roll unique, M = toggle mash, L = force legendary. **Aesthetic**: Bowlby One SC cyan→magenta→orange gradient title, Audiowide pad/button labels, Major Mono Display tag, Share Tech Mono body. Per-family hue-coded borders/glows (gold/cyan/pink/magenta/lime/violet).

## issues
- Mash mode at minimum 180ms interval can stack 5+ voices/sec — the −10dB compressor catches peaks but heavy mashing may cause audible pumping. Intentional flavor; could add a max-poly cap.
- BRASS family with extreme `slideTo` (±30Hz semitone-ish offset) sometimes produces near-non-fart-like brass blats. Acceptable as part of the "unique" rolling.
- The 5-tier rarity rolls are independent per press — no pity timer, so a user might never roll legendary in 100 presses (expected ~1 in 100). The Force Legend button exists for guaranteed exposure.
