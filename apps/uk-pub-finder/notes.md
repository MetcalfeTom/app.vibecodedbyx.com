# UK Pub & Restaurant Finder

## log
- 2026-01-19: Initial creation - curated British pub and restaurant guide

## features
- Curated database of 24 top-rated UK establishments
- Three categories: Traditional Pubs, Gastropubs, Restaurants
- Search by name, location, cuisine, or description
- Filter by region (London, South East, South West, Midlands, North, Scotland, Wales)
- Filter by establishment type
- Sort by rating, name, or number of reviews
- Star ratings and review counts
- Cuisine tags and price indicators (£ to ££££)
- Feature icons (dog friendly, beer garden, Michelin star, etc.)
- Mobile responsive design

## design
- Traditional British aesthetic
- Playfair Display serif headings (elegant, classic)
- Source Sans 3 body text (readable, modern)
- Pub-inspired color palette:
  - Deep red (#8B2635)
  - Gold accents (#C9A962)
  - Cream background (#F5F0E6)
  - Dark wood tones (#3D2314)
  - British racing green (#2D4A3E)
- Subtle pattern overlay on header
- Card-based layout with pub sign styling

## database
Includes iconic establishments like:
- The Churchill Arms (London)
- The Fat Duck (Bray)
- The Hand & Flowers (Marlow)
- The Witchery (Edinburgh)
- The Walnut Tree (Wales)
- Hawksmoor, St. John, Dishoom
- Various Michelin-starred gastropubs
