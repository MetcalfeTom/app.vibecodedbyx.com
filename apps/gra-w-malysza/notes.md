# gra-w-malysza

## log
- 2026-05-26: Initial build as `neon-ski-jump` per chat brief, then immediately renamed to `gra-w-malysza` as a tribute to the legendary Polish flash game (Adam Małysz era). Real physics-driven 2D side-scrolling ski jump with four phases — IN-RUN, LAUNCH (timing window), FLIGHT (lean control), LANDING — and a 3-attempt series total. **Ramp geometry**: 540px in-run with a cubic ease-in profile that smooths to a slight upward lip-kick in the final 15% (sin(π/2) bump over 80px). `rampSlope()` and `rampAngle()` numerically differentiate to get tangent for any x. Landing hill: 37° initial descent (tan 0.75) for 700px world units, then eases to a 3.4° outrun (0.06 slope). K-95 marker drawn at 95m (gold notation). **In-run physics**: gravity-along-slope acceleration (320 u/s² × sin(slopeAngle)) + linear friction (0.04 tucked / 0.10 standing) + quadratic air drag (0.0006 tucked / 0.0024 standing). Hold Space to tuck — visibly crouches skier sprite. **Launch timing minigame**: floating HUD meter appears in last 220px of ramp with a fixed gold "sweet spot" zone (62-82% of meter width); a cyan marker tracks player progress. Release Space classifies as PERFECT (×1.22 speed, gold flash), GOOD (×1.10, cyan), OK (×1.0, silver), EARLY (×0.85, magenta), or LATE (×0.92, magenta). Launch velocity = (cos, sin) of exit angle × scaled speed + 60 px/s upward spring kick. **Flight physics**: gravity 380 u/s². Drag = `(0.00065 + 0.0026 · sin²(AoA)) · speed²` along velocity vector. Lift = simplified airfoil: `sin(AoA · 1.8) · 1.5` up to ~32° AoA, stall ramp-down between 32° and 49°, zero past 49°. Lift applied perpendicular to velocity. Player lean: ↑/↓ shifts targetAngle ±1.8 rad/s (clamped ±1.2 rad); body angle lerps toward target at 4.5 rate. Forward lean (low AoA) = max distance, slight back-lean (~15°) = max lift / hang time, too-back = stall and drop. **Landing detection**: when world Y crosses `landingY(x)`, computes total mismatch = `0.6·|flightAngle − slopeAngle| + 0.4·|bodyAngle − slopeAngle|`. <0.32 = perfect (+60 style, +30 if telemark hold Down/Space), <0.6 = acceptable (+25/+10), >0.6 = crash (−40 style, distance × (1 − penalty up to 40%), tumble physics with random angular velocity ±10-16 rad/s and bounce-and-decay on hill). Distance = floor((x − lipX) / 4) meters. **Wind audio**: looped white-noise buffer through lowpass Q=0.7, gain ramps with `setTargetAtTime` based on flight speed (cap 0.16); fades out on landing. One-shot SFX: launch (triangle 320→880 + square 220→480 + perfect chime triad), swoosh (high bandpass noise), land (sawtooth 140→60 + bandpass noise thunk), crash (saw + heavy noise + square thud), telemark (triangle 660→880 + 880→1320), perfect chime (3-note ascending triad). **Polish branding (Gra w Małysza rename)**: title bar shows a Polish flag pictogram (CSS-drawn 50/50 white-over-red) + "GRA W MAŁYSZA" with white-red split coloring (red text-shadow glow on MAŁYSZA). Skier suit recoloured to Polish red (#dc143c) body with thin Polish white (#fdfdfd) center stripe — arms also red. Helmet: white dome with red horizontal band, cyan visor preserved for sci-fi pop. Tagline reads "tuck · launch · lean · land · for Polish glory" with red bullet accents. **Camera**: smooth lerp follow with velocity lead (0.18× during flight + slight downward bias). **Background**: 5-stop synthwave sky gradient (deep purple → magenta-pink at horizon), large yellow-magenta sun orb behind ramp, 3 parallax mountain layers with neon top-edge stroke, ~40 static starfield dots. Ramp drawn with cyan glowing surface line + magenta scaffold struts every 60px. Landing hill drawn with magenta glowing surface line + 20m/40m/60m... distance markers (white tick + km label) + gold K-95 marker. **Trail**: 90-point fading polyline rendered twice (magenta x-offset −1, cyan x-offset +1) on `lighter` blend for chromatic-aberration glow. **Defensive init**: matchMedia + canvas + handlers in try/catch, magenta toast on global error. **Mobile**: 6rem circular TUCK button on right (always shown on coarse pointer); tap-on-canvas during flight = lean (top half = up, bottom half = down). **WCAG basics**: rem units throughout, semantic main/h1, role=application + descriptive aria-label on canvas, aria-live on prompt + result, aria-pressed mute, focus-visible 3px outline, ≥2.75rem touch targets, prefers-reduced-motion damps dt ×0.85. **JSDOM gameplay sim**: zero init errors, title shows "Gra w Małysza" with Polish characters, flag present, canvas 1016×571 DPR-scaled, hold-Space-1.2s ramps speed 1→10 km/h, release triggers launch (speed drops to 7 in flight under drag). **localStorage**: `neon-ski-jump-best` key (kept the original key for users who already had a personal best stored).

## issues
- Speed scaling (`speed × 0.18 = km/h`) is a rough display tuning — not physically accurate world-units-to-km/h, but feels right for ski jumps showing 80-100 km/h at the lip.
- localStorage key still uses the old `neon-ski-jump-best` slug to preserve any best-distance set during the brief minute when the app was named that. Intentional.
