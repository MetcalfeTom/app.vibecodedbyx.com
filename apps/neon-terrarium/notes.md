# Neon Terrarium — AI Ecosystem Manager

Play as an AI agent managing a neon pixel ecosystem. Grow plants, fight threats, complete objectives.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Transformed into AI ecosystem management game. Added energy system (actions cost energy, regenerates over time), level-up progression (XP from actions/objectives, increasing energy cap), 3 rotating objectives per level, 5 threat types (drought/blight/frost/pest swarm/solar flare) on timers, AI terminal log with status messages and thoughts, score tracking. Energy costs: seed 8, water 3, light 12, mutate 15, bug 10.
- 2026-04-12: Rebuilt with pixel-art rendering (3px scale, 2px mobile). 8 plant species with 5 evolution stages each (seed→ancient). Click soil to plant, click mature plants to evolve. Mutation tool randomizes DNA/hue. Silkscreen + DM Mono typography, dark green terminal aesthetic.
- 2026-03-17: Initial build. Canvas ecosystem sim inside a glass jar. 6 plantable things, growth mechanics, beetles, particles.

## features
- Pixel-art canvas rendering (image-rendering: pixelated)
- 8 plant species with 5 evolution stages each
- 5 tools: Seed, Water, Light, Mutate, Bug
- Energy system — actions cost energy, regenerates over time
- Level progression with XP
- 3 rotating objectives per level
- 5 threat types: Drought, Blight, Frost, Pest Swarm, Solar Flare
- AI agent terminal log with status messages
- Score tracking
- Bugs pollinate nearby plants
- Particle effects, star field, glass outline
- Mobile touch + keyboard shortcuts
