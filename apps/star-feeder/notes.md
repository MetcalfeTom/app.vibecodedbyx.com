# Star Feeder

Catch falling neon stars and feed them to a growing digital nebula.

## log
- 2026-04-17: Initial build. Arc-shaped catcher follows mouse/touch. Falling neon stars with rotation, wobble, and 4-6 points. 6 star colors. Rare triple-value gold-ring stars. Catching feeds energy trails to central nebula. 7 nebula evolution stages (Spark Dust → Eternal Radiance) with growing particle clouds and shifting hues. Combo system with escalating catch sounds (pitch rises with combo). 5 lives, screen shake on miss. Difficulty ramps with score (faster spawns, extra stars). WebAudio synth catches and miss sounds. Background starfield. Megrim + Azeret Mono typography, deep cosmic purple aesthetic.

## features
- Arc catcher follows mouse/touch with smoothing
- 6 neon star colors with glow and white core
- 4/5/6-pointed star shapes with rotation and wobble
- Rare triple-value stars with gold ring indicator
- 7 nebula evolution stages based on energy fed
- Nebula grows with radial glow layers and orbiting particles
- Energy trail particles from catch point to nebula
- Combo system: 3+ consecutive catches shown, pitch escalates
- Score popups (+10, +30) at catch point
- 5 lives with screen shake on miss
- Difficulty ramp: faster spawns, more stars at higher scores
- WebAudio: rising pitch catches, descending miss sound
- Background starfield with twinkling
- Start screen and game over with stats
