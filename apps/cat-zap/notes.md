# CAT-ZAP - Neon Laser Chase

## log
- 2026-01-08: Initial creation
  - Neon laser pointer controlled by mouse/touch
  - Cats with personality chase the laser
  - Fish treats to collect
  - Level system spawns more cats
  - Cat excitement affects behavior and pupil dilation

## features
- Red neon laser pointer with trail effect
- Cats with animated eyes, ears, and wagging tails
- Cat pupils dilate based on excitement level
- Fish-shaped treats with sparkle effect
- Level progression (more cats each level)
- Particle effects on treat collection
- Score tracking
- Grid background pattern

## controls
- Mouse: move to control laser
- Touch: drag to control laser

## cat behavior
- Cats chase the laser frantically
- Excitement increases with laser movement speed
- Pupils dilate when calm, narrow when excited
- Random pounce behavior when close to laser
- Tails wag faster when excited
- Bounce off walls

## design
- Deep dark background
- Red laser with glow effects
- Green glowing cat eyes
- Yellow fish treats
- Magenta particles and effects
- Orbitron + Exo 2 fonts
