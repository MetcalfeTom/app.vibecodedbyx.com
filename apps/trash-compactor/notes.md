# Neon Trash Compactor

## log
- 2026-01-13: Created neon trash compactor with draggable emotes and crushing press

## features
- Drag emotes from source panel into compactor bin
- Industrial-style crushing press with hydraulics
- Warning stripe hazard tape styling
- Crush button activates the press animation
- Emotes squish and create particle effects
- Compressed cube ejects after crushing
- Sound effects (drop, crush, cube eject)
- Stats tracking: emotes crushed, cubes made
- 30 common emotes to drag
- Double-click bin to add random emote
- Space bar shortcut to crush
- Stats persist in localStorage
- Sound toggle
- Neon industrial aesthetic
- Mobile touch support
