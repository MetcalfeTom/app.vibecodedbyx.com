# One Dim Dungeon

1D side-scrolling action game — you are a single pixel on a line, slashing through waves of neon enemies.

## log
- 2026-04-11: Initial build. Player pixel moves left/right on a single horizontal line, slash attack with neon arc trail. 4 enemy types (grunt=square, brute=diamond, phantom=flickering circle, skull=triangle) with distinct behaviors. Combo system with multiplier up to ×10, slow-motion on ×5+ combos. 3 HP with i-frames, screen shake on hit/death. Depth tracking as you move right. Difficulty scales with depth (faster enemies, more spawns, higher HP). Afterimage trail, slash glow, kill particles, ambient floating particles. Depth markers on the floor line. Mobile touch controls. Silkscreen + Share Tech Mono typography, deep crimson neon aesthetic.

## features
- Single-pixel player on a 1D line
- Slash attack with golden arc and trail effects
- 4 enemy types: grunt, brute, phantom, skull
- Combo system with score multiplier
- Slow-motion on big combos (×5+)
- Screen shake on damage
- 3 HP with invincibility frames
- Progressive difficulty (speed, count, enemy HP scale with depth)
- Afterimage trail on player movement
- Neon particle explosions on kills
- Depth counter and floor markers
- Mobile touch controls (left/right/attack)
- Keyboard: arrows/WASD + space/K to slash
