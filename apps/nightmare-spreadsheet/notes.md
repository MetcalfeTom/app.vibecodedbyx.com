# Nightmare Spreadsheet

Satirical spreadsheet app styled as "TaxCalc Pro 97" — a Windows 95-era tax form nightmare with grey cells, absurd tax regulations, and escalating penalties.

## log
- 2026-04-11: Initial build. Full Win95 aesthetic (title bar, menu bar, toolbar, formula bar, status bar, outset/inset borders). Form 1040-QZ with 7 sections: Gross Income, Adjustments, Tax Computation, Penalties & Interest, Schedule QZ-7 Reconciliation, Depreciation of Sanity, Signature. Every menu triggers an absurd error dialog. Clippy-style assistant with 12 tax nightmare tips. Random popup interruptions every 25-60s. Penalties increase on any interaction. Cancel button spawns another error. Editable cells that generate more errors. Recalc button adds penalties and flashes random cells red. Courier Prime font, #c0c0c0 grey palette. 80-row × 16-column grid.

## features
- Authentic Win95 UI chrome (title bar, menus, toolbar, scrollbars)
- Form 1040-QZ with contradictory/circular instructions
- 7 tax form sections with escalating absurdity
- Penalties auto-increase on every action
- Clippy-style popup helper with 12 despair tips
- Random compliance/session/update popups
- Editable cells that generate new errors
- Recalculate button that makes everything worse
- Cancel button that triggers MORE errors
- Formula bar with incomprehensible formulas
- Status bar tracking errors, warnings, penalties
- Section F: "Depreciation of Sanity" (hours, coffee, crying)
