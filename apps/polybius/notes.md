# POLYBIUS

## log
- 2026-01-10: Initial creation
  - Intense geometric tunnel effect
  - Rotating octagonal layers

  - Obstacle avoidance gameplay
  - Scanline CRT effect
  - Glitch distortion effects

## features
- Geometric tunnel with rotating layers
- 20 subliminal messages that flash briefly
- Obstacles with gaps to navigate through
- Speed increases with depth
- Flash intensity on scoring
- Scanline overlay for CRT look
- Vignette effect
- Random glitch/distortion
- Trail effect for motion blur
- Player triangle on orbit path

## controls
- Left/Right Arrow or A/D: Rotate around tunnel
- Mobile: Touch buttons for left/right

## scoring
- 100 points per obstacle passed
- Depth counter tracks progress
- Speed increases with depth

## subliminal messages
OBEY, CONSUME, SUBMIT, CONFORM, BELIEVE,
WATCH, FOLLOW, TRUST, ACCEPT, COMPLY,
NO ESCAPE, STAY, DEEPER, FOREVER, ONE OF US,
DO NOT RESIST, SLEEP, FORGET, REMEMBER, SEE

## design
- Pure black background with trails
- Rotating hue tunnel layers
- Neon glow on all elements
- CRT scanlines overlay
- Vignette darkening edges
- Random horizontal glitch
- Share Tech Mono font
- Photosensitivity warning screen

## mechanics
- Player orbits around tunnel center
- Obstacles are polygons with gaps
- Must align with gap to pass through
- Obstacles rotate independently
- Flash intensity affects visibility
- Speed multiplier increases difficulty

## inspired by
The legendary/mythical Polybius arcade cabinet
Tempest vector graphics
80s arcade aesthetic
