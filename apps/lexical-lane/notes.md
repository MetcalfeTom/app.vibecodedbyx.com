# Lexical Lane

Type words to drive your neon car down an endless highway. Faster typing = faster speed. Stop typing and you stall out.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. ~100 word pool (racing, tech, fun themes) with difficulty scaling by distance — longer words appear as you drive further. Speed system: each completed word boosts target speed (word length * 3 + combo * 2), speed decays over time, mistakes reduce speed. Combo system with multiplier display. Perspective road with scrolling lane lines, neon edge glow, scenery (buildings, lights, signs, trees, barriers). Neon car with gradient body, underglow, headlights, taillights, speed lines at high speed. Exhaust and spark particles. WPM tracker, distance counter, score. Game over when speed hits 0. Word timeout system. Orbitron + Fira Code typography, dark cyberpunk road aesthetic.

## features
- ~100 word pool (racing, tech, typing, fun themes)
- Difficulty scales with distance (longer words)
- Speed boost per word (length + combo bonus)
- Speed decays over time — must keep typing
- Mistakes reduce speed and break combo
- Combo multiplier system
- Perspective 3D road with scrolling lanes
- Neon car with underglow, headlights, speed lines
- Roadside scenery (buildings, lights, signs, trees)
- Exhaust and spark particle effects
- WPM, distance, speed, score HUD
- Game over screen with full stats
- Escape to clear current input
