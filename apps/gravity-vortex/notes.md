# Gravity Vortex

## log
- 2026-01-05: Initial creation - neon physics game with black holes

## features
- Click and drag to launch glowing orbs
- Black holes with realistic gravity physics
- Trajectory prediction while aiming
- Combo system for consecutive captures
- Level progression with more black holes
- Neon visual effects with glow and trails
- Particle explosions on capture
- Mobile touch support
- Responsive canvas

## gameplay
- Start with 10 orbs
- Launch orbs into black holes to score
- Different black holes have different point values
- Combo multiplier for quick successive captures
- Level up every 1000 points, gain 5 more orbs
- More black holes appear at higher levels
- Game over when all orbs are used

## physics
- Gravitational constant G = 800
- Each black hole has mass affecting pull strength
- Orbs follow Newtonian gravity equations
- Trajectory preview shows predicted path

## design
- Orbitron font for sci-fi aesthetic
- Dark space background with grid
- Purple/magenta black holes with accretion disks
- Multi-colored glowing orbs with trails
- Particle explosions on capture

## technical
- Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- Canvas-based rendering
- 60fps game loop with delta time
- Touch events for mobile
