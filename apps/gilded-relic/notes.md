# Gilded Relic

Pixel-art dungeon crawler with procedural rooms, monsters, traps, and retro loot. Descend deeper for better gear.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. Procedural dungeon generation (5-9 rooms per floor, corridor connections, doors at entrances). 6 tile types (wall with brick pattern, floor, door, stairs, chest, trap). 9 monster types (Rat→Dragon) scaled by floor. 10 weapons, 5 armor, 3 potions, 3 scrolls, gold drops. 5 loot tiers (Common→Legendary) with color coding and stat scaling. Turn-based combat with ATK/DEF/damage rolls. Monster AI (chase when visible, attack when adjacent). Fog of war (radius 4 reveal). Traps visible only when adjacent. Auto-equip better gear. Inventory bar with click to use/equip. Floor progression (+2 maxHP, +1 ATK, heal 5). 8-directional movement (WASD/arrows/QEZC + d-pad buttons). Click-to-move on canvas. Game over screen with stats. Silkscreen + Press Start 2P typography, dark dungeon aesthetic.

## features
- Procedural dungeon (5-9 rooms, corridors, doors)
- 9 monster types scaling with floor depth
- 10 weapons, 5 armor, 3 potions, 3 scrolls
- 5 loot tiers (Common → Legendary)
- Turn-based combat with ATK/DEF system
- Monster AI (chase + attack)
- Fog of war
- Hidden traps
- Chests with loot + gold
- Auto-equip better gear
- 12-slot inventory with click to use
- Floor progression with stat boosts
- 8-directional movement
- Touch controls (d-pad buttons)
- Click-to-move on canvas
- Damage flash effect
- Item sparkle animation
