# Grape Duel

Purple neon card battle game with funny fruit stats.

## Log
- 2026-01-28: Initial creation with 12 fruit cards

## Features
- Top Trumps style card battle gameplay
- 12 unique fruit cards with ridiculous stats
- Purple/magenta neon aesthetic
- Click stat to battle, winner takes both cards
- Win condition: collect all 12 cards
- Animated card reveals and victory effects

## Stats
- **Squishiness** (20-95): How mushy when squeezed
- **Vitamin Power** (45-99): Nutritional dominance
- **Sass Level** (30-100): Attitude rating
- **Throwability** (20-95): Aerodynamic potential

## Technical
- Pure HTML/CSS/JS, no dependencies
- Bangers + Nunito fonts
- Deck shuffling with Fisher-Yates
- Card state management
