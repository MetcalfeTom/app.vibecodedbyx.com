# EMO SLASH

## log
- 2025-12-29: Initial creation
  - Rhythm game with scrolling lyrics
  - Click/tap lyrics as they cross the hit line
  - Perfect and Good timing zones
  - Combo system with multiplier (up to x4)
  - 60-second rounds
  - Original parody emo lyrics (35+ phrases)
  - Score based on timing accuracy
  - Visual slash effects on hit
  - Sound effects for hits and misses
  - Emo aesthetic: black, pink, cyan
  - Vignette and scanline overlays
  - Ratings: True Emo, Scene Kid, Poser, Normie

## notes
### Scoring:
- Perfect hit (within 30px of line): 100 * multiplier
- Good hit (within 80px): 50 * multiplier
- Miss: 0 points, combo reset

### Multiplier:
- x1 at start
- +1 for every 10 combo
- Max x4

### Ratings:
- 95%+ accuracy with more perfects = TRUE EMO
- 85%+ = SCENE KID
- 70%+ = POSER
- Below = NORMIE

### Aesthetics:
- Permanent Marker + Rock Salt fonts
- Hot pink (#ff1493) primary
