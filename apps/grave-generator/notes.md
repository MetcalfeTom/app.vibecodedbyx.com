# Grave Generator

Create your own weird digital tombstone with a custom epitaph.

## log
- 2026-01-22: Initial creation with 6 tombstone styles and 45+ random epitaphs

## features
- Custom name, birth/death years, and epitaph text
- 6 tombstone styles: Classic, Gothic, Cross, Heart, Angel, Spooky
- 45+ random weird (but PG) epitaphs
- Real-time preview
- Download as PNG (via html2canvas)
- Copy text to clipboard
- Share functionality
- Dark spooky theme

## epitaph examples (all PG)
- "Died doing what they loved: ignoring low battery warnings"
- "Finally got out of replying to emails"
- "404: Life not found"
- "Ctrl+Alt+Deleted"
- "Gone to touch grass permanently"
- "Achievement unlocked: Game Over"
- "Here lies someone who read the terms and conditions"

## tombstone styles
- Classic: Rounded gray stone
- Gothic: Pointed top dark stone
- Cross: With cross symbol
- Heart: Purple tones with heart
- Angel: Cream colored with angel emoji
- Spooky: Dark with skull and purple glow

## content policy
- All content kept PG/family-friendly
- No profanity in preset epitaphs
- Max character limits to prevent abuse
- Lighthearted/humorous tone
