## issues
- **Glass-lid RNG (45% shatter) is intentional** — fire safety is gambling sometimes. Could expose seed for replays.
- **Flour explosion is delayed 240ms** so the powder visually disperses before igniting. Some users may pick "flour" expecting instant fail-feedback; the 240ms feels right cinematically.
- **Class K extinguisher always succeeds** — historically accurate for cooking-oil fires; only correct option in some rounds when no metal lid is offered.
- **Auto-fail at intensity 1.05** is harsh but enforces urgency. If users complain, raise growth rate floor or extend cap to 1.15.
- **Skillet on small saucepan rounds** uses `panSize:'small'` flag — only `smallMetal` (TINY POT LID) seals it. Both metal lids visible simultaneously is rare (round 3, 7) to teach the size lesson.
