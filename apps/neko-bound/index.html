NEKO·BOUND

six rivals. one alley. all paws on deck.

  1. You're TABBY, the protagonist cat. Pick a move each turn — your rival picks one too
  2. Moves split into attack, heal, buff, and debuff types
  3. Some moves cost no PP (basic claw); strong moves like HAIRBALL HURL have limited uses
  4. NAP TIME heals 30 HP but you skip a turn — only safe when the rival is bopping not biting
  5. CATNIP FRENZY doubles your attack but costs 8 HP — high-risk, high-reward
  6. Defeat all six to claim the alley. Each rival heals you partway up
