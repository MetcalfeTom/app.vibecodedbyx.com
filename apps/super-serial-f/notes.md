# Super Serial F

Control a giant letter F jumping over evil pickles. It's exactly as weird as it sounds.

## log
- 2026-04-13: Initial build. Endless runner platformer starring the letter F. 5 pickle types (normal, gherkin, big dill, flying, roller) with angry faces. Double jump, stomp mechanic (bounce off pickles for combos), floating platforms. Collectible SERIF letters for bonus points. Parallax city background with stars and scrolling ground. Squash/stretch animations, particle bursts, screen flash on hits. Speed increases over time. Local storage high score. Bungee Shade + Silkscreen typography, dark purple/neon yellow aesthetic.

## features
- Play as a letter F with face, legs, and expressions
- 5 pickle enemy types (normal, small, big, flying with wings, rolling)
- Angry pickle faces with eyebrows and frowns
- Double jump
- Stomp mechanic — bounce off pickles from above
- Stomp combo counter
- Floating platforms
- SERIF letter collectibles (200 points each)
- Squash/stretch on jump/land
- Particle burst effects
- Trail effect behind F
- Parallax city skyline background
- Progressive difficulty (speed, spawn rate, enemy variety)
- Local storage high score
- Death screen with stats
- Touch/click/space to jump
- 16:9 responsive scaling
