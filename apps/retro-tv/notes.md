# Retro TV

80s TV simulator with wood-grain cabinet, fuzzy static, and fluffy hair movie channels.

## log
- 2026-04-17: Initial build. Wood-grain CRT TV cabinet with screen curvature, scanlines, glare reflection, and speaker grille. 12 channels: 2 static/off-air, color bars, and 9 content channels. Show channels feature silhouette characters with animated fluffy 80s hair (ellipse-based poofy hair with side poofs). Channel types: action show (Hair Force One), soap opera (Dynasty of Curls), cop show (Miami Vice Grip), sci-fi (The Perm Dimension), karate show, aerobics, MTV music visualizer, news with scrolling ticker, and infomercial with flashing prices. Static noise buffer, VHS tracking bars, horizontal glitch lines. Channel knobs, power button with LED, keyboard (arrows) and swipe controls. VT323 + Bungee Shade typography, warm wood-grain aesthetic.

## features
- Wood-grain CRT TV cabinet with 3D styling
- Screen curvature vignette, scanline overlay, glare reflection
- 12 channels including static, color bars, and 9 content channels
- Content types: shows, news, music, infomercial
- Animated fluffy 80s hair on all character silhouettes
- Static noise buffer (160x120 upscaled for retro feel)
- VHS tracking bar effect (random + on channel switch)
- Horizontal glitch lines (random pixel shifting)
- Channel switch static burst
- Power on/off with LED indicator
- Channel knob controls
- Keyboard: arrows to change channel, P to power
- Swipe up/down on mobile to change channel
- Channel number display (green phosphor style)
- News ticker scrolling, infomercial price flashing
- MTV-style music visualizer bars
- Speaker grille with holes
