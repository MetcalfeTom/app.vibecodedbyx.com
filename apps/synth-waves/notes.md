# synth-waves

## log
- 2026-05-26: Initial build. Real generative lo-fi engine + neon FFT visualizer (not a pre-recorded loop — every note is synthesized live via Web Audio). **Scheduler**: lookahead scheduler at 25ms intervals with 120ms scheduleAheadTime. Each bar = 16 sixteenth-note steps. `scheduleStep(step, time)` decides per-step what voices fire: kick on steps 0,8 + occasional ghost kick on step 6 (60% chance); snare on steps 4,12 + occasional flam on step 14 (25%); closed hat every 8th step with louder beat-1/beat-3 emphasis; open hat fill on step 14 (40%); bass on every beat (steps 0,4,8,12) at the current chord root in octave 2; pad once per bar held 95% of bar length; lead pentatonic blip with `params.leadDensity × 0.18` probability per even step. **Chord progression**: 4-bar ii7-V7-Imaj7-vi7 (in C: Dm7 - G7 - Cmaj7 - Am7) expressed as semitone offsets above key root. Key selectable C/D/E/F/G/A/B via 7-button row (semitone offsets 0/2/4/5/7/9/11). Lead notes drawn from major pentatonic [0, 2, 4, 7, 9] in 5th octave with 35% chance of octave shift. **Swing**: 3 chips — straight (0), soft (0.18), jazz (0.32). Even sixteenth steps get `secondsPerStep × (1 + swing)`, odd steps get `× (1 - swing)`, producing real shuffle feel without changing the audible step count. **Synthesis**: KICK = 125→40 Hz sine sweep (140ms) + 1800Hz bandpass noise click (12ms) + transient amplitude shape. SNARE = bandpass-noise burst at 1700Hz Q=1.2 (200ms) + 220→140Hz triangle body sweep (100ms) for thump. HAT = highpass-noise at 6500Hz, closed = 50ms with steeper decay envelope, open = 180ms gentler. BASS = sine fundamental + triangle 2× octave (-8¢ detuned) blended at 0.08 ratio, exponential AR envelope. PAD = per-chord-note 3-voice cluster (saw at -detune¢, saw at +detune¢, triangle 2× octave), all routed through a shared lowpass biquad (Q=1.6, cutoff = `params.padCutoff` modulated by per-voice sine LFO with `params.lfoDepth × padCutoff` swing); the pad bus also sends 55% wet to a convolution reverb. LEAD = triangle through lowpass at 2800Hz, exponential AR, also reverb-sent. **Reverb**: procedural 2.0s noise impulse response (cubic decay `(1 - i/len)^2.2`), wet send gain = `params.reverb`. **Vinyl bus**: looped 2s noise buffer through lowpass 2400Hz + highpass 70Hz, gain = `vinyl × 0.10`, with sparse 0.08% probability per sample pops adding click character. **Customizable oscillating frequencies** (the brief): 8 knobs — Pad Cutoff (220-3200 Hz, controls the LFO target + base of the warm filter sweep), LFO Rate (0.05-2.5 Hz, how slowly the cutoff wobbles), LFO Depth (0-100%, fraction of cutoff that LFO modulates), Detune (0-40¢, pad voice spread for warmth), Lead Density (0-100%), Vinyl (0-100%), Reverb (0-100%), BPM (60-110, transport bar). All knob changes propagate live via `setTargetAtTime` so adjustments are smooth without zipper noise. **Visualizer**: full-canvas with three layers — (1) synthwave background: 5-stop sky gradient, sun disc with horizontal scan-slit Mass-Effect-style cut (slit gap 0.10·sunR with progressive width growth), sun pulses with kick energy detection (sub-bass FFT bins 0-3 averaged, threshold 180 with 220ms refractory); (2) oscilloscope at top 18% of canvas with 3-pass chromatic-aberration draw (-1/+0/+1 px offsets in pink/cyan/white); (3) 64-bar FFT spectrum at bottom 20% with hue ramp 200-360°, per-bar vertical 3-stop HSL gradient + shadow blur glow. Perspective grid floor recedes toward horizon with kick-pulse-modulated cyan glow + scrolling horizontal lines (bgPhase × 80 mod gap). 16-step current-position indicator strip across the bottom edge — bright cyan square on the current step with shadow blur, dim 10% white on the others. **Kick detection**: subEnergy = avg(fftData[0..3]); when > 180 and 220ms since last pulse, set kickPulse=1.0 and let it decay × 0.92/frame to drive the visual pulse on sun + grid glow. **Defensive init**: matchMedia + canvas + every handler in try/catch, magenta error toast on window.error, audio ensure-on-demand pattern (AudioContext only created on first user interaction to comply with autoplay policy). **WCAG basics**: rem units throughout, semantic main/h1/aside, role=img + descriptive aria-label on canvas, aria-pressed on key + swing + play + mute toggles, aria-label on every input, focus-visible 3px outlines, ≥2.4rem touch targets, prefers-reduced-motion no-ops the canvas filter. **Aesthetic**: Audiowide cyan→magenta→gold gradient title, Major Mono Display tagline, Share Tech Mono panel + control labels, Rubik Mono One for HUD numerals. Color coding: cyan=oscillator/transport, lime=panel section headers, magenta=tempo/master, violet=key, gold=swing. **JSDOM smoke test**: zero errors, all knob values format correctly (78 BPM, 1200 Hz, 0.40 Hz → 1.50 Hz after tweak), 7 key buttons render with default C pressed, play button toggles to ◼ pause with aria-pressed=true, key swap (C→G) flips both aria-pressed states correctly, swing+mute chips toggle.

## issues
- Lookahead scheduler runs at 25ms intervals — perfectly fine on desktop; mobile background tab throttling could cause tempo hiccups. Acceptable; this is a music app, intended to be foreground.
- Kick detection threshold (180/255 sub-bass) is tuned for the kick synth's spectrum; if pad cutoff is very low and pad notes hit sub-bass region, may get false pulses. Acceptable — the visual just pulses slightly during big pad chords too.
- 4 hard-coded chords (ii-V-I-vi). Could expand to more progressions or random chord generation; kept simple to make it instantly recognizable as lo-fi.
