# Analog Computer Dashboard

## log
- 2026-01-18: Initial creation
  - 4 vibrating voltage meters
  - Patch bay with 12 signal nodes
  - Draggable patch cables
  - Oscilloscope with live waveforms

## features
- Voltage meters with vibrating needles
- Patch cable system:
  - Click two nodes to connect
  - Click cable to remove
  - Colored cables (orange, blue, green, etc.)
  - Droopy cable physics
- Signal nodes:
  - Inputs: SIN, SQR, TRI, NOISE
  - Process: AMP, MULT, INTEG, DIFF
  - Outputs: CH A, CH B, SCOPE, METER
- Oscilloscope:
  - Live waveform display
  - Waveform changes based on connections
  - Rotatable control knobs
- Indicator lights (Power, Compute, Overload)

## design
- Courier Prime + Orbitron fonts
- Vintage brown/beige/brass palette
- Inset panel shadows
- Brass bezels and copper accents
- Green phosphor oscilloscope

## controls
- Click nodes to connect with cables
- Click cables to remove
- Drag knobs to rotate
