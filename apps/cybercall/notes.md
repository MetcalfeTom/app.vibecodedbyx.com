## features
- Animated neural waveform with dual sine waves
- 10 AI caller personas (NEXUS-7, ARIA-X, etc.)
- Live call simulation with duration tracking
- Call types: inbound, outbound, transfer
- Call tags: resolved, escalated, pending
- Performance metrics:
  - Average duration
  - Resolution rate
  - Satisfaction score
  - AI efficiency
- Scanline overlay effect
- Real-time clock display
- Queue counter
