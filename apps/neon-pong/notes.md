# Neon Pong

## log
- 2026-01-19: Initial creation - neon pong with splitting balls and screen shake

## features
- Classic pong gameplay with neon aesthetic
- Ball splits into two every paddle hit
- Max 32 balls on screen
- Screen shake on every point scored
- Score counter for both players
- Ball trails with glow effects
- Each ball gets random neon color
- Touch controls for mobile
- Responsive canvas sizing

## design
- Dark background with neon glow effects
- Magenta left paddle, cyan right paddle
- Rainbow gradient title
- Orbitron font for retro-futuristic feel
- Dashed center line
- Ball trails for motion blur effect

## controls
- W/S: Left paddle up/down
- Arrow Up/Down: Right paddle up/down
- Touch: Drag on left/right side of screen

## physics
- Ball speed slightly increases on split (capped at 15)
- Paddle movement adds spin to ball
- Balls bounce off top/bottom walls
- New ball spawns when all balls scored
