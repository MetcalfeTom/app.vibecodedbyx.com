# Sandwich Stack

## log
- 2026-01-19: Initial creation - sandwich stacking game with falling ingredients

## features
- Moving plate controlled by mouse, touch, or arrow keys
- Falling ingredients from the sky
- 10 ingredient types (bread, lettuce, tomato, cheese, bacon, egg, patty, pickle, onion, top bread)
- Score based on ingredient points
- Stack height tracking
- Particle effects on catch
- Floating score popups
- Progressive difficulty (faster spawns)
- Stack stability physics
- Animated clouds background
- Mobile touch support

## design
- Fredoka font (playful, rounded)
- Sky blue to green gradient background
- Cartoon-style ingredients with emojis
- White plate with shadow
- Colorful score panels

## controls
- Mouse: Move to position plate
- Touch: Drag to move plate
- Arrow keys / A,D: Move plate left/right
- Space: Start/restart game
