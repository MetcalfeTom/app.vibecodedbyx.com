# void-wrestling

## log
- 2026-05-01: Created. **High-stakes zero-G arena duel — gravity is a lie.** Single-file vanilla JS canvas. **Concept**: 1P vs AI inside a circular cosmic arena. No floor, no ceiling, no gravity — both fighters drift through frictionless vacuum, accelerating only when they thrust. Win by either reducing the foe's HP to 0 OR ejecting them past the arena's void edge. **Player input**: WASD thrust (any direction, no facing-locked controls), CLICK to BOOST (one-shot velocity bump toward the cursor, 600ms cooldown), SPACE to GRAPPLE (when within 70 world-units of foe — locks both fighters in orbit for 1.2s; if your YEET meter is full at grapple time, releases as a 1100-velocity throw of the foe in your facing direction for 14 dmg + stun), SHIFT to brake (multiplies velocity by 0.92/frame). Arrow keys mirror WASD; Space + arrow keys preventDefault to avoid page scroll. **Physics**: thrust = 280 u/s² accel, max speed cap = 480 u/s soft (with 1.5× hard cap), 1.5%/frame velocity damping (vacuum bleed prevents perpetual escalation). Fighter-fighter collision: elastic-ish bounce with momentum transfer (`j = -(1+e) × vRel / (1/m_a + 1/m_b)` at e=0.85), separates the two by half the overlap each. Damage on slam: when the relative-velocity-along-normal > 90 u/s, apply `4 + min(18, (vRel-90) × 0.10)` HP damage to the slower-along-normal fighter (the "absorber"). Slam dealer also charges yeet meter at 1.2× the dmg amount. Edge handling: at 92% of arena radius the void exerts soft pushback (`-(dx/d) × k × 18` where k scales with how far over); past `R + 30` units = instant KO via ejection. **AI**: 4 modes — APPROACH (close distance from far range, occasional 4%/frame boost), CIRCLE (perpendicular orbit at mid-range to set up slam, tangential 70%/inward 30%), SLAM (all-in chase when within 70 u), GRAPPLE (when in range AND yeet meter ≥ 70). Edge-recovery overrides ALL modes — if foe is past 85% radius, throttle inward and boost back at 95%. HP <30% biases toward CIRCLE (defensive). Per-frame ±5° heading wobble for character. **Yeet meter**: 0..100, charges from damage taken (heavy slams) AND from damage dealt (lighter charge); full meter = a halo ring around the fighter + ability to YEET on grapple release. Grapple without full yeet just gives a brief lock with no special damage. **Sprite**: chunky pixel-art wrestler — 24×22 colored body block + 16×12 head + 6×6 arm tabs that reach toward foe when grappling (else toward thrust direction) + 6×8 darker leg blocks + 3×3 ink eyes + ouch mouth (white pixel slash) for 0.4s after taking a slam. Outer radial-gradient glow in fighter's color, flashes white-yellow on hit. Trail: positions stamped at 40%/frame while thrusting, 24-pos buffer with size+alpha falloff. **Arena**: cyan ring (60% saturation pulsing 1.8/s), inner violet pad ring at 92% radius, edge-warning radial gradient (`rgba(255,60,80,0)` → `0.45` from 92%R → R+30u), distant pixel-art crowd (80 dots ringing the arena at R+22..38, color cycles cyan/magenta/grey at 5/7/n indexes, sin-bobbing). Center crosshair dot. **Particles**: impact bursts (24 white-cream dots at slam contact, scaled by damage), boost trail (14 fighter-color dots), yeet release (28 magenta dots). Screen shake on impact + scaled by `vRel × 0.05` and an additional 22-unit punch on yeet release. **Audio** (Web Audio synth): thrust = 180Hz triangle blip per frame while pressed, impact = 80Hz square thump + bandpass-noise 600Hz scaled by intensity, boost = 140→220Hz sawtooth two-tone, grapple = 540→720Hz square pair, yeet = 200Hz noise burst + 4-note ascending square arp, win = 5-note ascending major arpeggio (392/523/659/784/988), lose = 5-note descending sawtooth, edge-warning = random 480-540Hz square click 4%/frame when near edge. **HUD**: 2 fighter cards (cyan-bordered "YOU" left, magenta-bordered random foe-name right) showing HP bar (green→amber→red) + YEET bar (amber gradient). Cards are top-left/right-aligned with backdrop-blur. Big centered "FIGHT!" / "YEETED" / "GRIP" / "GRAB!" announce text in Bowlby One that pops in 0.7-1.4s. **Title screen**: gradient-fill BOWLBY-ONE "VOID WRESTLING" + Press Start 2P "gravity is a lie" subtitle + italic VT323 lore line + cyan ENTER button. Result overlay: golden VOIDLORD (win) or red EJECTED (lose) card with reason text + REMATCH button. **Foe names**: 10-pool random per match — Voidrek / Crushipod / Null-Fang / Hollow Jaw / Gravitron / Obsidian / Starslam / Lord Anti / Xenorage / Lacuna. Pollinations OG.

## issues
- Damage from slam scales with relative velocity, but the yeet-charge bonus also scales — so slam-spamming a slow opponent can fill the yeet meter without much HP gain. Acceptable for the comeback mechanic.
- Edge-pushback is constant-strength regardless of player vs foe, so a pinned fighter near the edge can repeatedly bounce back unless slammed hard. Could increase pushback strength as fighter sits on the edge longer.
- AI's grapple gate (`yeet ≥ 70` AND in range AND `Math.random() < 0.25` per frame) means the AI sometimes spam-attempts grapples. Fine since the grapple lock duration plus cooldown spacing keeps it from being abusive.
- No stamina/cooldown on player thrust — they can hold WASD forever. Velocity cap + damping prevents runaway speed but might feel "too easy" to control. Tuning question.
- Mobile: tap-to-boost works but there's no continuous WASD-equivalent. Could add a virtual joystick.
