# Chaos Magnets

Neon magnetic particle simulator — spawn positive, negative, and neutral particles that attract, repel, cluster, and collide with sparks and field lines.

## log
- 2026-04-11: Initial build. Canvas-based particle physics with 3 polarities: positive (red, +), negative (blue, −), neutral (yellow, ○). Magnetic force: opposites attract, same repel, neutrals weakly attract all. Soft collision with bounce and velocity exchange. Opposite-polarity collisions emit gold sparks and curved field lines, small chance to merge into neutral. Same-polarity collisions emit colored sparks. Fade trail rendering for motion blur. Connection lines between nearby opposite particles. Speed-based glow intensity. CHAOS button explodes everything outward and randomly flips polarities. Click/drag to spawn. Caps at 500 particles. Chakra Petch + Azeret Mono typography, deep void aesthetic.

## features
- 3 polarity types: positive (+), negative (−), neutral (○)
- Magnetic attraction (opposites) and repulsion (same)
- Neutral particles weakly attracted to everything
- Collision sparks with polarity-colored effects
- Curved field lines on opposite-polarity collisions
- Rare particle merging (annihilation → neutral)
- Fade trail motion blur rendering
- Connection lines between nearby opposite particles
- CHAOS button: explode + random polarity flip
- Click/drag to spawn particles
- Speed-based glow intensity
- Collision counter in HUD
- 500 particle cap for performance
- Soft wall bouncing
