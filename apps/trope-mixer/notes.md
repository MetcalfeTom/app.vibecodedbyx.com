# Trope Mixer

## log
- 2025-12-29: Initial creation
  - Movie genre mashup generator
  - 14 genres: Musical, Slasher, Rom-Com, Sci-Fi, Western, Horror, Film Noir, Sports, Fantasy, Mockumentary, Heist, Zombie, Courtroom Drama, Kaiju
  - Each genre has tropes, settings, and character archetypes
  - Generates movie title, tagline, and plot synopsis
  - Lock buttons to keep a genre while remixing the other
  - History of recent mashups
  - Film strip aesthetic with dark cinematic theme
  - Keyboard support (Space/Enter)

## notes
### Genres included:
- Musical, Slasher, Rom-Com, Sci-Fi
- Western, Horror, Film Noir, Sports
- Fantasy, Mockumentary, Heist, Zombie
- Courtroom Drama, Kaiju

### Generation system:
- Random title from templates + genre words
- Tagline from template pool
- Plot mixes tropes, settings, characters from both genres
- 4 random trope tags displayed

### UI Features:
- Lock genre buttons to keep one constant
- Film strip borders for cinema aesthetic
- Bebas Neue font for titles
- Gradient text effects
