- The Hollow Congregation cult theme
- Main eye sigil symbol (animated pulse)
- Nine Truths of the Congregation
- Scripture/manifesto text
- Hidden 9-page classified FEMA document easter egg

  - Click main eye symbol 9 times
  - Click smaller sigil icons

  - The Emergence event timeline
  - Faction profiles (Congregation, Unveiled, Forgotten)
  - Identification protocols
  - Containment procedures
  - Handwritten annotations
  - Redacted sections
  - Coffee stain effects
- Pagination with 9 pages
- Escape key to close document
