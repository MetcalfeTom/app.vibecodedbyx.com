# Neon Rush

Infinite neon racer. Dodge obstacles at light speed.

## log
- 2026-04-10: Initial build. Canvas infinite racer with 5-lane system, triangle player ship with engine glow and trail, neon-colored obstacles (6 colors) with rounded rects and glow, scrolling grid road with lane dividers and edge glow. Speed increases over time (0.0003/frame), obstacle spawn rate increases, double/triple spawns at high speed. Screen shake + particle explosion on death. Swipe + touch buttons + keyboard (A/D, arrows). Best score in localStorage. Vignette overlay, speed lines at high velocity. Orbitron + Azeret Mono typography, cyan/magenta neon aesthetic.

## features
- 5-lane road with scrolling grid
- Triangle ship with engine glow and trail
- Colorful neon obstacles with glow
- Infinite speed scaling
- Particle explosions on death
- Screen shake on impact
- Speed lines at high velocity
- Swipe controls + touch buttons + keyboard
- Best score persistence (localStorage)
- Vignette effect
- Mobile responsive
