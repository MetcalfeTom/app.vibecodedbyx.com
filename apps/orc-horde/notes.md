# Orc Horde

Spawn pixel orc warriors and watch them march toward the Dark Tower.

## log
- 2026-04-13: Initial build. 5 unit types (Grunt, Brute, Shaman, Warg Rider, Siege Tower) with unique pixel art, stats, and animations. Orcs march left-to-right toward a dark tower with evil eye window. Tower fires arrows at orcs, damaging/killing them. WAAAGH button spawns 30-unit mixed wave. Particles (blood, rock, dust, fire). Screen shake on tower hits. Depth sorting by Y. Blood moon, distant mountains, stone tower with battlements and fire. Click ground to spawn grunts. Banners on some brutes. MedievalSharp + Silkscreen typography.

## features
- 5 orc unit types with unique pixel art
  - Grunt: sword/shield, fast
  - Brute: axe, heavy armor, banner carrier
  - Shaman: staff with magic glow, robed
  - Warg Rider: mounted on warg beast, spear
  - Siege Tower: rolling tower with fire and skull
- March animation with leg bob and body sway
- Dark tower with evil eye, battlements, fire torches
- Tower arrow defense (random targeting)
- HP bars on damaged units
- Blood/rock/dust/fire particles
- Screen shake on tower impacts
- Depth-sorted rendering
- WAAAGH mass spawn (30 units)
- Click ground to place grunts
- Blood moon night sky with mountains
- Army/fallen counter
- Touch support
