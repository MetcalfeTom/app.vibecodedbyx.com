# Mate Clicker

A yerba mate themed idle/clicker game.

## log
- 2026-01-22: Initial creation - full clicker game with upgrades and achievements

## features
- Click the mate gourd to earn sips
- 10 upgrades (7 auto-generators, 3 click power upgrades)
- 8 achievements to unlock
- Autosave every 30 seconds
- Responsive design for mobile
- Floating click effects
- Toast notifications for achievements

## upgrades
- Better Bombilla (0.1 sips/sec)
- Premium Yerba (0.5 sips/sec)
- Artisan Gourd (2 sips/sec)
- Hot Thermos (8 sips/sec)
- Mate Circle (30 sips/sec)
- Yerba Plantation (100 sips/sec)
- Mate Factory (400 sips/sec)
- Stronger Sip (+1 per click)
- Double Gulp (+5 per click)
- Mega Chug (+25 per click)
