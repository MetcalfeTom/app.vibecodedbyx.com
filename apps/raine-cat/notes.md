# Raine the Cat

Moonlit rooftop platformer — guide Raine across rooftops collecting fish, yarn, and toy mice while dodging crows.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Rainbow accents (ears, collar, paws, tail tip, bell, jump particles, platform edges all cycle HSL). Scary cucumbers hazard (dark green with glowing red eyes, evil grin, danger aura, wobble; Raine leaps back in terror on contact). Reduced platforms (wider gaps, smaller platforms, higher gap chance).
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. Slate-gray cat with blue eyes, bell collar, animated tail/legs/blink. 3 collectibles (fish 10pts, yarn 25pts, mouse 50pts). Crow hazards with flapping wings. Procedural rooftop generation with chimneys/antennas, crumbly platforms, parallax buildings with lit windows, star field, moon. Double-jump, 9 lives, difficulty ramp. Heart + sparkle particle FX. Mobile controls. Silkscreen + Lexend Deca typography, moonlit purple/slate aesthetic.

## features
- Canvas-drawn cat with expressive animations (tail wag, leg walk cycle, blinking, whiskers)
- 3 collectible types: fish, yarn balls, toy mice
- Crow hazards with red eyes and flapping animation
- Procedural infinite rooftop generation
- Crumbly platforms that collapse underfoot
- Parallax city buildings with lit/unlit windows
- Star field with twinkling, detailed moon with craters
- Double jump with landing particles
- 9 lives (cats!), invincibility frames after damage
- Difficulty ramp (more gaps, more crows over time)
- Distance-based scoring + collectible bonuses
- Mobile touch controls
- localStorage best score
