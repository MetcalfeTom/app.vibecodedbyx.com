# neon-jet-fighter

## log
- 2026-05-27: Initial build per tt2513073's chat brief (fast 2D side-scrolling jet shooter where player color shifts on each kill). Full-screen canvas, WASD/arrow movement, Space or click to fire, mobile joystick + FIRE button. **The headline mechanic**: every enemy kill bumps `player.hue += 18°` (cycling the full spectrum every 20 kills), so chain-killing rapidly visibly walks the jet through cyan → magenta → amber → lime → violet → and back. Bullets fire in the player's *current* hue too, so the trail behind you matches your jet color in real time. **4 enemy types** with distinct geometry + behavior: GRUNT (arrow, straight-left at 130-250 u/s, 1HP, 100pts), SINE (hex, vertical sine path amp 60-110 freq 1.4-2.2, 1HP, 200pts), DIVER (diamond with eye, homes on player with capped velocity 280+, 2HP, 350pts), BOMBER (large ellipse with wings + cannon glow, 4HP, 700pts, fires aimed shots every 1.2-1.8s). Spawn rate ramps from 1.4s → 0.25s as `state.difficulty` climbs (1 unit per 6 seconds capped at 20). **Player upgrades**: 10 kills unlocks a twin-link second bullet stream offset −9px, 25 kills adds a third at +9px (so a 25+ chain looks like a fan of color-cycling bullets). **Chain bonus**: kills within 1.2s of each other accumulate `state.chainCount`; every 5th chain kill awards +500 points + a "CHAIN ×N" indicator in the player's hue at the top of the screen. **Audio** (Web Audio synth, no samples): shoot = 720→1100Hz square 40ms (cheap pew); kill = triangle pitched by current hue (320 + hue·1.6 Hz, 180ms) + 1200Hz bandpass-noise crack; hue-shift accent = quick triangle bleep at 540 + hue·1.2 → 880Hz; hit = 200→60Hz saw + 280Hz bandpass noise; game-over = 4-note descending sawtooth dirge + noise wash. **Background**: 5-stop vertical sky gradient (deep void → magenta-pink at horizon) + 140 parallax stars with per-star depth (z 0.2-1.2) scrolling left at 60+difficulty·4 u/s scaled by z. Stars wrap when they pass x<-2. **Player jet** rendered procedurally: pentagon body with vertical gradient in current hue (75%→55%→40% lightness), darker wing strakes (hue+340°), white cockpit with a hue-shifted inner dot, sin-modulated flame thrust trailing left in player hue, and a damage-flash red overlay on iframes. Shadow blur 16 in current hue for the glow. **Particles**: 14-50 per explosion (radius-scaled), additive blend with shadowBlur, gravity 80, decay 0.95/frame. Floating "+points" text rises and fades. Camera shake on hit (instant 18, decay 0.86/frame). **HUD**: vital pips top-left, current hue swatch+angle, kills counter (lime), score (gold). All update on state change via refreshHUD(). **Game-over modal**: pop-in dialog with final score + kills + hue achieved, re-launch button. **Defensive init**: try/catch on canvas + audio + every input handler, magenta error toast on window.error. **WCAG basics**: rem units, semantic main + dialog, role=application + descriptive aria-label on canvas, aria-pressed on mute, role=dialog aria-modal on game-over, focus-visible 2-3px outlines, ≥2.5rem touch targets, prefers-reduced-motion damps dt ×0.85. **Aesthetic**: Bowlby One SC cyan→magenta→gold gradient title, Major Mono Display tagline, Audiowide HUD labels, Share Tech Mono body, Rubik Mono One numerals. Per-card hue accents (vitals neutral, hue card pulses with player swatch, kills card lime, score card gold).

## issues
- The 18° hue step per kill cycles every 20 kills exactly — at high chain counts the visible color matters less since it's spinning so fast. Could throttle the visual hue (vs the score hue) at very high speed if it becomes too strobe-y.
- Bomber bullets aim at the player's current position (not predicted), so they're easy to dodge by moving perpendicular. Intentional — keeps the "fast" feel without surprise hits.
- DIVER homing acceleration can stack if you stand still long enough; capped via max speed but very high difficulty could let multiple divers converge.
