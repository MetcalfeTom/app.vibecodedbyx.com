# silver-smoocher

## log
- 2026-04-27: Created. **Pixel-art tycoon clicker** where you smooch jet-pack-equipped silver foxes for coins. Cookie-Clicker-style economy: 10 generators with `cost * 1.15^owned` pricing, 15 one-time multiplicative upgrades, localStorage save (`silver-smoocher-save-v1`) with debounced 1500ms saves + 12s auto-save + beforeunload flush. **Sprite system**: parametric `drawOldMan(ctx, ox, oy, scale, opts)` draws a 24×30 pixel old man via `ctx.fillRect` ops — each variant configurable through `opts` (card color, prop {cane/bingo/paper/cup/mic/none}, dentures, glasses, hearing, wave, pucker 0..1, jet 0..1, flameTick). **Rocket backpack** drawn first (behind body): cone above head, pack on right side with porthole, fins below, animated 3-frame flame at bottom when jetting. **Featured click target** at scale 7 in canvas top-center; click anywhere on the stage = `+clickPower` coins, sets pucker=1.0 (eyes squint, lips extend, jet flames flare), spawns 4–12 heart/coin particles + DOM floating "+N" text. **Buddy grid** below: each owned generator instance gets a small (scale 2) buddy in a 9-col grid (cap 36 visible, max 12 per gen), each pulses on its own 1.6–4.1s cadence emitting a `+coin` float. **Generators** (10): Walking Stick (15¢, 0.1cps) → Bingo Card → Denture Polish → Recliner → Bird Feeder → Rocking Chair → Cruise Cabin → Bingo Hall → Senior Lounge → Eternity Garden (75B¢, 1.6M cps).
- 2026-04-27: Added **Prostate Power** — legendary retired rapper as the 11th, ultra-premium unlockable. Cost `1e15` ¢ (1 quadrillion), `25,000,000` cps. **Endgame gate**: requires owning ≥1 Eternity Garden first (locked card reads "unlock the eternity garden first"). When owned, the FEATURED click sprite swaps to the rapper variant + gold spotlight halo orbits around him. **Sprite extension**: added 5 new opts to `drawOldMan` — `cap` (backwards baseball cap covering the bald top + side wisps, dark with gold accent stripe + tiny snapback adjuster + brim sticking out the back-left since cap is backwards), `shades` (big black wraparound rectangular sunglasses with shadesH bridge highlight + tiny gold logo), `chain` (gold chain across collar + dollar-bling pendant medallion on chest with goldHi shine pixel), `grill` (one front tooth swapped to gold when smiling), and the new `prop:'mic'` (microphone with grille/ball head + gold band + dark stem + skin-color hand wrapping the handle, held up to mouth). New PAL entries: `gold/goldDp/goldHi/shades/shadesH/cap/capLt/capRim/mic/micGrill/micBall/jersey/jerseyHi/jerseyN`. **Shop styling**: `.item.legendary` border = solid gold, gradient bg, animated `legendaryPulse` glow keyframe (12px→24px gold shadow), name in gold w/ goldDp emboss-shadow, `<span class="item-tag">★ LEGENDARY</span>` chip above name. Legendary lock state disables the pulse animation. Buddy + shop-icon canvases now pass cap/shades/chain/grill through to the sprite renderer so the rapper looks consistent everywhere.

## issues
- **PAL.gold was used but not defined** before this update — line 657-659 had `PAL.gold||'#f5c849'` fallback (the fallback was always firing). Now properly added to PAL so the cardigan buttons + chain + cap accents share one source of truth.
- **Rapper overlay layering**: cap/shades/chain are drawn AFTER the standard head/face/body, intentionally covering the bald top, regular eyes/glasses, and cardigan collar. If a future variant needed the rapper to ALSO have hearing aid visible, it would currently be hidden behind the cap — fix would be to draw the cap brim first, hearing aid, then cap crown.
- **Featured sprite swap is binary** — owning even 1 Prostate Power flips the headline sprite permanently to the rapper. This is intentional (he's the star of the show once he shows up). If users complain, could rotate every N seconds between regular old man and rapper.
