## issues
- **`quantizeFreq` ranges octaves 1-7** which spans ~30Hz to ~3.7kHz. Below 30Hz target, quantization can pull notes too far — clamped to nearest in-range semi.
- **Sputter LFO at 0Hz** (slider min) effectively turns off the gas-chamber fricative texture — pads become straight tones. Acceptable (some users want clean auto-tuned subs).
- **AutoStrength=0** still passes through `pitchShift` slider — auto-tune strength only governs scale-snap, not the manual transposition.
- **Recording playback uses setTimeout** which is gc-jitter-prone; for tight-pocket beats this is sloppy by ~10-30ms. Acceptable for fart phrases. Could swap to `audioCtx.currentTime` scheduling if precision becomes a complaint.
- **Formant tail** only runs on tpain/alien/horn/sus flavored pads — turning every pad into a vowel-pad makes it sound less like farts. Selective by design.
- **REC dot + reels animate** even on pads that produce no audible sound (if mute or AudioContext suspended); keeps UX feedback responsive.
