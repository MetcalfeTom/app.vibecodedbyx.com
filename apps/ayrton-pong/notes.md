# ayrton-pong · notes

## log
- 2026-05-15: v1 — a quick, simple neon pong made for chatter **@ayrtonalexis** by request. Chat ask: "create a simple ping pong game with neon colors and smooth controls for @ayrtonalexis." Stripped-down companion to `smooth-pong` (which is the same author's earlier, more elaborate pong with paddle rotation + mouse-velocity power) — this one keeps it classic: pink paddle (you) vs cyan paddle (bot), first-to-7, no swing mechanics, no buckets, just clean reflexes. **Court**: 16:9 logical 800×450 canvas with DPR-aware scaling, dark `#04020f` background inside a violet 2-px border with violet glow. Faint full-page scanline overlay + page-level pink/cyan/violet radial glows behind the stage. **Paddles**: 12 × 84 px. Player smoothly follows the mouse (critically-damped: desired velocity = `sign(diff) × min(|diff|·14, MAX)`, paddle velocity eases toward it at 12/s); pressing W/S or ↑/↓ switches to keyboard mode (accel 3800 px/s², drag 7/s when no key held). Switching back to mouse requires >16 px of movement so a still cursor never hijacks keyboard play. Touch drag also drives the paddle. AI tracks the ball with a 0.085 smoothing factor + 24 px `sin` jitter when ball is incoming; idle-drifts near center via slow sine when the ball moves away; max speed capped at 78% of human max so it's beatable. **Ball**: 8 px radius, base speed 360 px/s, +28 per paddle hit capped at 800; reflection angle from where it hit the paddle (`hit ∈ [-1, 1]` → `±0.85 rad`), plus 18% of the paddle's vertical velocity for English. Yellow trail (last 14 positions, growing alpha). **Audio (Web Audio synth)**: square-wave blip at 290 Hz on wall, 520/440 Hz on left/right paddle hit, sawtooth descending sweep on bot-score, triangle ascending arpeggio on player-score, C5-E5-G5 chord on win. Default ON, toggle in the HUD. **HUD**: shimmering rainbow `Audiowide` title "Ayrton Pong" (animated background-clip 7 s gradient), dedication line `a quick neon pong · made for @ayrtonalexis` with ayrtonalexis in gold, big Major Mono Display scores side-by-side (pink left, cyan right, gold dot separator), Space Mono controls pill row with kbd-styled key hints + a mute toggle. **Overlay states**: start ("Move with mouse · W/S · ↑/↓ &nbsp; first to 7"), pause, win ("You win · gg, ayrton · Play again"), lose ("Bot wins · rematch?"). **WCAG basics**: canvas `role="application"` + summary aria-label, scores `aria-live="polite"`, focus-visible 2 px pink outline, 44 px button targets, `prefers-reduced-motion` disables the title shimmer. Pollinations OG flux seed 20140.

## issues
- Deliberately minimal — no power-ups, no game modes, no leaderboard. The user asked for "simple," so I kept it simple.
- Touch input drives the paddle directly to the touch Y, which can feel "snappy" on mobile compared to mouse's eased follow. Could critically-damp the touch target like the mouse for parity.
