# catweazle-3d · notes

## log
- 2026-05-16: v1 — **third-person 3D stealth/escort adventure** built fresh in response to chat ask: "rebuild catweazle from scratch as a 3D third-person adventure with stealth and puzzles, where Carrot protects the chaotic wizard." Shipped as a NEW app (`catweazle-3d`) alongside the original parchment text-adventure `catweazle` — refused to delete the existing app since v1.1/v1.2/v1.3 had chat contributions on top of it.
  - **Engine**: Three.js r128 (CDN), PCFSoftShadow, HemisphereLight + DirectionalLight + AmbientLight, sRGB output, fog 35→95 in muted sky-grey.
  - **World** (200×200 ground at y=0, world bounds clamped ±48): low-poly English parish — 5 thatched cottages south of a circular cobble village green, a stone church at z=10 with a wooden spire and cross, a red phone box on the green, the **MoD water tower (Castle Saburac)** at (-22,6) carried over from v1.3 of the parchment app (fence, antenna, red warning lamp), a yellow hay field with 4 hay-bale cylinders at z=8-11, scattered trees, a misty pond at (-8,18) with hovering puff spheres, paths drawn as ochre PlaneGeometry strips between zones, and the **standing stones (goal)** at (0,0,35) — 5 stones in a partial circle + 2 pillars + capstone arch + moss circle.
  - **Player (Carrot)**: hand-assembled inline-mesh — cylinder trousers (blue) + cylinder shirt (cream) + brown tie box + sphere head + hemispherical orange hair sphere on top + 3 freckle dots + 2 eye dots + 2 slim arm cylinders that swing while walking. Walk bob via `Math.abs(Math.sin(playerWalkPhase)) * 0.06`. Camera-relative WASD movement. Pointer-lock for mouse yaw. AABB collision (axis-separated) against obstacles list populated as buildings are created.
  - **Catweazle NPC**: cylinder robe (brown, wider bottom) + torus rope belt + sphere head + long stretched white cone beard + tall conical purple hat with 4 little gold octahedron stars orbiting + flat brim cylinder + wild eyebrow boxes + sclera+pupil eyes for the slightly-mad look. **Touchwood the toad** rides on his left shoulder (stretched green sphere + 2 yellow eye dots). Walk bob like Carrot.
  - **AI state machine**: `follow → panic → shouting → calm → follow`. Panic timer counts down 18-32s. When it fires, picks nearest of 6 hard-coded appliances (toaster, lamp post, phone box, light switch on cottage, church tannoy, water-tower vending machine), each with a hand-written shout line in his voice (`"AAAARGH! THE METAL MOUTH HATH BREATHED CHARRED BREAD UPON ME!"`). He sprints (5.2 u/s) to the appliance, then **shouts for 7 seconds** — raising the global watch alert by `dt * 0.07` and self-bewildering by `dt * 2.4`. Player must reach him (`< 3.0` m) and press **Space** to shush. Shushing returns him to `calm` state, halves global alert, resets panic timer to 18-32s. Pressing **E** calls him to come back. Idle quips every ~9-19s via `TW_IDLE` pool (8 lines).
  - **Guards** (3 villagers with hand-built bodies — trousers + jacket cylinder in different colours + flat cap + peak): **Constable Tubb** (blue, patrols the cottage cluster), **PC Ramsbottom** (rust, patrols the north path to stones), **Vicar Cudgel** (plum, wanders the green). Each has a waypoint patrol with 1.5s pauses, vision cone visualized in 3D as a translucent BufferGeometry wedge (radius 9, angle ~75°, color shifts yellow→orange→red by state). Detection: dot-product cone check + 8-step LOS raycast against obstacles. Sneaking (Shift) reduces effective detect range to 55% AND detection accumulation rate. Detection time > 0.7s → state becomes `spotted` ("OI! YOU THERE!"), guard chases at 3.8 u/s; touching player (`< 1.4` m) = caught = lose a life + reset positions. After 14m chase distance the guard gives up and becomes `hunting` for 5s, then back to patrol. Catweazle shouting within 14m of a guard nudges them into `hunting` state.
  - **Win condition**: collect all 3 floating runes (octahedron + ring halo + soft light pillar — Iron at the water tower base, Glass in the hay field, Voice on the water tower platform), then walk into the stone circle with Catweazle within 6m. Triggers `winGame()` — `sndWin()` arpeggio + endcard with prose: "Adamcos! Salamandastron! ... the door opens, just for a heartbeat."
  - **Lose condition**: 3 lives lost OR Catweazle's bewilderment hits 100 (sustained shouting + unshushed panics drive it up). Endcard varies by reason — "Wellness Retreat near Reigate" for bewilderment, "village hall paperwork" for catches.
  - **HUD**: 3 cards across the top — runes (3 illuminated discs), wizard wits + watch alert bars (parchment palette, gold→rust gradient), lives (3 rust hearts that grey out). Floating speech bubble (parchment with ink border + black tail) projects from a 3D anchor (`mesh.position + (0, 2.6, 0)` through `project(camera)`) to screen space. Bottom-left key hint pill. Bottom-right circular minimap canvas (140px) showing player as orange dot + facing line, Catweazle as plum dot, guards as state-coloured dots + vision wedges, runes as cyan dots, stones as gold dot. Flash messages center-screen for catches/rune pickups/objective hints.
  - **Audio (Web Audio synth)**: shush (noise burst), shout (noise + sawtooth + square sub), rune pickup (4-note arp), caught (descending sawtooth pair), win (6-note triangle arp), call-wizard (2-note triangle).
  - **Controls**: WASD walk (camera-relative), Mouse orbit camera (click canvas to lock pointer, Esc to release), Shift sneak (0.45× speed + much harder to detect), Space shush (must be within 3m of Catweazle), E call him to your side. Touch fallback: single-finger drag rotates camera (no movement yet — mobile is best for spectating).
  - **Aesthetic**: muted parchment + ink palette via CSS vars (`--ink:#1a1208`, `--paper:#f3e6c5`, `--moss:#4a6a32`, `--rust:#c34a32`, `--gold:#d9a43a`, `--plum:#6a2b6b`). Fonts: UnifrakturCook for the masthead, IM Fell English for body prose, IM Fell English SC for labels, IBM Plex Mono for HUD numerics. Intro card explains controls + premise; restart button on the endcard resets all state cleanly.

## issues
- Three.js r128 ~600KB first load — fine on desktop, ~2s on mobile.
- No proper LOS raycast — using an 8-step segment check against obstacle AABBs. Good enough for cottage corners but misses thin walls. A real `THREE.Raycaster` against scene meshes would be heavier; current approach feels right.
- Mobile touch movement isn't wired — only camera drag. Would need a virtual joystick overlay. Desktop-first for v1.
- Guard "hunting" state is a simple timer — they don't actually walk toward the last-seen position. Adding a `lastSeen` Vector3 + go-to-investigate would feel smarter.
- The wizard's pathfinding is "straight line + AABB collision" — he can get stuck on corners. Could use simple repulsion from obstacles when blocked > 1s, or add a node-based grid. Currently he gets unstuck once the player moves enough that the line-of-approach clears.
- No save/load. Each session is a fresh run.

## relationship to /catweazle (the parchment app)
- The parchment text adventure at `/catweazle` is **not deleted**. v1.3 still serves the misty pond + MoD water tower + clickable appliances + Carrot + Amulet of Adamcos + 1970s grain treatment.
- `/catweazle-3d` is a **second take** — a third-person stealth/escort game that takes the same characters and setting and turns them into real-time gameplay.
- The two share lore: water tower as Castle Saburac, electrickery as the central comic conceit, Touchwood, Carrot as Catweazle's translator/guardian, the three runes (iron/glass/voice), the standing stones as the goal.
- Chat can compare the two and tell us which direction to build out next.
