Catweazle & the Boy

Thou art Carrot, the farm boy. A wizard from the year ten-and-sixty-six hath fallen into thy hayloft — wild of beard, soft of head, terrified of the toaster.

Touchwood the toad watcheth from his shoulder.

Three runes lie scattered about the parish — iron, glass, and voice. Gather them and lead him to the standing stones ere the village watch findeth either of you.
