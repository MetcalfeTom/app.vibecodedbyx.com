## issues
- Cobra venom triggers on SNAKE too (regex matches both); I left SNAKE out of the active catalog but the check stays for future-proof.
- Speech voices vary wildly by OS — iOS Safari has good native voices; some Android browsers have only one robotic voice. Pitch 1.15 and rate 0.78 are calibrated for clearer kid-friendly pronunciation.
- Bonus round spawns 8+7+5=20 balloons total per family. On a small phone screen that's a lot at once. Kid can tap-pop quickly though; uncaught balloons just float off the top.
- 9 families × avg 3.7 words = ~33 words per full loop. Loops back to family 0 after the last.
- The intro overlay's `aria-modal="true"` blocks the underlying scratch canvas correctly but doesn't trap focus aggressively — fine for a play-and-tap game.
- Confetti and venom both use DOM elements; cleanup is on a per-particle timeout. At very high spam, GC could blip — but kids can't realistically hit a balloon faster than the animations finish.
- Word-reveal % sampling uses `ctx.getImageData()` which is the perf-heaviest call per pointermove. Throttled with `Math.random() < 0.15` (only ~15% of pointermove events run a check).
