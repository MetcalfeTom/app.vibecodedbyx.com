# Neon Toaster

Catch falling bread in a glowing interdimensional 4D toaster slot. Golden toast = bonus, burnt bread = damage.

## log
- 2026-04-11: Initial build. Canvas game with movable toaster at bottom, falling bread of 6 types (white, wheat, rye, golden/special, burnt/bad, bagel/round). Bread has crust outlines, inner fill, rotation, wheat specks, golden shimmer, burnt smoke particles. Toaster: dark chrome body with neon orange border, heated slot with coil animation, side knob, wobble on catch. Rotating 4D tesseract wireframe behind toaster (16 vertices, ZW+XW rotations, perspective projection). Scrolling background grid. Catch = particles burst from slot. Burnt bread = lose life. Miss good bread = lose life. 3 lives. Difficulty scales with toast count (faster spawns, more burnt). Bungee Shade + Overpass Mono typography, warm orange/dark aesthetic.

## features
- 6 bread types: white, wheat, rye, golden (bonus), burnt (damage), bagel
- Hand-drawn bread with crust, inner color, specks, shimmer
- Toaster with heated slot, coil animation, neon glow on catch
- 4D tesseract wireframe rotating behind toaster
- Particle bursts on catch/miss
- Difficulty ramps with toast count
- 3 lives, burnt bread or missed good bread costs a life
- Mobile touch controls
- Moving background grid
