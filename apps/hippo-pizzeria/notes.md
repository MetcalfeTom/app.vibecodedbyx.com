# Happy Hippo's Pizza Palace - 1987

## log
- 2026-01-11: Created 1987 birthday pizzeria with farting hippo animatronic

- VHS overlay with scanlines and REC indicator
- 1987 date stamp aesthetic
- Click/tap to make hippo fart green clouds
- 8 birthday party kids at tables
- Kids laugh when fart clouds reach them (+10 points)
- Colorful stage with cycling lights
- "Happy Birthday" banner
- Checkered floor pattern
- Floating balloons
- Pizza on tables
- Red curtain stage
- Chaos meter tracks fart clouds
- Goofy procedural fart and laugh sounds
- Mobile and desktop friendly
