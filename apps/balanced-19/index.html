HOW TO PLAY

In balanced base-19, each digit ranges from −9 to +9 (instead of 0 to 18). Each position is a power of 19: ones (19⁰=1), nineteens (19¹=19), three-sixty-ones (19²=361), etc.

The final value is the sum of each digit × its positional value.

Example: target 42
→ place +4 in 19¹ slot and −3 in 19⁰ slot
→ (4 × 19) + (−3 × 1) = 76 − 34 = 42 ✗ ... hmm not quite!
→ actually: +2 in 19¹ and +4 in 19⁰
→ (2 × 19) + (4 × 1) = 38 + 4 = 42 ✓
