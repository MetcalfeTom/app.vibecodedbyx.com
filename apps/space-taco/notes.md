# Space Taco

## log
- 2026-01-17: Initial creation
  - 3D space shooter with taco spaceship
  - Neon salsa projectiles
  - Space foxes as enemies with helmets and jetpacks
  - Jalapeño stars in background
  - Score and combo system
  - Salsa power meter
  - Game over and restart

## features
- 3D flying taco spaceship
  - Shell, lettuce, meat, cheese, tomatoes
  - Engine glow
  - WASD/Arrow movement
- Neon salsa projectiles
  - Red/orange glowing blobs
  - Yellow particle trail
  - Click or Space to shoot
  - Salsa power meter (regenerates)
- Space foxes
  - Orange fox with space helmet
  - Jetpack with cyan flames
  - Glowing magenta eyes
  - Float and wobble animation
  - Spawn periodically
- Jalapeño stars (100)
  - Green glowing peppers
  - Twinkle animation
  - Replace traditional stars
- Salsa explosion particles on hit
- Combo system
  - Chain hits for multipliers
  - "SPICY!" combo text
- Score tracking
- Foxes salsafied counter
- Game over on collision
- Restart button

## design
- Bangers + Orbitron fonts
- Yellow, orange, red, green neon palette
- Dark purple space background
- Mexican food + space aesthetic

## controls
- WASD or Arrow keys: Move taco
- Click or Space: Shoot salsa
- Avoid foxes, shoot them with salsa
