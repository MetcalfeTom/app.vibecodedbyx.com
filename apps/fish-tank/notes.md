# Neon Fish Tank

## log
- 2026-01-13: Created neon fish tank with glowing fish and bubble particles

## features
- Glowing neon fish with 10 color varieties
- Realistic fish movement with tail wagging
- Fish swim at different speeds and depths
- Vertical drift behavior
- Bubble particles rising and popping
- Swaying seaweed/plants with glow
- Sand floor with rocks
- Animated water surface ripples
- Click/tap anywhere to add fish (max 50)
- Bubbles spawn at click location
- Toggle bubbles on/off
- Clear tank button
- Fish counter display
- Mobile touch support
