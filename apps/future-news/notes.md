# Future News Ticker

## log
- 2026-01-05: Initial creation - cyberpunk news ticker with user submissions

## features
- Scrolling marquee ticker with "BREAKING" label
- User-submitted headlines from the future
- Year selection (2030-3000)
- Custom news source field
- Optional submitter callsign
- Real-time updates via Supabase subscription
- Retro cyberpunk aesthetic

## design
- CRT scanlines and flicker effect
- Neon cyan/magenta color scheme
- Orbitron + Share Tech Mono fonts
- Glowing text effects
- Scan animation on headline cards
- Dark terminal aesthetic

## database
- Table: future_headlines
- Columns: headline, year, source, submitter_name, user_id, created_at
