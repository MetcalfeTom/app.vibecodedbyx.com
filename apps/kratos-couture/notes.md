# Kratos Couture

## log
- 2026-01-07: Initial creation - Kratos walks the runway in designer fashion
  - Red-soled Prada stiletto heels that CLICK like thunder
  - Leather handbag carrying his Blades of Chaos
  - Designer gold sash across muscular torso
  - Paparazzi flashes and rose throws
  - Applause meter gameplay

## features
- Kratos in red-soled stiletto heels (Louboutin-style)
- Leather designer handbag with blade handles poking out
- Gold chain strap on handbag
- Pose for camera flashes (SPACE)
- Pirouette spin move (P)
- Heel clicks create sparkle effects and score
- Paparazzi take photos for bonus points
- Roses thrown when posing
- Applause meter - keep the crowd entertained!
- Fashion runway with spotlights

## controls
- LEFT/RIGHT: Strut the runway (heels click!)
- SPACE: Strike a pose (camera flash + roses)
- P: Pirouette spin

## mechanics
- Walking generates heel click sounds and points
- Posing gains major style points and applause
- Pirouettes are worth the most points
- Applause meter depletes over time
- Game ends when applause hits zero
- Keep strutting to maintain crowd energy!
