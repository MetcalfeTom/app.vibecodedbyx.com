# Ghost Scanner

Sweep a dark room with your scanner to find hidden ghosts before time runs out.

## log
- 2026-04-16: Initial build. Canvas dark room with 9 furniture silhouettes, scanner light cone follows cursor, EMF proximity bar. 5 ghost types with unique behaviors (drift, lurk, dart, pulse, chase). Ghosts only visible within scanner radius, eyes follow cursor. Click to capture when visible. 60s timer, score with time bonus. Scan pulse effect, capture particles, scanline overlay. Nosifer + Share Tech Mono typography, green-on-black terminal aesthetic.

## features
- Dark room with barely-visible furniture
- Scanner light cone follows cursor/touch
- EMF proximity meter (green→yellow→red)
- 5 ghost types: Whisper (drift), Shade (lurk), Flicker (dart), Hollow (pulse), Screamer (chase)
- Ghosts have wobbly ethereal bodies with tracking eyes
- Click to capture visible ghosts
- 60-second time limit
- Score with time bonus per capture
- Scan pulse ripple effect
- Ambient floating particles and scanline effect
- Touch support
