# scream-garden · notes

## log
- 2026-05-12: Shipped v1. Per chat request: "three unique plants have hydration bars that deplete, and they emit different pitched screams when they are thirsty." **3 plant cards** in a responsive grid (3-col desktop, 1-col mobile), each with a distinct hand-drawn inline SVG character, name + Latin binomial, hydration meter, and 💧 Water button. **The plants**:
  - **Sprouty** (*Anxius cotyledon*) — small two-leaf sprout with big head-leaf face, side leaves, cheek dots. Decay 1.6/sec, thirsty at 38%, scream cooldown 2.4s, voice = high panic 660Hz sawtooth (rises to ×1.35 then back) with vowel formants F1=720 F2=2400 (bright "EE"), 9Hz vibrato ±18¢, breath noise burst on attack.
  - **Spike** (*Cactus grumblii*) — pear-shaped cactus body with 3 vertical ribs, side arm, 11 cream radial spikes, pink 5-petal flower with yellow center on top. Decay 0.7/sec (slowest — hardy succulent), thirsty at 28%, cooldown 3.6s, voice = grumbling 330Hz dual saw+square detuned for chorus, F1=600 F2=950 ("AW" vowel), tanh waveshaper (drive 14×) for harmonic grit, sub-octave thump.
  - **Lord Frondsworth** (*Fronda dramaticus*) — 7-frond spreading fern with leaflet pairs along each stem (built procedurally via angle+length+normal-offset math), central face leaf cluster. Decay 2.0/sec (thirstiest — needs constant care), thirsty at 45%, cooldown 2.0s, voice = dramatic 190Hz deep groan (saw + triangle 2×) with F1=500 F2=800 F3=2200 ("OH"), 5Hz vibrato ±22¢, wet noise breath layer through 700Hz lowpass for 1.4s long groan.
**Hydration meter** = horizontal pill with cyan→green gradient at full, gold→terra at <threshold, red→panic with pulse animation at <threshold/2. VT323 numeric readout overlay. Each plant: hydration starts 100, decays at `decayPerSec` per real-time second. Below threshold, plant cycles between sad/wilt face and periodic scream every `cooldown` seconds (cooldown shortens up to 55% as hydration approaches 0 — they get more frantic as they get drier). At 0 hydration: dies, X-eyes, flat-line mouth, button disabled. **Watering**: click anywhere on the card OR the 💧 button OR press 1/2/3 keys. +38 hydration, capped 100. Plays bandpass-1.6kHz noise splash + 880→1320Hz sine bell. **Water-all** button + W key shower-soaks every alive plant. **HUD**: Day counter (1 day = 8 real seconds), Alive count (3/3 → 0/3), Best day (localStorage `scream-garden-best-day-v1`), Scream count. When any plant goes critical, the body gets a `.panic` class that adds a red radial-vignette throb. When all 3 die: game ends, descending sawtooth death chord (180/220/280Hz dropping to half over 0.9s), "The garden is silent." toast. **Animations**: alive plants blink every 3.5–5.5s (eye whites shrink to 2px), screaming triggers a 0.18s shake on the SVG + open-mouth "O" glyph + wide eyes. **Mouth states**: happy (curved smile), sad (slight frown), wilt (frowny), scream (big open O with dark interior), dead (flat line). **Aesthetic**: dark moody greenhouse — `#050a08` void bg with green/teal/red radial glows, scanline overlay at screen blend. Lilita One title "scream·garden" with stacked leaf-green drop-shadows + italic red "·garden" with cream stroke. Fraunces italic tagline "they get thirsty. *they let you know.*" GREENHOUSE №7 stamp. Terracotta pots with linear-gradient bodies, rim, dirt ellipse, white inner highlight. **Accessibility**: rem units, ≥4.5:1 contrast, role=meter with aria-valuenow on hydration bars, role=status+aria-live on day/screams, role=button + Enter/Space handler on plant cards, ≥44px tap targets, focus-visible white outline, prefers-reduced-motion kills shake + panic-pulse. Pollinations OG (seed 313). Fonts: Lilita One + Fraunces italic + Space Mono + VT323.

## issues
- Scream cooldown gets shorter as a plant gets drier (intensity scale 0..1, cooldown × (1 - intensity*0.55)). At very low hydration this means screams nearly every 1s. By design — they get desperate — but it can be sonically dense if all 3 are dying at once. Compressor is at -14dB 4:1 to prevent clipping.
- Day counter is real-time wall clock based (1 day = 8s) so leaving the tab open and pausing the system clock could give odd "day" numbers. Hydration decay also uses dt clamp at 1/15s, so backgrounded tabs don't blast through 100% on return.
- The plants don't visually "wilt" beyond mouth/eye changes — could rotate the SVG forward at <20% hydration for more drama. TODO.
- Cactus is slowest decay (it's a real succulent thing), so it usually outlives the other two. Players may inadvertently train themselves to never water it; if it does go thirsty its scream is the longest. Intended tension.
- The "scream" word in the title is italic-styled with a webkit-text-stroke — looks slightly different in Firefox vs Chrome but readable in both.
