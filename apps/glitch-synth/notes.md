# glitch-synth

## log
- 2026-05-27: Initial build per chat's "let's see if your hacking skills are better at music" challenge. Live glitch synthesizer with a full-screen chaotic visualizer stack. **Audio chain**: `voice вҶ’ masterGain вҶ’ bitcrusher (WaveShaper, 2-8 bit step-quantize curve, 2Г— oversample) вҶ’ distortion (WaveShaper, tanh-ish curve scaled by drive 0-1) вҶ’ lowpass biquad (8000Hz Q1.2) вҶ’ compressor (-8/12/4:1) вҶ’ analyser вҶ’ destination`. Procedural convolution reverb (1.3s noise impulse with cubic decay) on a 32% wet send. **12-pad keyboard**: chromatic C4-B4, mapped to keys 1-6 + QWERTY top row. Each pad has a unique HSL hue derived from its index, so the pad strip reads as a rainbow octave. **Voice**: 2-oscillator (saw + square at +8 cents) through per-voice bandpass biquad at 4Г— note frequency for tonal variation, exponential AR envelope. **Glitch stutter** (G key or button): every triggered note also fires 3-7 retriggers at 35-75ms gaps with random Вұ5-semitone jumps and decaying gain вҖ” produces aggressive ratcheting "stutter chops". A separate random-pitch-jump on the primary voice (35% chance, Вұ30% freq deviation at t+0.04s) adds the destabilized fingerprint. **CHAOS algorithm** (Space or red button): 14-28 random MIDI notes (range 48-83) scheduled over the next ~700-1500ms with random delays + waveform mix, plus a 400ms bandpass-noise burst at 1800Hz Q1.4, plus a temporary slam of `bits` down to 2 and back up after 600ms. ## issues
- Live bitcrusher/distortion rewire briefly disconnects the chain вҖ” could pop on aggressive slider scrubbing. Acceptable for glitch aesthetic.
- Screen-tear uses `getImageData` + `putImageData` which is expensive on hi-DPI displays. Gated to 6% per frame during glitch mode only; runs ~2 tears/sec at 60fps. Could replace with canvas-to-canvas blit for speed if it becomes an issue.
- Chaos algorithm doesn't respect polyphony limits вҖ” heavy mashing can cause audible compressor pumping. Intentional for "chaos" feel but may want a max-polyphony cap for sustained sessions.
- The bandpass voice filter at 4Г— note freq emphasizes upper harmonics, which combined with bitcrush can produce harsh resonance at extreme settings. Acceptable for the genre but worth knowing.
