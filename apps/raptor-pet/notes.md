## features
- Utahraptor pet grows by eating peanut butter
- The Incredible Gassy ally with toxic green gas clouds
- Gas clouds block incoming red bullets
- Sail bombs - throwable explosives that destroy bullets in area
- Sloppy's Clothes - rare magenta shirt with "S" logo
  - Grants 10 seconds of invincibility
  - Raptor wears the shirt visually
  - Glowing magenta aura when active
- Peanut butter pickups (golden jars)
- Bomb pickups (sail bomb icons)
- Growing raptor with feathers and claws
- Bullet dodging survival gameplay

## controls
- CLICK: Drop peanut butter
- SPACE: Spray toxic gas cloud
- B: Throw sail bomb
- Buttons also available on screen

## characters
### Utahraptor
- Orange/red feathered dinosaur
- Eating animation when consuming peanut butter
- Grows larger with each peanut butter eaten
- Has feathered crest and sharp claws

### The Incredible Gassy
- Green blob character
- Friendly expression, opens mouth when spraying
- Creates toxic green clouds that block bullets
- Gas regenerates over time

## items
### Peanut Butter (PB jars)
- Drop from clicking
- Golden pickups spawn and float across screen
- Each jar grows raptor by 0.15x size
- 100 points per jar eaten

### Sail Bombs
- Throwable explosive with sail on top
- Arc trajectory, explodes on ground
- Destroys all bullets in 150px radius
- Pickups spawn occasionally

### Sloppy's Clothes (RARE)
- Magenta shirt with white "S" logo
- Floating with sparkles around it
- 10 seconds of invincibility
- 500 bonus points
- Raptor visually wears the shirt

## enemies
### Farting Giraffe
- Tall yellow giraffe with brown spots
- Spawns from screen edges, max 2 at a time
- Chases raptor's x position
- LETHAL LICK ATTACK: Pink tongue strikes at raptor when close
- GAS CLOUD MECHANIC: Releases brown/yellow fart clouds
- Fart clouds are lethal to raptor on contact
- Player gas clouds neutralize giraffe farts
- Gas clouds can destroy giraffes (3 HP each)
- 350 points for defeating a giraffe
- 30 points for neutralizing fart clouds
- Sloppy's Clothes grants immunity to both attacks

### Burping Mole Rat
- Pink wrinkly naked mole rat creature
- Spawns from screen edges, max 3 at a time
- Chases raptor's x position along the ground
- Burps feather clouds toward raptor
- Feather clouds stun raptor for 2 seconds
- Gas clouds can destroy mole rats (2 HP each)
- Gas clouds also destroy feather clouds
- 200 points for defeating a mole rat
- 25 points for destroying feather clouds
- Sloppy's Clothes grants immunity to stun
