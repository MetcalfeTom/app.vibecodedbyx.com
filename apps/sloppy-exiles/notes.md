# sloppy-exiles

## log
- 2026-05-03: Created. **Isometric click-to-move ARPG** — Diablo / Path of Exile arcade-flavoured slice. Single-file canvas 2D, no build step. **Iso projection**: 64×32 tile (2:1 ratio), `worldToScreen` / `screenToWorld` helpers convert (gridX, gridY) ↔ pixel space. 24×24 tile grid, border tiles + 12 interior pillars are blockers. Camera lerps toward player at `dt × 6` rate. **Player** at grid centre — cloaked figure with hood, glowing ember-orange eyes, dagger held to facing side. Click anywhere on stone → walk there; click an enemy → walk into auto-attack range. **Combat**: auto-attack triggers when an enemy is in `attackRange = 1.4` tiles, 0.6s cooldown, damage = `attackDmg * meleeMul × (crit ? 2 : 1)`. **3 active skills** unlocked via the skill tree, fired with `1 / 2 / 3`: Cleave (8 mp, 1.6s cd, 2.4-tile AOE for 2× weapon damage), Frost Bolt (12 mp, 1.0s cd, ranged 1.6× projectile that chills targets 2s @ 45% speed), Life Drain (20 mp, 4.5s cd, 3-tile AOE 1.8× damage + heals 25% of total). **Enemies**: skeleton mobs with bone-pixel sprite (legs + ribcage + skull + jaw), per-frame flash on hit, frost halo when chilled, level label + HP pip above. Spawn cadence ramps `Math.max(0.6, 2.0 - waveT * 0.04)` — wave gets denser as time passes. **Loot**: 60% drop chance per kill, 4 item slots (weapon/helm/robe/ring), 5 rarity tiers with weighted roll (60/25/12/2.5/0.5%): common/uncommon/rare/epic/legendary, color-coded gold→green→yellow→purple→orange. Stat scales with rarity multiplier (1.0/1.4/1.9/2.6/3.5×) and player level. Auto-equip on pickup if upgrade. Equipped stats compose: weapon = +attackDmg, helm = +maxHp×2, robe = +maxMp×2, ring = +crit chance. **Skill tree** with 3 branches × 3 nodes: Combat (Sharper Strike, Cleave, Berserker — under 30% HP gives +60% dmg), Arcane (Mana Affinity, Frost Bolt, Mana Surge), Shadow (Vitality, Life Drain, Shadow Strike — +15% crit). Branches gate by prereq node; each costs 1-2 skill points. Earn 1 point per level-up. **Inventory**: 32-slot grid + 4 equipped-slot row showing what's worn. Click an item to swap it into the matching slot (current item bumps to inventory). **Atmosphere**: dark void background, per-tile cracked-stone shading with crack lines + occasional purple rune glyphs (ᚠᚱᛞᛟᛇᛉᛗ), pillars rendered with proper iso 3D faces (left/right/top) + blood-red ember dots on top, drifting orange ember particles upward, big radial vignette. **Audio**: Web Audio synth — hit (sawtooth + square), crit (square + triangle pair), cast (triangle two-tone), pickup (bright square), level-up (4-note ascending arpeggio), death (3-note descending), bone-crack (sawtooth + filtered noise burst). **HUD**: TL level, gold, skill points; bottom row HP bar + 3 skill icons + mana bar; centre-bottom XP bar. Toast banner for loot pickups, level-ups, system messages. **Aesthetic**: UnifrakturCook display title "Sloppy Exiles" with blood + ink drop shadow, Cinzel for stat labels, IM Fell English italic for body. Palette deep blood-red `#a82226`, ember `#ff8a33`, rune-purple `#a86bff`, bone `#cfb98a`, gold `#d9a13a`. Pollinations OG, ⚔️ favicon. **Accessibility**: rem units throughout, no html font-size override, ≥4.5:1 contrast (paper on near-black HUD cells, gold-on-black accents), semantic `<main>`/`<aside>`, canvas `role="application"` with full control summary aria-label, overlays `role="dialog" aria-modal="true"` toggling `inert`, focus-visible 4px gold outline, `prefers-reduced-motion` skips the dmg-pop float animation, skip link to canvas.

## issues
- Pathfinding is direct-line-with-blocksAt only — no A*. Player walking to a clicked point on the far side of a pillar will get stuck against the pillar's edge. They can re-click around it, but a proper grid A* would feel better.
- Camera doesn't auto-pan toward enemies (e.g. on damage taken) — player can be partially off-screen if running away from a chasing mob with the lerped camera trailing.
- Enemy AI is simple "walk straight at player". They bunch up and don't try to flank.
- No pause menu — Esc just toggles a paused state with a banner; pressing T or I does open the skill tree / inventory which paused the game, but there's no traditional pause-menu UI.
- Projectile collision uses simple distance check, no path sweep — at very high speeds a frost bolt could tunnel past a slim enemy.
