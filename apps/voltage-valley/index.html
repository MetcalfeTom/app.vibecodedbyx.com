Round 1 Auction

How To Play

Light up cities. First to power 6 cities with fuel in the Power phase wins.

Phase · Auction

Click a plant (top row, "current market") to start bidding. Pay at least the plant's number. Opponents may counter-bid. You may skip bidding once per round.

Phase · Fuel

Click fuel cells in the Market to stock a plant's bunker. Fuel prices rise as supply drops.

Phase · Build

Click any highlighted city to wire it. Pay the connection cost on the edges + 10₴ per city slot taken.

Phase · Power

Click your plants to fire them. Fuel is consumed, cities up to that plant's capacity light up & pay income.

Income Scale

0 cities → 10₴, 1 → 22, 2 → 33, 3 → 44, 4 → 54, 5 → 64, 6 → 73.
