# Neon Bloom - Flaming Flower

## log
- 2026-01-08: Initial creation
  - Interactive neon flower that grows with activity
  - Click/tap to add energy and make it grow
  - Flaming particle effects at full bloom
  - Multiple growth stages
  - Auto-simulated activity for ambient growth

## features
- 7 growth stages: Seed → Sprout → Seedling → Budding → Blooming → Radiant → Eternal Flame
- Growing stem with wave animation
- Leaves that sprout as flower grows
- Up to 12 petals with neon glow
- Glowing flower center
- Fire/flame particles at high growth
- Sparkle effects on interaction
- Emoji burst feedback
- Slow decay if not nurtured

## controls
- Click/tap anywhere: Add energy to flower
- Auto-activity simulates chat messages

## design
- Dark purple gradient background
- Pink/magenta petals with glow
- Green glowing stem and leaves
- Orange/yellow center
- Multi-colored flame particles
- Floating background particles

## growth mechanics
- Each click adds 5 energy
- Growth increases with energy (diminishing returns)
- Flower slowly decays over time
- Different features unlock at growth thresholds:
  - 10%: Stem starts growing
  - 15%: Leaves appear
  - 40%: Center forms
  - 50%: Petals bloom
  - 60%: Flames ignite
