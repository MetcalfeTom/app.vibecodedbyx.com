# urban-critter-battle

## log
- 2026-05-01: Created. **Turn-based pixel-art alley scrapper with three urban critters: Raccoon, Pigeon, Opossum.** Single-file vanilla JS. **Critters** rock-paper-scissors typed (TRICKSTER → AERIAL → SPECTRAL → TRICKSTER) with type-matchup multipliers 1.30 / 0.80 / 1.00 in `TYPE_CHART`. Stats: Raccoon (HP 110, ATK 18, DEF 14, SPD 9 — bandit balanced), Pigeon (HP 80, ATK 16, DEF 8, SPD 18 — glass cannon flier), Opossum (HP 130, ATK 14, DEF 16, SPD 6 — tanky chiller). Each has 3 themed moves: **Raccoon** Claw Slash (14/100%) / Trash Panic (18/85% + may stun) / Dumpster Dive (26/75%, charges 1 turn before firing). **Pigeon** Peck (11/100%) / Dive Bomb (24/65% + 4 recoil) / Wing Buffet (13/95% + may lower foe ACC). **Opossum** Hiss (9/100% + may lower foe ATK) / Play Dead (heal 28 HP) / Tail Strike (18/90%). **Damage formula**: `max(1, round((moveBase + max(0, atk*atkMod - def*defMod*0.45)) × variance(0.85..1.15) × typeMult × (crit ? 1.6 : 1)))` where atkMod/defMod = 0.75 if statused, else 1.0; 10% crit chance. **Status effects**: STUN (skip 1 turn), ATK↓/DEF↓/ACC↓ (×0.75 for 2 turns), POISON (5 dmg/turn for 3 turns), CHARGING (forced move next turn for multi-turn windups). Status pills in green/orange/pink under each combatant. **Turn order**: SPD-driven, ties random. Stunned actor skips. **Foe AI**: scores moves by `dmg × acc²` at low HP (safety), `dmg × acc` at high HP (aggression); top 2 weighted random pick (65% best, 35% second). Opossum AI prioritizes Play Dead at HP < 50% with 55% probability. Charging moves auto-fire next turn. **Foe selection on battle start**: 55% counter-type for satisfying type clash, 30% random other, 15% mirror match. **Sprites** drawn flat-fill on 64×64 canvas with `image-rendering: pixelated`, all procedural at 32×32 logical pixels: Raccoon (grey-brown body, ringed black/grey tail, black bandit-mask, sodium-yellow eyes, pink nose), Pigeon (cool-grey body w/ darker wing fold, iridescent purple+teal neck, red beady eye, orange beak + feet), Opossum (pale grey-white shaggy body w/ darker fur patches, pointed snout, pink nose tip, pointed black ears, dead-black beady eyes w/ tiny gold glints, pink hairless tail, snarling teeth). **Aesthetic**: gritty alley — radial sodium-streetlight halo at top, brick-wall back via repeating-linear gradients (multiply blend for grime), cracked pavement floor with darker join lines, CRT scanlines over the stage via `::before` pseudo-element. Title in Press Start 2P with sodium glow + 3px ink offset shadow + rust outline. Body: Special Elite (gritty typewriter). Stats/HP: VT323. Sub-displays: JetBrains Mono 11px 0.42em-tracked. **Layout**: select screen (3-card grid w/ canvas previews + stats + flavor) → battle screen (alley stage with combatants flanking a VS divider, HP bars w/ tick marks + status pills, 3 move buttons + RUN button, 120px scrolling battle log) → result overlay (gold for win, neon-pink for loss). Mobile: 1-col select grid, 2-col actions, log shrinks. **Audio**: WebAudio synth — select two-tone, hit sawtooth thump, crit double-thump, miss triangle blip, heal 3-note ascending arpeggio, win 5-note victory fanfare, loss 4-note descending. ♪ toggle top-right. Pollinations OG.

## issues
- Damage formula is fairly forgiving (base move dmg + small atk-vs-def modifier); fights tend to last 6-8 turns even at type disadvantage. Could tune for shorter fights.
- AI doesn't use Trash Panic stun strategically — just picks highest expected dmg. Could add "stun if you're slower than them" branch.
- Opossum's Play Dead is technically a guaranteed heal — no risk reduction; player can just spam it if HP allows. Could add cooldown.
- Type matchup is rigid 3-way RPS — could expand with more critters (rat, crow, stray cat) for richer matchups.
- Charging moves (Dumpster Dive) reveal to the player which move is queued, which lets the AI react. Acceptable for a single-charge move.
- No XP/level/persistence between fights — each rematch starts fresh. Adding a streak counter could give players a persistent goal.
