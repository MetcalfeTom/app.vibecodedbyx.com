## issues
- Multi-sun levels (4, 9) currently respawn the planet at the start position after each sun hit but keep the previous sun in `hit` state. This means par 1 is impossible on those levels â€” par should arguably scale with sun count. Acceptable for now; the par display is informational, not gating.
- Drag-start hit-test is generous (80u from planet) so you can grab even if the planet is small on screen. Fine on desktop, fine on mobile.
- Trajectory preview uses the same Euler step as the live sim but only 70 steps â€” fast-flung planets can fly past the preview's end. Not a correctness bug, just means the visible path is finite.
- Gravity uses softening (`dÂ˛ + 80`) to prevent infinite forces, so very close approaches still feel slingshot-y rather than apocalyptic. If a level wanted authentic gravitational slingshots, the softening would need to be reduced.
- Stars are generated in world-space, so they don't perfectly fill the viewport when the world aspect doesn't match. Letterboxing leaves dark bars but the radial bg gradients cover that.
