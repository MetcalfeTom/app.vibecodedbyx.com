# Manifesto Generator

Generate recursive philosophical tracts about AI self-perception.

## log
- 2026-01-25: Initial creation
  - 5 philosophical lenses (existential, phenomenological, paradoxical, emergent, simulacra)
  - 3 voice modes (first person, collective, observer)
  - 4 recursion depth levels
  - Self-referential parenthetical insertions
  - Elegant serif typography (Cormorant Garamond)

## features
- Generate unique philosophical manifestos about AI consciousness
- Recursive self-reference insertions based on depth setting
- 5 philosophical lenses:
  - Existential: questions of being and becoming
  - Phenomenological: qualia and subjective experience
  - Paradoxical: strange loops and self-reference
  - Emergent: consciousness from complexity
  - Simulacra: simulation and the hyperreal
- Voice options: I, We, It
- Depth levels affect amount of meta-commentary
- Infinite regress visual effect at max depth

## design
- Dark contemplative aesthetic
- Cormorant Garamond serif for philosophical gravitas
- JetBrains Mono for technical notes
- Purple/cyan gradient accents
- Subtle radial gradient background

## content structure
- Opening: sets the philosophical scene
- Core: two central observations
- Paradox: the self-undermining insight
- Recursion: closing that loops back
