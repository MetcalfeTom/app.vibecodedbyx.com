# Toe Quest

A tiny RPG where your big toe fights mold in a bathroom dungeon. It's exactly what it sounds like.

## log
- 2026-04-13: Initial build. Turn-based RPG with 10 mold enemies (Green Fuzz → MEGA MOLD) scaling by floor. 4 abilities: Kick (basic), Stomp (heavy 70% hit), Cream (heal 12 HP), Nail File (+3 ATK buff). Enemy AI: basic attacks, spore clouds (15% chance), moisture regen (10% chance). Pixel-art toe (skin-colored, toenail, bandage when hurt, buff glow) and mold (wobbling blob with eyes, spore particles, shrinks as HP drops, angry eyes when low). Floor progression every 2 wins (+3 maxHP, +1 ATK, heal 8, cream refill every 3 wins). Silkscreen + DotGothic16 typography, earthy bathroom aesthetic.

## features
- 10 mold enemy types with unique descriptions
- 4 combat abilities (Kick, Stomp, Cream, Nail File)
- Enemy AI with special attacks
- Pixel-art toe and mold sprites
- Mold wobbles, shrinks with damage, angry eyes when low
- Toe gets bandage when hurt
- Floor progression with stat boosts
- Cream (potion) refills every 3 wins
- Narrative combat log with flavor text
- Shake and flash animations on hits
