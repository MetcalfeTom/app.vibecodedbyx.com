# Neon Survivors

Vampire Survivors-style auto-battler roguelike. Move to dodge, auto-fire at nearest enemies, collect XP gems, level up with upgrade picks, survive endless waves.

## log
- 2026-04-11: Initial build. 2400x2400 arena, WASD/arrow/touch joystick controls. Auto-targeting projectiles at nearest enemy. 5 enemy types (drone, tank, swarm, ghost, brute) with progressive unlocking. 13 upgrades: damage, speed, fire rate, extra projectile, blast radius, pierce, XP magnet, max HP, regen, orbital shields, crit chance, luck, laser beam. Difficulty ramps over time (spawn rate, batch size, HP scaling, boss waves every 60s). Minimap, screen shake, damage numbers, XP bar, HP ring. Chakra Petch + Fira Code typography, neon green-on-dark aesthetic with glow effects.

## features
- Auto-fire at nearest enemy within range
- 5 enemy types with increasing difficulty tiers
- 13 upgrade options (pick 1 of 3 on level up)
- Laser beam: piercing line attack every 2s, damages all enemies in path
- Orbital shields: spinning orbs that damage nearby enemies
- XP magnet attracts gems, luck multiplies XP
- Enemy separation physics (prevents stacking)
- Progressive difficulty: faster spawns, larger batches, HP scaling
- Boss waves every 60 seconds
- Minimap with enemy/player positions
- Screen shake on hits
- Floating damage numbers with crit highlight
- Touch joystick for mobile
- HP ring around player
