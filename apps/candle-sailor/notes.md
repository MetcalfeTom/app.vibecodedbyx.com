# Candle Sailor

## log
- 2026-01-17: Initial creation
  - Pixel art sailing game with dark moody ocean
  - Small pixel sailboat with tilting animation
  - Red bearish candles to dodge
  - Green bullish candles for bonus points (+50)
  - Dark atmospheric sky with stars and crescent moon
  - Multi-layered animated ocean waves
  - Fog effect at bottom
  - Press Start 2P pixel font
  - Sound effects (collect, crash, start)
  - Mobile touch controls
  - High score persistence in localStorage
  - Increasing difficulty over time

## features
- Pixel art aesthetic throughout
- Dark moody atmosphere (navy/purple tones)
- Animated starfield with twinkling
- Crescent moon in sky
- 8 layers of animated ocean waves
- Wave highlights with subtle lines
- Fog effect at water bottom
- Pixel sailboat with:
  - Dark wood hull
  - Animated sail (wind effect)
  - Small flag
  - Tilt based on movement
  - Bobbing on waves
- Red candles (bearish) - instant death
- Green candles (bullish) - 50 points + splash effect
- Particle splash effects
- Web Audio API sounds
- Score increases over time
- Game speed increases with score

## controls
- Arrow keys or A/D to steer left/right
- Mobile: on-screen arrow buttons

## gameplay
- Sail through dark bearish waters
- Dodge the falling red candles
- Collect green candles for bonus points
- Survive as long as possible
- Speed increases over time
