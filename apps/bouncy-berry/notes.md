# Bouncy Berry

## log
- 2026-01-18: Initial creation
  - Neon glowing strawberry emoji
  - Click to jump to random position
  - Bouncy squish animations
  - Web Audio API sound effects
  - Particle and glow ring effects

## features
- Click strawberry to make it jump randomly
- Click anywhere else to make it jump there
- Squish animation on click
- Bounce animation on landing
- Particle burst (sparkles, hearts, stars)
- Expanding glow ring effect
- Trail effect showing previous position
- Pop and boing sound effects
- Sound toggle button
- Click counter
- Responsive to window resize

## design
- Fredoka font
- Dark purple/teal gradient background
- Neon pink glow on strawberry
- Pink accent color (#ff3366)
- Smooth cubic-bezier transitions

## controls
- Click strawberry: jump to random position
- Click anywhere: strawberry jumps to that spot
- Sound toggle: mute/unmute effects
