# neon-cat-vibe

## log
- 2026-05-06: shipped — neon platformer. A glowing tabby leaps through an endless-procedural digital world, dodges giant spray bottles, and unleashes a sonic purr to distract them.
  - **Cat character (vector pixel-style)**: rounded-ellipse pink/magenta body with gradient + neon shadow, round head with two triangle ears (gold inner ear), cyan glowing eye-slits with shadow blur, gold nose, white whiskers, four small leg ovals, animated **tail with neon glow + sin-wave swish**. Squash on landing (scale lerps 0.7 → 1 over ~125 ms), facing flips with `scale(facing × ...)`. Pink-or-gold trail (gold while shielded) of last 10 positions when moving fast.
  - **Movement**: 290 px/s run speed, 1700 px/s² ground accel, 1100 px/s² air accel, 1900 px/s² gravity. Jump initial −680 vy, coyote time 0.12 s, jump buffer 0.10 s, **double-jump** (2 jumps reset on landing, +1 reset on stomping a spray bottle). **Variable jump height** (release Space early caps the rise to −260). **Dash** on Shift — 0.18 s burst at ±720 px/s with 0.18 s i-frames + bandpass-noise whoosh, 0.9 s cooldown. Touch controls show on coarse pointers: ◀ ▶ ▲ » ♪ buttons map to ArrowLeft / ArrowRight / Space / Shift / purr.
  - **Procedural endless level**: the world scrolls left at a base 240 px/s ramping with distance. `maybeSpawn()` runs while `lastSpawnX < worldX + 1.6 W`, picking weighted chunk patterns: simple ground / floating gap+platform / stair-step climbs / arc-of-orbs over a hole / spike pit with a shield orb. Old chunks get culled past the left edge.
  - **Spray bottle enemies (chat ask)**: tall translucent cylinder bottle with a cyan liquid surface, pink trigger handle (flips left/right with patrol direction), gold nozzle, angry brow + red eyes. Spawned on wide ground chunks after the first 600 px of warmup, with an increasing chance up to ~55% on far-out grounds. **Patrol AI**: walks back-and-forth between platform edges at 60 px/s. Every 1.6–3 s it samples the cat's position; if within 320 px horizontal and 90 px vertical, fires a horizontal **water blast** (cyan ellipse + trailing droplets) at 320 px/s + a high-pitched bandpass-noise spray. **Stomp test**: cat falling onto top kills the bottle (75 score, cyan particle burst, +1 jump reset, springy bounce); side-touch on an awake bottle hurts the cat. Dies into orbs of pink particles + zZz.
  - **Sonic purr distraction (chat ask)**: bound to **Q / E / P** (edge-triggered so holding doesn't keep retriggering) plus the ♪ touch button. Drains 50% of the **Vibe** meter, runs for 1.4 s, freezes every spray bottle within a 240 px radius for 3 seconds (drooped sleep pose with `zZz` particles + closed eyes). Also wipes any in-flight water blasts inside the radius. Visualized as **3 expanding cyan rings** spawned at the cat's screen position with staggered −0.12 s offsets, growing at 280–360 px/s with shadow-blur halo. Audio: 90 Hz sawtooth through a 600 Hz lowpass with a 22 Hz frequency-modulating LFO for the proper rumble-purr feel. Vibe meter regens at 32% / s (full recharge ~3 s) when not actively purring; live HUD pill shows percentage with `PURRING…` / `PURR · Q` / `CHARGING NN%` states.
  - **Yarn balls (chat ask)** — pink fluffy ball with concentric arc winding lines + a dangling thread tail. Spawns at ~10% chance per ground chunk (bumped to 20% if cat has fewer than 3 lives). Pickup grants `+1 life` (capped at 5), +25 score, magenta particle burst, three-note ascending chime.
  - **Golden milk bowls (chat ask)** — placed every 1800 px of distance on a small floating pedestal so they're always reachable. Half-ellipse gold chalice with a milk-white surface, a hovering halo crown, and four orbiting sparkles. Pickup awards **+500 score**, grants the gold shield aura ("blessed" state), 1.6 s i-frames, and fires an angelic ascending C-E-G-C-E arpeggio plus a fountain of gold particles.
  - **Damage flow**: spike contact, side-bottle contact, water-blast hit, or fall-off-screen → if shielded, shield breaks with a 1 s i-frame bounce; else `−1 life` + 1.4 s i-frame + bounce. At 0 lives the cat freezes mid-air and the game-over overlay slides in 800 ms later.
  - **HUD (top of screen)**: live Lives heart row (`♥ ♥ ♥` cyan-pulse pip when shielded), Score (Audiowide gold with 12-px shadow blur), Distance in meters (Audiowide cyan), and the new **Vibe meter** with cyan→magenta gradient fill. Lower-third on coarse pointers shows the touch buttons.
  - **Background**: deep purple-black `#0a0420` body with three radial corner glows (pink + cyan + violet). 60 parallax stars with `Math.sin`-twinkle. Synthwave horizon grid: glowing pink horizon line + cyan perspective bands + worldX-shifted vertical traces emanating from the vanishing point. Gradient floor below the horizon.
  - **Audio**: all Web Audio synth — jump (square sweep 440→880, gentler 660→1320 on double-jump), land (120→60 Hz sine thud), collect (880 + 1320 Hz triangle two-note), hurt (sawtooth 400→80 descend), dash (bandpass noise sweep), spray-bottle hiss (highpass-noise burst), purr (rumble described above), yarn (700/1100/1500 Hz triangle ascending), milk (5-note major arp + octave harmony), game over (4-note descending sawtooth).
  - **Persistence**: best score in `localStorage['neon-cat-jumper-best']` (key kept from the dev path) with a results card on game-over showing Score / Distance / Best.
  - **Aesthetic**: Audiowide title with pink + magenta neon shadow + cyan accent on `cat`, Cormorant Garamond italic tag, Press Start 2P keys, IBM Plex Mono HUD, `image-rendering: pixelated` on the canvas. Title screen wears `gone` class with opacity transition for a clean fade-out.
  - **Accessibility**: rem units, semantic `<canvas role="application">` with full keymap aria-label, role="status" live region on HUD, ≥5 rem touch buttons (well above the 44 px target), focus-visible cyan outlines, `prefers-reduced-motion` no-ops the shield-pip pulse, skip link to canvas.

## issues
- The water blast collision uses a fixed 22 px radius around the projectile centroid — corners of the cat sprite occasionally clip a blast that visually misses; could be tightened with a per-frame swept AABB if it becomes a problem.
- Spike pits sometimes overlap with the next ground chunk's enemy patrol if the rng is unlucky — the patrol's minX/maxX is clamped to its host platform so the bottle just gets a tiny patrol range, not a clip.
- The "blessed" state from milk grants the shield + i-frames but doesn't extend the shield's gold aura cap; double-shielding by collecting an arc-shield orb mid-blessing is benign (just same shield).
- Best score key is still `neon-cat-jumper-best` from the dev path — leaving it that way so existing sessions don't lose the record. Could migrate later.
