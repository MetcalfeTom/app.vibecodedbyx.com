# Neon Synth

## log
- 2026-01-13: Created neon audio synthesizer with step sequencer and rhythmic visualizer

## features
- 16-step sequencer with 6 note rows (pentatonic scale C4-C5)
- Real-time frequency spectrum visualizer with mirrored bars
- Color-coded rows (red, orange, yellow, green, cyan, magenta)
- Oscillator waveforms: sine, square, sawtooth, triangle
- Draggable knobs for:
  - Volume
  - Attack time
  - Decay time
  - Low-pass filter frequency
- Transport controls (play, pause, stop)
- Adjustable tempo (60-200 BPM)
- Genre presets:
  - Techno (130 BPM, square wave)
  - House (124 BPM, sine wave)
  - D&B (174 BPM, sawtooth wave)
  - Ambient (80 BPM, sine wave)
- Space bar to toggle play/pause
- Step preview on click
- Web Audio API synthesizer
- Neon cyberpunk aesthetic
- Mobile touch support
