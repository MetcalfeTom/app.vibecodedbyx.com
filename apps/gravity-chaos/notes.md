# Gravity Chaos

Chaotic physics playground — spawn neon balls, place gravity wells and repulsors, and watch orbital mayhem unfold.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. N-body physics with gravity wells (attractors) and repulsors. Balls: variable radius/mass, hue-based colors, velocity trails, specular highlights, speed-based glow. Wells: spinning spiral arms, concentric field rings, pulsing core on slingshot events (balls too close get ejected with energy boost). Repulsors: dashed field rings, reverse spiral rotation. Ball-ball elastic collision with mass-weighted impulse and overlap separation. Collision sparks. Wall bouncing with energy loss. Drag to fling balls with velocity preview line. BOOM button explodes everything outward. Right-click to remove wells. Starts with 3 wells (2 pull + 1 repel) and 20 balls. Fade trail rendering. 300 ball cap. Audiowide + Share Tech Mono typography, dark void with neon glow aesthetic.
