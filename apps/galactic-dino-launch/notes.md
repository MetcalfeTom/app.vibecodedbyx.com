# galactic-dino-launch

## log
- 2026-04-30: **Major refactor: Kitty Cannon vertical perspective + persistent shop with 1-5 upgrades + glass astronaut helmet pass.** The old 2D solar-system slingshot was replaced with a side-view cannon-and-bounce game. Cannon at world `(0, 6)` aims via ↑/↓ keys (or WASD), hold SPACE to charge (rate scales with CHARGE SPEED upgrade), release to launch. T-Rex flies through 6 px/m world space, gravity pulls down at `30 m/s²` (reduced by 70% above 1200m altitude for the "approaching space" feel). On ground impact (`y <= 0`): vy reflects with `0.62` bounce coefficient + vx scales by `0.92` friction + spin += vx*0.3 → multiple bounces possible like classic Kitty Cannon. Once moving slowly enough (`speed < 1.5` while grounded for >0.6s), run ends. **Camera** locks dino at 30% from left with smooth horizontal+vertical follow. **Sky gradient** shifts color with altitude (deep navy near ground → space-black above 600m), **stars fade in** above 600m altitude with twinkle. Sun draws in upper right. **Parallax hill layers**: 3 procedural sin/cos-blend layers (FAR / MID / NEAR) drawn at 0.3/0.55/0.85 parallax with darker → lighter purple silhouettes. Foreground grass tufts every 25m + distance markers every 100m on the ground. **Infinite procedural content**: jetpacks spawn at 60-140m intervals with random heights (14-104m), within a 1500m window ahead of the dino, removed when 400m behind; asteroids spawn similarly above 80m altitude in the high-flight zone. **Persistent shop** ("PREHISTORIC SPACE PROGRAM") with 4 tracks at levels 1-5: **LAUNCH POWER** (cannon force × 1.0..2.5), **JETPACK** (kick strength 8..16), **FUEL TANK** (capacity 100..240, drain 24..12/s), **CHARGE SPEED** (rate 0.012..0.028/frame). Costs scale 50/150/400/1000 per level (1600 to max one track, 6400 to max all). **Coins earned** on game-over: `floor(distance/25 + altitude/60 + bounces*2)`. Persisted to `localStorage['galactic-dino-economy-v1']`. Shop UI shows on intro AND game-over screens with shared bank counter, level pips (5 dots filled), buy buttons that auto-disable when broke or maxed, and a `buyChimeSfx` two-note rising sine on purchase. Both shop instances cross-refresh on any buy so they stay in sync. **Glass astronaut helmet** pass on the dino sprite: ellipse glass body at 0.18 opacity, additive lighter halo (radial 0.35 → 0 over 14u radius), gold rim band ring around the neck with 4 amber bolts at 90° intervals, curved white reflection arc on the upper-left, tiny pinprick highlight, all rendered AFTER the head so it reads as "T-Rex inside a bubble". **HUD**: distance / altitude / speed / fuel / coins, plus today's leaderboard panel. **Submission**: distance is the leaderboard score (was previously distance from Earth). **Game-over verdicts** rebuilt for the new game ("crossed the karman line", "rolled across the continent", "made it to the dunes", etc.). All upgrades + Supabase + audio + leaderboard + intro/game-over flow preserved.
- 2026-04-30: Created. **2D physics-based slingshot game where a helmeted T-Rex launches from Earth and falls through the solar system's gravity wells.** Single full-viewport canvas + Supabase-backed daily leaderboard via `galactic_dino_scores` table (`name, score, ymd, max_speed`, default RLS, indexed on `(ymd, score)` for fast top-N today queries). **Solar system**: 10 bodies — Sun (mass 1500), Mercury, Venus, Earth (the launch pad), Moon, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn (with elliptical ring), Uranus, Neptune — at `(x, y)` positions where 1u ≈ 1 gigameter for HUD readability. Mass values tuned for slingshot drama (Sun pulls hardest at 1500, Jupiter at 400). **Gravity** is summed each frame from all 10 bodies, softened with `+4` denominator term to avoid singularities. Asteroid belt (60 rocks) between Mars and Jupiter + Kuiper belt (80 rocks) past Neptune; collisions with rocks reflect the dino's velocity along the contact normal with an extra 1.4× rebound and add visual spin. Crashing into a planet (within `r + 1.2u`) ends the run with the planet's name in the verdict. **Launch**: aim with mouse, hold SPACE (or pointer-down) to fill a charge meter, release to fling. Power = `9 + charge*22` so a tap-launch can still reach orbit, full charge sends the dino past Jupiter on a good vector. **Mid-flight thrusters**: WASD/arrows fire small thrusters consuming fuel (24/s, 100 max) — useful for course-correcting around Saturn or kicking out of an inner-system trap. **Jetpack power-ups**: 14 cyan-glowing capsule pickups float along the route at varied y-offsets between 25 GM and 920 GM. Each has a pulsing radial halo, gold strap, red nozzle, sparkle highlight, and bobs via `sin(bob * 1.4)*0.6`. Touch one (within ~3u) and the dino gets a `kick = 9` velocity boost along its current heading + 1.5u outer-system bias + 25 fuel + a "+ JETPACK" floating Press Start 2P label that drifts up over 1.5s + a 5-note ascending C-E-G-C-E square arpeggio with sub-thump SFX. **T-Rex sprite**: chunky body ellipse (7.5×9.5), tapering tail with curl, 3-spike dorsal ridge, two chunky legs with claw markings, the iconic tiny T-Rex arms with even tinier hands, and a longer skull with jaw line + 2 visible teeth. Cyan-tinted transparent helmet dome (8×6 ellipse) covers the head. Cyan jetpack tank with gold strap visible on the back when zoomed > 0.6. Body rotates smoothly toward velocity with a 6/s lerp + asteroid-impact spin decay (`0.93^(60dt)`). **Camera**: smooth-follow + auto-zoom (1u → 24u distance threshold, zoom range 0.4–2.4) so the player can track the dino across hundreds of GM without the planets becoming unrecognizable. **Trail**: 200-point fading gold polyline behind the dino. **Starfield** parallax (220 stars, 30% camera-coupled) with twinkle. **HUD top-right**: DISTANCE GM (amber), SPEED km/s (KM = velocity × 12 for arcade scale), FUEL %. **Today's leaderboard** panel below HUD: Supabase query for `ymd = today` ordered by score DESC limit 10, refreshed every 12s + on game-over. Score insert is upsert-better-only (only updates if new score > existing for this user/day). **Game over** verdict messages tier by distance: stranded inner system / past Mars / made it to Jupiter / past Saturn / outer planets / interstellar. **Audio**: rocket boom + sub thump on launch (intensity-scaled by charge), saw whoosh per thruster pulse, square+sub bump on asteroid hit, noise+sub explosion on planet crash, and the ascending arp on jetpack pickup. **Controls**: aim mouse, hold SPACE (or tap+hold canvas) to charge, WASD/arrows for thrusters, R to restart. Mobile-friendly touch (charge by holding pointer down). Pollinations OG.

## issues
- Aim arrow uses `ctx.translate/rotate` to draw the arrowhead which then does a little setTransform dance to restore. Works but inelegant; should use save/restore around the arrowhead instead.
- Charge mechanic doesn't show a visible "max" tick — once you hit 1.0 it just stays. Could add a "MAX" pulse to communicate.
- Jetpacks don't respawn within a single run by design — they're scarce, you get one shot per launch. Restart regenerates them.
- The T-Rex sprite faces "up" by default; rotation logic adds π/2 so when velocity is rightward (yaw=0) the sprite rotates correctly. Slightly counterintuitive but consistent.
- `roundRect` polyfill isn't in this file — used a fallback to plain `rect` for browsers that lack it. Modern browsers (2022+) all support it.
- Submission only fires when `state.myName` is set. If a user plays without the intro, they don't get on the leaderboard. The intro is mandatory so this is fine.
- The Sun is positioned to the LEFT of Earth (x=-150) and the launch defaults to upward/rightward — players who fling backward into the sun get a brutal lesson, by design.
- Daily leaderboard rolls over at UTC midnight (`new Date().toISOString().slice(0,10)`). Players in different timezones may see "yesterday" still active.
