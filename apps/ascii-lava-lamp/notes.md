# ascii-lava-lamp

## log
- 2026-05-26: Initial build. Pure-ASCII lava lamp where every cell of the canvas grid is rendered as a monospace glyph chosen by a 2D metaball field. Egg-shaped bulb profile (asymmetric — widest ~62% down for that classic teardrop), narrow neck above, silver cap on top, ▒▓-shaded base below. **Metaball physics**: 3-10 blobs with `{x, y, vx, vy, r, temp}`. Buoyancy drives the cycle: temp rises strongly when blob is below 78% of bulb height, cools when above 18%, gentle ambient cooling elsewhere — produces the real-lamp convection (heat at base → rise → cool at top → sink). Soft attraction between nearby blobs (cohesion `0.6/d²`) adds the merging-globule look. Soft-wall reflection at bulb edge (45% velocity damping on contact). **Field-to-char mapping**: cells inside bulb compute `Σ r²/(d²+0.4)` summed across blobs, with Y axis pre-squashed by 1.7× so blobs look circular despite chars being taller than wide. Field > 0.55 → pick char from ramp `·:+*ox#%@` by intensity, with HIGH-CONTRAST color: 95% saturation, lightness ramping 28% (edge) → 84% (core), tight hue band ±9° around `currentHue`, faint warm-yellow rotation at the core. Cells below threshold render as `·` in `#1c0e0c` for "dark glass fluid" backdrop. **Slow color drift**: `hue = palette.center + sin(elapsed/cycleSec * 2π) * palette.spread` — gives a sine-wave drift within each palette's range, default 120s cycle (configurable 20-360s). 6 palettes: magma (red→orange), ember (amber→yellow), toxic (chartreuse→green), cyber (magenta→violet), ocean (cyan→blue), aurora (wide teal→magenta sweep). **Interaction**: tap/click on the canvas finds the nearest blob, slams temp to 1, vy to -6, and bumps radius — local heat surge. Shake button kicks every blob with ±18 random impulse and rerolls temp. Defensive init: matchMedia + canvas wrapped in try/catch, global error handler renders a magenta toast at the top so silent failures become visible. JSDOM smoke test: 0 errors, hue drifts 016°→018° in 800ms, palette + shake both fire cleanly. **Aesthetic**: Monoton title in lava-gold + ember-red glow, Major Mono Display subtag, JetBrains Mono bold for the glyphs. WCAG basics: rem units throughout, semantic main/h1/button/label, aria-pressed on palette buttons, aria-label on canvas describing the visual + interaction, aria-live on hue readout, focus-visible 3px ink outlines, 2.75rem min interactive targets, prefers-reduced-motion slows dt by 4×.

## issues
- Metaball squash factor (1.7×) is tuned for a JetBrains Mono cell ratio of ~0.58 — if a user's installed monospace has a wildly different ratio, blobs may look elongated. Acceptable.
- High blob count (10) + small CHAR_H = ~3000 cells × 10 blobs = 30k field ops per frame. Fine on desktop, OK on phones; degrades smoothly via rAF backpressure.
