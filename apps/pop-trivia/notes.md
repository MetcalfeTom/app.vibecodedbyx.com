# 2000s Pop Trivia

How well do you know 2000s pop music?

## log
- 2026-04-10: Initial build. 49 questions about 2000s pop music across 9 categories (Artists, Albums, Songs, Videos, Pop Culture, Awards, Records, Collabs, Behind the Scenes, Soundtracks, Viral). 4-choice multiple choice, shuffled options. 3 modes: Normal (15 questions, 15s timer), Speed (15 questions, 8s timer), Endless (all 49 questions). Score = 100 base + time bonus (×3) + streak bonus (up to 5×5). Keyboard shortcuts (1-4 or A-D). Grade system: Pop Royalty (90%+), Chart Topper (75%+), Radio Regular (60%+), Casual Listener (40%+), Dial Turner. Progress pips. Righteous + Lexend Deca typography, dark purple/neon gradient aesthetic with hot pink, cyan, gold accents.

## features
- 49 trivia questions across 9 categories
- 3 game modes (Normal, Speed, Endless)
- Timed questions with countdown
- Streak bonus scoring
- Keyboard shortcuts (1-4 / A-D)
- Grade/rank system
- Shuffled question and answer order
- WebAudio SFX
- Mobile-friendly
