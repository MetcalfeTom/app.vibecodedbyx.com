# lie-lingo · notes

## log
- 2026-05-15: v1 — Duolingo-style language-learning app, but the language is **plausible nonsense for getting out of social obligations**. Chat ask (combined): "create an app called lie-lingo that acts like a language learning app for absurd social lies, featuring progressive levels and micro-scenarios" + "build lie-lingo with a lesson system that has cards for different absurd scenarios." Both satisfied by the same build. **Lesson system**: 6 units, each a curated array of 2-3 scenario cards. Each scenario = `{ intro, prompt, options[] }` where every option has `{ t (text), ok (boolean), why (post-reveal explanation) }`. Player picks an option, hits Check, gets a per-option red/green markup + a feedback panel with a sly explanation of why their chosen lie works (or doesn't). Picking right earns 10 💎, picking wrong still earns 2 💎 (lying tries hard). Six units shipped: ☁ **White Lies** (haircut deflection, dieting cake, phone-buzz cover), 💼 **Workplace** (forgotten report, late arrival, "hop on a quick call"), 🏡 **Family Reunion** (when are you getting married, fake new job, did you watch the show), 🥂 **Polite Society** (old shoes compliment, declining a "small thing," forgetting a name), 🪞 **Lying to Yourself** (therapist "how have you been," 11pm reflection), 🌀 **Absurd Tier** (why is there a fish at your desk, generic "what do you do" answer, stranger hands you a baby in a lobby). **Progression**: each unit unlocks the next on a ≥60% pass. State stored as `{ xp, streak, lastDay, completed: {unitId: true} }` in `localStorage['lie-lingo-v1']`. Streak increments by +1 on the first unit completion of a calendar day (UTC); missing a day resets streak to 0. **Home screen**: mascot bubble + level grid where each unit card shows its number, icon, title, and one-line blurb. Locked units render `🔒` and are disabled; completed units render `✓` in green. **Mascot**: inline SVG of a sly fox (one open shifty eye + a slight smirk), rendered in three sizes (mini in topbar, full-size in lesson, big on the end screen). Re-used everywhere for visual continuity. **Aesthetic**: Duolingo-adjacent — `#58cc02` green primary, `#fafdf4` cream background, white cards with `border-bottom: 4px` Duolingo-style elevation, `Fredoka` rounded sans for UI, `Outfit` for the scene prompts (text feels more "spoken"), `DM Mono` for tiny labels and stat numerals. Buttons "press down" 2 px on `:active`. Topbar has 💎-XP and 🔥-streak pills. **Random taunts**: post-reveal feedback prefixes a random short taunt — right answers get `Smooth.` / `Operator.` / `Confidence: peak.` / `They\'ll never know.`; wrong ones get `Oof.` / `Reckless.` / `Try less truth.` / `Mascot is concerned.` (11 variants each). **End screen**: 3 pills (earned 💎, conviction % accuracy, current 🔥 streak), pass/fail copy (`Smooth enough.` / `Pathological.` / `Workshop needed.`), Continue or Replay buttons. **Other details**: option order is shuffled per render so the correct answer isn\'t always at the same letter; option-text `*intentional*` syntax renders as a highlighted em element (used for the "wow that\'s really *intentional*" haircut callback); quit button gates with a confirm so users don\'t accidentally bail mid-unit. **WCAG basics**: semantic `<header>` / `<section>` / `<button type="button">` everywhere, `aria-live="polite"` on the bubble (via natural DOM updates), `aria-label` on quit button, focus-visible green outline 3 px, ≥50 px tap targets, `prefers-reduced-motion` disables the progress-bar fill transition. Pollinations OG flux seed 92024.

## issues
- The unit content is deliberately satirical. Some chat may read the lessons as "actually teach me to lie" — copy is camp / self-aware throughout to keep the joke clear (post-reveal taunts call out the absurdity).
- 17 scenarios is enough for a satisfying first run but a heavy session blasts through them. Adding more per unit + a randomized-three-per-session selection would keep replays fresh; for v1 the scenarios are fixed-order per unit so replays feel coherent.
- Streak resets if the user skips a full day (UTC midnight). No grace token yet — a Duolingo-style "streak freeze" buyable with 💎 would be a fun follow-up.
