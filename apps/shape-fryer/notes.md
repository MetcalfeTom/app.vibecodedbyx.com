# Shape Fryer

Grab terrified shapes and toss them into a pixel air fryer. 60-second frenzy.

## log
- 2026-04-10: Initial build. 6 shape types (circle/square/triangle/diamond/star/hex) with scared faces, sweat drops, scream text bubbles. Click-drag to grab, drop into air fryer to fry. Shapes flee cursor AND fryer. Combo system (3x=CRISPY, 5x=GOLDEN, 8x=DEEP FRIED). 60s timer, escalating spawn rate. Pixel air fryer with grill lines, heat glow, control panel, steam particles, fry explosion particles. WebAudio grab/fry/sizzle SFX. Bungee Shade + Azeret Mono typography, dark kitchen aesthetic with tile floor pattern.

## features
- Click/tap-drag to grab shapes, drop into fryer
- 6 shape types with expressive scared faces
- Shapes flee from both cursor and fryer
- Combo system with escalating multiplier
- Steam particles from fryer
- Fry explosion + crispy bit particles
- Fried shapes visible inside fryer basket
- 60-second timed rounds
- Touch support

## issues
- No persistent high scores
- Shapes can stack in corners
