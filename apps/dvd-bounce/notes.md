# DVD Bounce

## log
- 2026-04-06: Initial build — classic bouncing DVD logo screensaver. Color changes on every wall hit, confetti explosion + screen flash on corner hits. Ghost trail effect, wall counter, corner counter. Click to nudge. Lexend Mega + Space Mono typography, black void with neon glow.

## features
- Bouncing DVD VIDEO logo with rounded rect, disc icon, glow
- 15 neon colors, cycles on every wall bounce
- Corner hit detection with confetti explosion (80 particles)
- Screen flash on corner hit
- Ghost trail (12 frames) with fading opacity
- Wall hit counter and corner hit counter in HUD
- Click anywhere to nudge the logo's velocity
- Speed clamped between 1-6 to stay watchable
- Hidden cursor for screensaver feel
- Trail rendering via alpha fade background

## issues
- Logo size is fixed 160x80, could scale with screen
- No fullscreen toggle
- Click nudge can make it too fast or too slow near clamp edges
