GroceryRank

Fifty everyday healthy foods, one sortable table. Rank by cheapest per serving, by nutrient density (protein + fiber + micronutrients per calorie), or by the sweet spot — nutrition per dollar.

Prices are rough U.S. estimates per serving (2026); nutrition values are approximate. Top 5 of the active ranking get medals.
How the scores work · nutrient density = (2×protein g + 2.5×fiber g + 1.5×micro score) ÷ (calories/100) — nutrients per calorie, so watery greens shine · value = density ÷ price — nutrients per dollar, where lentils and oats go to war · micro score is a hand-set 0–10 for vitamin/mineral richness · this is a budgeting toy, not medical advice. eat plants, drink water.
