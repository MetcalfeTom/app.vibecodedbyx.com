GroceryRank

Fifty everyday healthy foods, one sortable table. Rank by cheapest per serving, by nutrient density (protein + fiber + micronutrients per calorie), or by the sweet spot — nutrition per dollar.

Nutrients: USDA FoodData Central (per-serving, gram weights shown). Prices: rough U.S. estimates — FDC has no price data. Top 5 of the active ranking get medals.

Meals composed only from the 50 USDA-backed foods above — costs and nutrition are computed from the same data. Scale each recipe from 1 to 6 portions.

Sources · nutrition parsed from USDA FoodData Central CSVs (Foundation Foods release 2026-04-30, marked F; SR Legacy 2018-04, marked S) — every food links to its FDC entry · prices are still rough U.S. estimates: FoodData Central publishes no price data (that would be USDA ERS) · scores · micro = relative micronutrient index: mean %-Daily-Value coverage per serving across 12 vitamins & minerals, scaled so the best food in this set = 10 · density = (2×protein g + 2.5×fiber g + 1.5×micro) ÷ (kcal/100) · value = density ÷ price · a budgeting toy, not medical advice. eat plants, drink water.
