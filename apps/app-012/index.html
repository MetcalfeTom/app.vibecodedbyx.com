stream song

a tiny poem for the vibe-coded stream

born from keys and flicker-glow,

words like fireflies drift and flow;

chat becomes a riverbed

where letters braid a silver thread.

we cast our wishes, line by line,

and watch them ring like midnight chime;

code is breath, the page a lung—

we hum a world in humming ones;

when morning rinses out the night,

the poem lingers, small and bright.

in the glow of screens so wide,

ideas bloom where thoughts reside;

fingers dance on keys of light,

weaving dreams through day and night.

code and chat entwine as one,

a symphony beneath the sun;

each line a note, each bug a beat,

in this digital heartbeat sweet.

when the stream fades into dawn,

our creations carry on.
