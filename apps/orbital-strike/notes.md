# Orbital Strike - Neon Space Shooter

## log
- 2026-01-09: Initial creation
  - Epic neon space shooter
  - 4 enemy types with different patterns
  - 3 boss types with increasing difficulty
  - 5 powerup types
  - Wave-based progression
  - Bomb system for emergencies
  - Mobile touch controls

## features
- Wave-based enemy spawning
- 4 enemy types: Scout, Fighter, Bomber, Elite
- Boss fights every 5 waves
- 5 powerups: Shield, Rapid Fire, Spread Shot, Bomb, Extra Life
- Particle effects on everything
- Scrolling starfield
- Score system
- Lives system
- Mobile d-pad and fire button

## enemies
- Scout: Fast, 1 HP, straight path
- Fighter: Medium, 2 HP, zigzag pattern
- Bomber: Slow, 3 HP, shoots back
- Elite: 5 HP, sine wave pattern

## bosses
- DESTROYER (wave 5): 50+ HP, spread shot
- OVERLORD (wave 10): 80+ HP, more bullets
- TITAN (wave 15): 120+ HP, spiral patterns

## powerups
- Shield (cyan): 8 seconds invincibility
- Rapid Fire (magenta): 3x fire rate for 10s
- Spread Shot (green): 3-way bullets for 8s
- Bomb (orange): Screen clear, +1 bomb stock
- Life (red): +1 life (max 5)

## controls
- WASD/Arrows: Move ship
- Space: Fire (hold for auto-fire)
- B: Use bomb
- Mobile: D-pad + fire/bomb buttons

## design
- Dark space background (#0a0a12)
- Cyan player ship with orange engine
- Magenta cockpit glow
- Color-coded enemies and powerups
- Orbitron font for sci-fi feel
- Boss health bar during fights
- Wave counter
