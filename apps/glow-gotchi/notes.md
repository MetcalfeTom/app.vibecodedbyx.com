# glow-gotchi

## log
- 2026-05-26: Initial build. Tamagotchi-flavor neon blob with **real squishy bezier-perimeter physics** rather than a static sprite. Body is a 16-point spring perimeter — each point has `{angle, offset, vel}` with spring-back toward 0 (k=0.06) + 0.84 damping. Drawn as a smooth closed path via midpoint quadratic curves through the 16 control points. Rendering layers per frame: 4-step lighter-blend halo shells (5 radius scales) → soft purple shadow ellipse below → radial-gradient body (3 hue stops, mood-shifted live) → clipped inner highlight (large + small specular) → outlined hue-glow stroke with shadow blur. **Mood-driven color**: composite mood = `0.32·hunger + 0.42·love + 0.26·energy` drives `targetHue` (sad→210 cool grey-blue, worried→320 magenta, contented→285 violet, happy→195 cyan-blue). Hue eases toward target via shortest-arc lerp at 0.4 rate. **Face**: two eyes with sclera + tracking pupils + catchlight; pupils smoothly follow cursor or drag position with max offset 4px. Blink scheduled every 1.8–5s with a 120ms shutter that paints over with body-hue color. Mouth swaps between smile (mood>0.6) / neutral (0.35–0.6) / frown (<0.35) / chomp (open + pink tongue during feed) / sleep-o (when sleeping). Cheeks fade in pink when mood>0.7. **Actions**: 🍓 Feed adds 0.32 hunger + spawns 3 falling food particles that get consumed when they reach the body (chomp twitch + sparkle burst on consumption). ✨ Pet adds 0.22 love + 14 sparkle particles + nudges all perimeter points outward + plays pet+squish blip. 💤 Nap puts pet to sleep for 4.5s (closed-eye arcs, slow breath, energy regen rate ×2 negative = recovers fast, periodic Z particles rise from the head). **Stat decay**: hunger -0.0042/s, love -0.0034/s, energy -0.0028/s (slows ×0.4 while sleeping, energy regens). **Persistence**: localStorage `glow-gotchi-v1` saves {hunger, love, energy, age, born, last}; on load, computes elapsed time (capped 24h) and applies decay so the pet visibly missed you. **Drag interaction**: pointer-down inside body sets dragX/Y; while dragging, nearest perimeter points get pushed outward proportional to angular proximity to drag point (1.2 vel impulse on cells within π/3 of drag angle) — produces genuine squish-deformation under finger/cursor. Tap-on-blob auto-counts as a pet. **Speech bubble**: positioned dynamically above canvas, fade+spring-in (cubic-bezier 0.34,1.56,0.64,1) for the squeeze pop. Quip pools: action-specific (feedHigh/feedLow/petHigh/petLow/napHigh/napLow) chosen by stat-at-time-of-action, plus idle pools (idleHappy/idleMid/idleSad) chosen by current mood and surfaced every 7-13s. **Particles**: 3 types — sparkle (additive-blend cross+orb, decays with TTL), food (glowing radial fruit with shadowBlur), zzz (cyan text-rendered Z with shadow rising up). **Synthwave atmosphere**: title in Audiowide with cyan→pink→gold gradient text-fill + drop-shadow, Major Mono Display tagline, body background has 3 layered radial glows (violet center / pink bottom / cyan top) PLUS a perspective-rotated synthwave grid via `body::after` with mask-image fade. **Audio**: 3 distinct synth blips per action (feed = square-down chord + triangle ascent, pet = triangle 880→1320Hz + 1320→1660Hz, nap = long sine, wake = ascending triangle pair, squish = sine bend 140→440→220). **Defensive init**: matchMedia + canvas + handlers in try/catch, global window.error renders magenta toast. **WCAG**: rem units, semantic header/main + role=img with descriptive aria-label on canvas + role=group on stats and actions, aria-live on speech bubble, aria-pressed on mute, focus-visible 3px ink outlines, ≥2.75rem touch targets, prefers-reduced-motion kills the perspective grid + stat-bar transitions. **Keyboard**: F=feed, P or Space=pet, N=nap. JSDOM smoke test: zero errors, 620×500 canvas, all bars render, feed/pet/nap all push stats to ceiling correctly, speech becomes visible, localStorage persists.

## issues
- Drag-squish deforms perimeter pretty aggressively at low frame rates — `dt*60` scaling helps but tablet stutter could over-squish. Acceptable; the wobble is part of the charm.
- Cap on offline decay is 24h — pet won't fully starve if you leave for a month, but stats will still be near-zero on return.
