# existential-ants · notes

## log
- 2026-05-14: v1 — interactive ant-farm sim with philosophical thought bubbles. Chat request: "create an interactive ant-farm simulation called existential-ants where ants have random philosophical thought bubbles while they work." **Aesthetic** — vintage field-study journal: cream-parchment cards, sepia/soil palette `#5b3a1d` / `#8a5a31` / `#cba37a`, dashed dividers, hand-typeset Playfair Display title with `<em>Ants</em>` in ember `#c25a1f` italic, Cormorant italic tag, IBM Plex Mono caps for HUD/labels, classic "block-shadow + outset border" buttons that mimic letterpress. The whole thing reads like a clipping from Formica Inc. field notes (the corner stamp literally says "FORMICA INC. · field study #74"). **Farm rendering**: two canvases — `farm` for the dirt+ants composite, `fx` reserved for future particles. A third **offscreen** canvas `dirtCanvas` holds the baked background (sky strip with a soft sun radial gradient + green grass tufts at the surface, then 4-stop dirt gradient from `#8a5a31` down to `#2c1c0a` with 5 wavy strata lines + thousands of grain dots + 12 pebbles), rebuilt only on resize so the per-frame cost is one `drawImage`. **Tunnel network**: 5-6 procedurally placed `chambers` and 3 surface holes; edges connect surface→chamber and chamber→chamber via quadratic bezier curves with a random perpendicular midpoint offset. Each tunnel is stroked three times for depth (dark outline, mid fill, light sheen) and 50 sampled points per curve go into `pathPoints[]` for ant routing. Chambers are radial-gradient discs with a rim shadow. Surface holes are tiny dark ellipses. **Ants**: 28 spawn at boot (button adds more). Each ant has `{x, y, heading, speed, target, walkPhase, size, thoughtT, bubble, bubbleUntil}`. They pick a target — 65% a chamber, 25% a path-point near the current trail, 10% a crumb if one exists — and wobble toward it via a damped heading update (`heading += ±turn` with 0.6 rad/s random jitter), arriving and re-picking when within 6 px. Speed modulates lightly via `sin(walkPhase)` so they "scuttle" instead of glide. The body is canvas-drawn each frame: 6 wiggling legs (sinusoidal `phase + i*0.7`), abdomen + thorax + head black discs, 2 antennae, a faint highlight dot on the abdomen. All rotated by heading. **Thought bubbles**: each ant has `thoughtT` that ticks down at random intervals (8–22 s after the previous), and on zero `emitThought()` fires: picks from a curated 50-entry `THOUGHTS` array of short existential one-liners ("is the queen real?", "i carry a crumb the size of my own grief", "the trail is the truth, the truth is the trail"). The bubble is a DOM element appended to the farm wrapper, positioned absolutely above the ant, styled as a parchment-cream rounded rect with a `::before/::after` tail. CSS `bubblePop` keyframe scales+opacity-ins on appear; `bubbleFade` removes it after `3 s + length·22 ms`. Bubble follows the ant every frame and flips below if it'd clip the top of the farm. **Click an ant**: hit-test within 12 px → `emitThought(ant, longOverride=true)` picks from a 7-entry `LONG_THOUGHTS` array of paragraph-length confessions ("we do not, technically, have a religion. we do have a queen, who has never spoken…"). Long bubbles use an ember border + larger max-width + 6.5 s display. **Crumbs**: click anywhere in dirt to drop a parchment-yellow disc with a brown ring; nearby ants get their target reassigned to it. Crumbs decay over 7-11 s and are removed. The "drop a crumb" button drops one at a random ground position. **Journal sidebar** (bottom-left, glassy parchment card): live-updates with a reverse-chronological list of overheard thoughts, time-stamped `HH:MM:SS` in IBM Plex Mono caps; keeps the last 14. "Reset journal" button clears the list (ants keep philosophizing). **HUD strip** top-right: ant count, thoughts emitted, active crumbs — Playfair Display numerals on parchment. **Audio** (Web Audio synth, **muted by default** per polite-default): each thought emission fires a tiny triangle-wave chime at 740–1060 Hz with a 0.4 s exponential decay; mute toggle in the dock unmutes and beeps once for feedback. **WCAG basics**: canvas `role="application"` + descriptive aria-label, journal sidebar `aria-label`, audio toggle `aria-pressed`, ≥44 px button targets, `prefers-reduced-motion` disables the bubble pop/fade animation. Pollinations OG flux seed 27272.

## issues
- The bubbles are DOM elements, not canvas-drawn, so they live above the farm and don't get clipped by the offscreen ant scaling. At extreme zoom-out on very narrow viewports, two ants in close proximity can have overlapping bubbles — could be solved with a stacking-bubble queue but the current cadence (one bubble per ant, ~10 s apart) makes collisions rare.
- The `dirtCanvas` rebuild on every resize is O(W·H) due to the grain-dot fill. Fast enough on desktop (<50 ms) but noticeable on a slow phone. Could cache and rescale instead of rebuilding.
- Ants ignore the visible tunnel walls — they walk freely across the dirt. The tunnels are visual atmosphere only. Could constrain pathfinding to tunnel paths but that's a much bigger refactor and the freedom currently feels organic.
