# Pixel Pond

## log
- 2026-04-06: Initial build — pixel art fishing aquarium with 10 fish species across 5 rarity tiers, hook-and-reel mechanics, species collection journal. Added blue/yellow starfish stuck to glass edges, silly googly eyes on all fish. Press Start 2P + Silkscreen fonts, deep ocean palette, 320x240 game resolution scaled up.

## features
- 10 fish species: Minnow, Goldscale, Crimson Fin, Jade Perch, Violet Eel, Neon Tetra, Sunspot Koi, Phantom Ray, Abyssal Fang, Golden Leviathan
- 5 rarity tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary — weighted spawn rates
- Fishing mechanics: cast (click where to drop), wait for nibble, reel in when hooked (4s window)
- Fish have depth preferences — deeper hook = rarer catches
- Blue and yellow pixel starfish stuck to glass walls and bottom
- Silly googly eyes on all fish
- Species collection with catch counts
- "NEW SPECIES" notification on first catch
- Catch overlay showing name, rarity, description, value
- Animated water surface, seaweed, bubbles, rocks, moon, stars
- Sparkle particle burst on successful catch
- 320x240 pixel art resolution scaled to screen
- Click/tap/space to interact

## issues
- Hook depth is random — could let player control depth
- Fish can be caught even if not visually near hook
- No persistent storage for collection
