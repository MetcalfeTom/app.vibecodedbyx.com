# Zen Neon Garden

## log
- 2026-01-11: Created zen neon garden with glowing seed planting

## features
- Click anywhere to plant glowing seeds
- Seeds sprout and grow into geometric trees
- 5 tree types with unique visual styles:
  - Fractal: classic branching with soft green glow
  - Crystal: sharp geometric blue branches with diamond leaves
  - Spiral: golden spiraling branches with star-shaped leaves
  - Willow: pink drooping curved branches
  - Bonsai: compact red tree with clustered leaves
- Trees sway gently in the breeze
- Particle effects when planting seeds
- Twinkling stars background
- Ambient floating particles
- Clear garden button to start fresh
- Tree counter
- Calming zen aesthetic
- Mobile and desktop friendly
