# Who Is Gary?

An investigation-style mystery game where players uncover clues about a mysterious figure named "Gary."

- Noir-inspired design with Special Elite typewriter font
- Multiple possible revelations for replayability
- Heartwarming twist: Gary is just a genuinely good person
