# Who Is Gary?

A dark noir investigation game about finding the truth about a mysterious figure named Gary. Spoiler: Gary remains elusive.

## Log
- 2024-12-20: Initial creation - simple detective/case file aesthetic
- 2024-12-20: Major overhaul - much darker noir aesthetic with:
  - Film grain overlay, rain effect, vignette
  - 9 pieces of evidence with unlock dependencies
  - Hidden secrets in text (click highlighted words)
  - Encrypted transmission with cipher puzzle
  - 3 different endings (Dark, Truth, Void)
  - Timeline of Gary sightings
  - Photo wall with AI-generated noir images
  - Whisper easter egg (appears briefly, clickable)
  - Redacted text in intro (click to reveal)
  - Ctrl+G secret shortcut
  - Progress saved to localStorage

## Design Philosophy
- Gary should ALWAYS remain mysterious and elusive
- Clues are vague and contradictory by design
- No definitive answer about who Gary is
- Multiple interpretations are valid
- The mystery IS the point
