# Who Is Gary?

A deep, dark, obsessively detailed noir investigation ARG about the impossibly elusive figure known only as Gary.

## Log
- 2024-12-20: Initial creation
- 2024-12-20: First overhaul - darker noir aesthetic
- 2024-12-20: MASSIVE expansion:
  - 7 depth levels (Surface → Gary)
  - 24 evidence pieces across all depths with unlock trees
  - 7 tabs: Evidence, Timeline, Witnesses, Signals, Locations, Anomalies, Photos
  - 6 detailed witness testimonies
  - 19 timeline entries spanning prehistory to 2147
  - 4 numbers station transmissions (hex encoded, infrasound)
  - 6 locations with coordinates
  - 12 SCP-style anomaly entries
  - 22 clickable secrets hidden in text
  - Sanity meter that decreases as you dig deeper
  - 5 different endings
  - Konami code easter egg (↑↑↓↓←→←→BA)
  - Ctrl+G secret
  - Periodic whispers in corner
  - Clearance level system

## Design Philosophy
- Gary is ALWAYS elusive - never a definitive answer
- Layers upon layers of mystery
- References: SCP Foundation, numbers stations, Cicada 3301, tulpas, Wow! signal
- The deeper you go, the more unsettling it gets
- Player becomes part of the mystery at Depth 7
- Sanity mechanic adds stakes

## References & Inspirations

## Secrets Guide
- Click highlighted words in evidence text
- Konami code: ↑↑↓↓←→←→BA (jumps to Depth 7)
- Ctrl+G = secret
- Watch for whispers in bottom left
- Decoder passwords: gary, pattern, forget, void, loop, me
- Redacted text in intro is clickable
