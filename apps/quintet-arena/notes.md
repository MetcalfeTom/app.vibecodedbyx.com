# quintet-arena · notes

## log
- 2026-07-11: v1 (findlogin's ask: 2.5D fighting game, five distinct pixel heroes, THREE unique skills each, camera zoom on skill activation, no external assets, map included, single HTML page, stable engine). **Engine**: 640×400 world, walk band x[26,614] y[112,384]; entities have (x, y=depth); depth-sorted draw list each frame (obstacles, torches, enemies, minions, player by y); camera lerp-follow + view clamp; `cam.punch` zoom (Q/E→1.3, R→1.5, eases back at 5/s, reduced-motion caps at 1.04); hitstop on heavy casts; screen shake; dt clamped 0.04; pause on blur/P. **Sprites 100% in-code**: string-grid pixel art (12×16 heroes, enemies 3 sizes, skeleton/pillar/crate/torch) → offscreen canvases via `buildSprite`; walk frame = mirrored bottom 4 rows; flip via ctx.scale; slime squash via scaleY sin. Ground pre-rendered once (speckle + tufts + border stones). **5 heroes × (basic + Q/E/R)**: BLADE arc slash / Crescent Dash (dash-through, per-dash hit set) / Bladestorm (0.6s spin ticks) / Executioner (0.32s windup, 45 dmg, instakill <22% hp "EXECUTED"); EMBER fireball+splash / Meteor (0.6s telegraph, r46, 40) / Flame Wave (10 fire projs, slow-burn) / Inferno Ring (follow annulus zone 5s); THORN fast arrow / Volley (8-fan) / Snare Trap (20s arm, roots 2s, one-shot) / Arrow Rain (zone 2.5s + arrow particles); VOLT chain-zap ≤120 range (dud click if none) / Storm Nova (expanding stun ring, annulus hit ±10) / Blink (move-dir or aim, 85u, arrival burst) / Overcharge (5s: atk 2× speed + chains to 2 extra at 60%); GRAVE piercing bone / Raise Dead (2 skeletons 9s, melee 9) / Soul Drain (3 nearest ≤140: 16 dmg, +8 heal each, tethers) / Reaper Field (r46 slow+dps zone 5s). **Systems**: zones {fill|ring|trap|rain} with per-enemy 0.4s tick maps, novas, meteors, bolts (jagged 4-seg), projs w/ pierce/splash/burn/per-enemy hit map, knockback decay `pow(.02,dt)`, stun/slow timers, enemy separation O(n²), obstacle ellipse push-out, enemies target nearest of player/minions and chew minions. Waves: 3+2w, imps from w2, brutes from w4, hp ×(1+0.12(w-1)), edge spawns, 2.2s interlude. **Verified in node** (no WebGL needed — pure 2D): syntax, all DOM ids, and full sim: 5 heroes × (40 attacks + Q+E+R) vs 6 spawned enemies + 30s soak to wave 4 — zero exceptions, all kits scored damage. Auto-aim design keeps mouse & touch parity (joystick + ATK/Q/E/R buttons). Jersey 10 + Silkscreen + Space Mono. WCAG: rem, semantic, dialogs aria-modal, canvas role=application, aria-live status, focus-visible, ≥44px targets, reduced-motion kills punch/shake/hitstop.
