Quick Help

Opening Principles

Control the center, develop your pieces, and castle early. Don't move the same piece twice unless necessary.

Tactical Thinking

Before moving, check for checks, captures, and threats (CCT). Look for forks, pins, and skewers.

Endgame Essentials

Activate your king in the endgame. Passed pawns are gold - push them forward while keeping them protected.

Chess Mindset

Every loss is a lesson. Stay calm, take your time - even grandmasters make mistakes.
