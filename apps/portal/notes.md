# Dimensional Portal

## Log
- 2024-12-16: Created interdimensional portal experience

## Features
- Swirling vortex portal animation with Canvas
- 8 different dimensions to travel through
- Mouse interaction affects portal distortion
- Particle effects spiraling into the void
- Warp transition effect when traveling
- Dimension info display (name, description)
- Coordinate readout with stability meter
- Optional ambient sound + warp sound effects
- Space/click to travel
- Responsive design

## Dimensions
1. The Void Between - purple/pink
2. Crystalline Expanse - cyan/teal
3. The Burning Cosmos - orange/red
4. Ethereal Gardens - green
5. The Mirror Dimension - magenta/pink
6. Quantum Foam - indigo/blue
7. The Dreaming Dark - grayscale
8. Solar Winds - yellow/gold
