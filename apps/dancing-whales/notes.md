# Dancing Whales

## log
- 2026-01-11: Created neon dancing whales with ocean beats

## features
- 5 colorful neon whales swimming and dancing
- Rhythmic movement synced to 80 BPM ocean beats
- Deep bass drum with watery texture sounds
- Melody plays every 4th beat
- Click/tap makes all whales jump
- Bubble particle effects when whales dance
- Ocean light rays in background
- Rising ocean particles
- Beat progress indicator bar
- Whale tail and flipper animation
- Whale eye details and belly stripes
- Mobile and desktop friendly
