# Void Fishing

## log
- 2026-01-15: Initial creation
  - Cast line into sea of static to catch digital ghosts
  - TV static background effect
  - 10 ghost types with rarity tiers (common to legendary)
  - Depth-based ghost spawning
  - Ambient ghosts floating in the void
  - Fishing mechanics: cast, wait, bite, reel
  - Ghost collection display
  - Glitchy VT323 + Creepster fonts
  - Sound effects for cast, bite, catch
  - Depth meter shows how deep your line is

## features
- Animated static background
- Wavy void surface with glow
- Curved fishing line with bobber
- 10 ghost types: Common Spirit, Skull Specter, Void Watcher, Bone Wraith, Data Vortex, Shadow Entity, Orb of Forgotten, All-Seeing Glitch, Void Itself, Static Lord
- Rarity tiers: common, uncommon, rare, epic, legendary
- Depth affects which ghosts you can catch
- 3-second window to reel after bite
- Collection panel shows unique catches
- Ambient ghosts swim in the void
- Glitch effects on ghosts

## controls
- Click to cast line (when ready)
- Wait for "SOMETHING BITES!" message
- Click to reel in (must be quick!)
- Deeper casts = rarer ghosts
