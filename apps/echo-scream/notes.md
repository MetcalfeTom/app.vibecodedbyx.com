# Echo Scream

Scream into your mic to grow a pixel monster. Louder = faster growth. Watch it evolve with new eyes, horns, wings, and more.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. WebAudio mic input with frequency analysis for volume detection. Monster grows from size 1 to 20 based on cumulative scream energy (louder = quadratic growth). 11 evolution milestones unlock traits: horns (size 2), spikes (3), extra eyes (5), tail (6), more limbs (8), fangs (10), wings (12), 6 eyes (14), tongues (16), crown of horns (18), final form (20). Pixel-art monster with procedural body (oval scaling), animated limbs with claws, flapping wings, writhing tongues, wiggling tails, breathing cycle, mouth tracks volume, pupils dilate when screaming. Scream aura rings, pulsing vignette, floating particles. Volume bar, trait badges, timer. Creepster + DM Mono typography, dark flesh horror aesthetic.

## features
- Mic input with real-time volume analysis
- Monster size 1-20 with quadratic growth from volume
- 11 evolution milestones (horns, spikes, eyes, tails, limbs, teeth, wings, tongues)
- Procedural pixel-art monster body
- Animated limbs with claws
- Flapping wings
- Writhing tongues
- Mouth opens with volume
- Pupils dilate when screaming
- Scream aura rings
- Breathing animation
- Volume bar
- Trait badges
- Pulsing vignette tied to volume
