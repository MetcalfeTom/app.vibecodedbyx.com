# Train Signals

## log
- 2026-01-19: Initial creation - train signaling game with difficulty modes and colorful locomotives

## features
- Three difficulty modes (Easy, Medium, Hard)
- Easy: Slower trains, longer spawn intervals, max 2 trains
- Medium: Normal speed, moderate spawning, max 3 trains
- Hard: Fast trains, frequent spawning, max 4 trains
- Colorful locomotives (red, blue, green, yellow, purple, orange)
- Track switching by clicking on switch points
- Signal controls to stop/start trains
- Color-coded stations - match train colors
- Lives system (start with 3)
- Score tracking for successful deliveries

## design
- Dark railway aesthetic with brown/amber colors
- Glowing signal lights (red/green)
- Train cars with locomotive and carriages
- Track grid visualization
- Orbitron + Roboto Mono fonts

## controls
- Click switches: Toggle track direction
- Click signals: Toggle stop/go
- Difficulty buttons: Change game mode

## gameplay
- Trains spawn from left side
- Guide trains to matching colored stations on right
- Use switches to change track paths
- Use signals to hold trains and prevent collisions
- Lose a life for collisions or wrong station delivery
- Game over at 0 lives
