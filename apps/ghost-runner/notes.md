# Ghost Runner

## log
- 2026-01-03: Initial creation - neon endless runner with digital ghosts

## features
- Neon endless runner gameplay
- 3 ghost types: ground, air, and low
- Jump and slide mechanics
- Increasing difficulty over time
- High score saved to localStorage
- Neon grid scrolling background
- Particle effects on jump/land/death
- Player trail effect
- Wobbly ghost animations
- Touch and keyboard controls

## design
- Dark cyberpunk aesthetic
- Press Start 2P retro font
- Cyan player with neon glow
- Magenta/yellow/red ghosts
- Scanline grid background
- Glowing ground line

## controls
- SPACE / UP / TAP: Jump
- DOWN / SWIPE DOWN: Slide
- Click canvas: Jump

## ghost types
- Ground (magenta): Standard ghost on ground, jump over
- Air (yellow): Floating ghost, slide under
- Low (red): Low ghost, jump over

## technical
- Canvas-based rendering
- RequestAnimationFrame loop
- Hitbox collision detection
- Responsive canvas sizing
- localStorage for high scores
