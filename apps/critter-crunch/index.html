HOW IT EATS

🌱 Seed — 1 coin. +2 per adjacent ☀️ Sun.

🐛 Worm — 2 coins. +5 per adjacent 🌱 Seed (eats them).

🐦 Bird — 3 coins. +8 per adjacent 🐛 Worm (eats them).

☀️ Sun — 3 coins. Feeds every Seed it touches.

🪨 Rock — 1 coin. Filler. Nothing eats rocks.

🦋 Butterfly — 2 coins. Pollinates adj Seeds ×3, Flowers/Trees ×2.

Symbols eaten this spin still pay out — but are removed from your bag forever. Adjacency counts all 8 directions.
