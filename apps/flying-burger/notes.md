# Flying Burger

## log
- 2026-04-02: Initial build. Neon burger simulator — catch falling toppings with a giant bun. Canvas-based, single-file HTML. Lilita One + Fredoka typography. Dark neon aesthetic with orange/green glow.

## features
- 10 topping types drawn with canvas shapes (lettuce leaf, tomato circle, cheese square, patty oval, onion rings, pickles, bacon strip, ketchup/mustard blobs, egg)
- 2 bad items: hot pepper, rotten fish — lose a life on catch
- Toppings stack visually on the bun, building a tower
- Neon glow on everything, particle sparkles on catch
- Bun floats with hover animation and leaves a neon trail
- Combo system with score multiplier every 5 combo
- Speed increases over time, spawn rate ramps up
- Game over on 3 bad items caught OR 10 toppings missed
- Final burger tower shown on game over screen
- HUD: score, combo, lives (hearts), burger layer count
- Touch + mouse controls, mobile friendly
- Start screen with demo burger, game over with replay
