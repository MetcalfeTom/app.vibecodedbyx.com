# Magnet Playground

Drag magnets around and watch glowing balls bounce and orbit.

## log
- 2026-04-13: Initial build. Neon physics sandbox with draggable magnets and bouncy balls. 8 neon ball colors with trails and glow, attract/repel magnets with pulsing field rings, force lines between magnets and nearby balls. Ball-ball elastic collisions. Gravity toggle, wall bouncing. Click to spawn balls, right-click for magnets, scroll on magnet to adjust strength, double-click to flip attract/repel. Touch support. Orbitron + Fira Code typography, deep purple/neon aesthetic with grid background.

## features
- Drag magnets to attract/repel bouncy balls
- 8 neon ball colors with trail and glow effects
- Attract (purple) and repel (green) magnet modes
- Pulsing field rings and curved force lines
- Ball-ball elastic collisions
- Adjustable magnet strength (scroll wheel, -3x to 3x)
- Double-click magnet to toggle attract/repel
- Gravity toggle
- Wall bouncing with energy loss
- Click to add balls, right-click for magnets
- Touch support for mobile
- Grid background
