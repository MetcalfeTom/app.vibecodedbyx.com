# B-Movie VHS Mayhem

Click a VHS tape to generate absurd B-movie titles from combined shocking fragments.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. Clickable VHS tape with label, reels, and viewing window. 8 title patterns combining 35 adjectives, 30 nouns, 15 verbs, and 22 locations for thousands of combinations. 20 taglines, 7 ratings (including BANNED), years 1957-1996. VHS static flash on click, tape wiggle animation. VCR click + tape noise SFX. Collection shelf (last 30 shown, 100 stored in localStorage). Rating badge on tape. CRT scanlines overlay. Creepster + VT323 typography, horror orange/red on black VHS aesthetic.

## features
- Clickable VHS tape with label, dual reels, viewing window
- 8 procedural title patterns for huge variety
- 35 adjectives, 30 creatures/nouns, 15 verbs, 22 locations
- 20 absurd taglines
- 7 ratings including BANNED and UNRATED
- Year range 1957-1996 for authentic B-movie feel
- VHS static flash effect on click
- Tape wiggle animation
- VCR click + tape hiss SFX (WebAudio)
- Collection shelf with hover details
- Persistent collection in localStorage (up to 100)
- CRT scanlines overlay
- Rating badge on tape corner
- Responsive layout
