# ASCII Aquarium

## log
- 2026-01-13: Created ASCII aquarium with swimming terminal fish

## features
- Multiple ASCII fish designs: ><>, ><))°>, etc.
- Fish swim left/right and turn at edges
- Vertical floating movement
- Rising ASCII bubbles (o, °)
- Swaying seaweed animation
- Water surface waves (~)
- Sandy floor texture
- CRT scanline effect
- Retro green terminal aesthetic
- Multiple fish colors (green, cyan, yellow, magenta, orange)
- Click to spawn fish at location
- Keyboard shortcuts: F=fish, P=pause, C=clear
- Fish and bubble counters
- Real-time clock in header
- VT323 retro terminal font
- Max 30 fish limit
