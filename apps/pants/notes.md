# Pants

A majestic pair of high-waisted denim jeans dancing across the screen.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Added stretchy matching shirt and leather belt with buckle. Shirt: V-neck with torso, arms that swing per dance move, sleeve hems, stretch lines that appear during stretchy moves, wrinkle details at waist tuck. Color-matched to denim (6 shirt colors). Belt: brown leather strap with grain texture, edge stitching, metal buckle with frame/inner/shine highlight/prong/belt hole.
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. Canvas-rendered high-waisted denim jeans with detailed construction: waistband, belt loops, button, fly, curved pockets, outseam/inseam stitching (dashed), knee whiskers, cuff hems, denim weave texture lines. 8 dance moves (walk, strut, moonwalk, twist, jump, kick, splits, disco) with unique leg/waist/rotation animations per move. 6 denim colorways (classic indigo, dark wash, stonewash, charcoal, bright indigo, black). Gold/white contrast stitching per colorway. Sparkle burst on move transitions. Shimmer trail behind each pair. Click to summon more pants. Disco floor background with colored tiles and roaming spotlight. Starts with 5 pairs, unlimited spawning. Screen-wrap movement. Playfair Display + Zilla Slab typography, deep purple ambient aesthetic.
