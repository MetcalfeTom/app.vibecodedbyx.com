# Neon Coffee Machine

Watch a glowing coffee machine drip brew in neon. Pick your roast, hit brew, feel the pulse.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. Canvas-drawn coffee machine with water tank, display screen with progress bar, brew group/nozzle, cup with handle, drip tray. 4 roasts (light/medium/dark/espresso) with different colors, drip counts, speeds, steam. Teardrop drip physics with splash particles on cup surface. Steam particles with sine drift. Neon orange trim pulses with each drip cycle. Cyan water tank with depleting level. WebAudio drip sounds (sine chirp) and brew start (sawtooth rumble). Orbitron + Share Tech Mono typography, dark with orange/cyan neon.

## features
- Canvas-drawn coffee machine (body, tank, display, nozzle, cup, tray)
- 4 roast selections with distinct colors/speeds
- Drip physics with teardrop shape and splash
- Steam particles from cup
- Water tank depletes during brew
- Display screen shows progress
- Neon orange glow pulses during brewing
- WebAudio drip + brew sounds
- Drip counter
- Responsive sizing
- Mobile touch support
