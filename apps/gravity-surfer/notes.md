# Black Hole Gravity Surfer

## log
- 2026-01-08: Initial creation - high-speed gravity surfing game
  - Orbit around a black hole at increasing speeds
  - Dodge neon shadow enemies that spiral inward
  - Boost ability to escape tight situations
  - Score multiplier for close calls
  - Particle trail effects
  - Accretion disk visual

## features
- Surf the event horizon of a black hole
- Speed increases over time (WARP factor)
- 3 types of neon shadow enemies (spiky, diamond, circle)
- Shadows spiral inward toward the black hole
- Boost ability with cooldown
- Close-call multiplier system
- Particle trail following surfer
- Accretion disk visual effect
- Starfield parallax background
- Invincibility frames on start

## controls
- LEFT/RIGHT or A/D: Turn around the orbit
- SPACE or W: Boost outward (escape danger)
- Mobile: Touch drag left/right, double-tap to boost

## mechanics
- Gravity constantly pulls you toward the black hole
- Boosting pushes you outward temporarily
- Shadows spawn faster as speed increases
- Multiplier builds from near-misses
- Game ends on shadow collision

## design
- Neon cyberpunk aesthetic
- Cyan/magenta/yellow color scheme
- Orbitron font for sci-fi feel
- Glow effects everywhere
- Motion blur trail effect
- Pulsing black hole core

## technical
- Canvas 2D rendering
- Orbital physics simulation
- Particle system for trails and explosions
