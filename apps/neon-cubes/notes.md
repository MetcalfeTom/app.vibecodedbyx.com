# neon-cubes · notes

## log
- 2026-05-13: Shipped v1. Per chat: "high-speed physics sandbox where blocks emit trails and explode into light on impact." Canvas-based, DPR-aware, max 110 cubes (oldest auto-explodes when cap is reached). **Cube physics**: each cube = `{x, y, vx, vy, size, angle, va, hue}`. Per frame: `vy += gravity*1100*dt`, x-damping `0.998^(dt*60)`, angular damping `0.99^(dt*60)`. Wall collisions bounce with the bounce slider as restitution + add random angular kick + play wall-tick sound if impact velocity > 150. **The juicy part — cube-cube collisions**: simple circle-overlap test (radius = size*0.55) on all pairs each frame. On contact, both cubes are removed AND an explosion fires at the midpoint with intensity derived from relative velocity (`0.45 + min(1.6, relVel/350)`). So fast head-on impacts produce significantly bigger explosions than gentle nudges. **Trail effect**: secret sauce is the per-frame fade overlay — `ctx.fillRect(0, 0, W, H, rgba(4,5,10, trailAlpha))` instead of clearing. At default trailAlpha=0.20, anything drawn the previous frame fades 80% per frame, leaving a 5-6 frame motion smear that reads as a glowing light streak behind every moving cube. The Trails slider goes 0.05 (very long ghosts) to 0.5 (almost no trail) so you can dial it in. Cubes themselves render with `globalCompositeOperation = 'lighter'` (additive blend) so trails STACK BRIGHTLY when crossing instead of muddying. **Explosion effect** has four layers: (1) 12-34 hsl-jittered radial particles with mild gravity (120 u/s²) and 0.94/frame velocity damping, ranging 120-580 u/s initial speed, 0.6-1.15s life; (2) one shockwave ring expanding at 520 u/s with `shadowBlur` glow; (3) one counter-hue secondary ring expanding slower (360 u/s) for color contrast; (4) a 0.18s white-core radial-gradient flash at the impact point; plus screen shake `min(22, current + intensity*7)` decaying 0.85/frame, plus a deduped 28ms boom sound (bandpass-noise crack + 160→36Hz sub thump + 1200Hz square sparkle). Hue is the average of the two colliding cubes — so red + green cubes make an amber explosion. **Velocity streaks**: every cube drawn faster than 90 u/s gets a world-space velocity tail line drawn behind it, length scaled by speed (capped at 2× cube size), thickness = size*0.45, additive blend with shadowBlur halo — gives high-speed cubes a comet-like motion trail in ADDITION to the fade-overlay smear. **Controls** (4 sliders + 4 action buttons + sound toggle): Gravity (-0.5 to 2.4g), Trails (0.05-0.5 alpha), Bounce (0.2-0.98), Speed (0.3-3× spawn velocity multiplier). Buttons: ✦ Burst 12 (spawns 12 cubes radial from center), ⏵ Auto (toggles edge-spawn every 0.18-0.43s from a random side at 200-700 u/s inward), ⚡ Detonate (explodes every alive cube at its current position), ↻ Clear (wipe everything). **Input**: pointerdown starts a drag, drag-and-release flings a cube at velocity = `delta × 0.65 / dt`. Quick tap (no drag) spawns at a random angle at 220 u/s. Pointer events unify mouse + touch via setPointerCapture. **Keys**: Space burst, A auto, D detonate, C clear. **HUD**: 3 cells at top of stage (CUBES alive in cyan Rubik Mono / BOOMS total in magenta / FPS in lime), aria-live on count. **Aesthetic**: void bg with cyan/magenta radial glows + scanlines + vertical 80px column lines for that cyberpunk feel. Title in Audiowide with cyan→bone→magenta gradient text-clip + glow. Cormorant italic tagline "tap, drag, fling. *watch them shatter.*" Stage chassis: dark void with cyan border + cyan inner glow + black inset shadow. Sliders: cyan-glow thumbs, hot-magenta primary button, hot-red detonate button. **Accessibility**: rem-based sizing, role=application on canvas, aria-live on stats, ≥44px touch targets, focus-visible gold outline, prefers-reduced-motion kills transitions (not the gameplay since it IS the gameplay). Pollinations OG (seed 12345). Fonts: Audiowide + Rubik Mono One + Space Mono + Cormorant Garamond italic.

## issues
- Cube-cube collision is O(n²) per frame — fine up to 110 cubes (the cap) but if you crank trails low + spawn rate up + somehow exceed the cap, perf would degrade. Cap enforced via oldest-auto-explode when spawning over limit.
- Bounce restitution applies only to walls. Cube-cube is always destructive — they explode and remove both. By design (the request was "explode into light on impact") but some users might want a non-destructive collide mode (bounce off each other). TODO.
- Additive blending (`lighter`) means overlapping cubes saturate to white — at high density the screen can get washed out. Compressor on audio + fade overlay limits visual damage.
- Auto-spawn pulls cubes from edges into the arena. With high gravity + low bounce they pile up at the bottom and constantly explode against each other — fun chain-reaction mode.
- FPS counter updates twice per second; rapidly fluctuating frame times may show jittery values.
- The pointerdown spawn fires on every tap, including taps on the HUD overlay cells (they have pointer-events: none in CSS so taps pass through correctly).
