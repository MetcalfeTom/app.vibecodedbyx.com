# Neon Kaleidoscope

## log
- 2026-01-12: Created neon triangle kaleidoscope with rotating geometric layers
- 2026-01-12: Added microphone visualizer - triangles bounce and spin faster to volume

## features
- 8 layers of rotating triangles with different speeds
- 6 color schemes: Cyan-Magenta, Fire, Ocean, Neon Pink, Matrix, Rainbow
- Symmetry control (4, 6, 8, 12-fold)
- Speed multiplier control
- Pulse effect toggle
- Microphone visualizer:
  - Triangles scale/bounce to volume
  - Rotation speed increases with volume
  - Glow intensity boosts with volume
  - Outer layers react more than inner
- Center mandala with rotating star
- Background particles orbiting
- Mouse/touch affects layer rotation speeds
- Neon glow effects with shadow blur
