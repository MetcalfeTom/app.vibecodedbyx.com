# Quarantine Pinball

## log
- 2026-01-07: Initial creation - neon pinball with virus balls

## features
- Virus balls with spiky corona appearance
- Left/right flippers with physics
- Circular neon bumpers (100-150 points)
- Target row at top (200 points each, 1000 bonus for all)
- Ball launcher with charge mechanic
- 3 lives (virus balls)
- Particle effects on collisions
- Biohazard symbol watermark
- Containment breach drain zone

## controls
- A / Left Arrow: Left flipper
- D / Right Arrow: Right flipper
- Space: Hold to charge launcher, release to fire
- Mobile: Touch buttons for flippers, tap launcher area

## design
- Dark quarantine theme
- Cyan/magenta/green neon accents
- Russo One font for titles
- Share Tech Mono for data
- Virus balls with rotating spikes
- Glowing bumpers with flash on hit
- Grid background pattern

## technical
- Canvas 2D rendering
- Ball physics (gravity, velocity, friction)
- Flipper physics with rotation animation
- Point-to-line collision for flippers
- Circle collision for bumpers
- Particle system for hit effects
- Touch support for mobile
