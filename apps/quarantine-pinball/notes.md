## features
- Heavy iron cannonballs with realistic weight
- Oak plank flippers with iron pivot rivets
- Gunpowder barrels as bumpers (with fuses and skull symbols)
- Oak railing walls with iron bands
- Iron anchor obstacles
- Gold coin targets (250 points each, 2000 bonus for all)
- Cannon launcher with power charge
- 3 iron shots (lives)
- Explosion particles on barrel collisions
- Smoke trails behind fast cannonballs
- Ship hold wood plank floor
- "The Depths Below" drain warning

## controls
- A / Left Arrow: Port (left) flipper
- D / Right Arrow: Starboard (right) flipper
- Space: Hold to charge cannon, release to fire
- Mobile: Touch buttons for flippers, tap launcher area

## mechanics
- Heavier gravity for iron ball feel
- Oak railings bounce with wood dust particles
- Barrel hits create fire/smoke explosions
- Anchor hits deflect ball with spark effects
- Gold coins shimmer and glow

## design
- Dark ship hold background
- Wood plank floor with grain pattern
- Oak/brown/gold color palette
- Skull watermark in center
- Gunpowder barrels with iron bands and rivets
- Pirata One / IM Fell English SC fonts

## technical
- Canvas 2D rendering
- Heavy ball physics (higher gravity, weight multiplier)
- Flipper physics with rotation animation
- Point-to-line collision for flippers
- Circle collision for barrels and anchors
- Multi-type particle system (fire, smoke, dust, sparks)
- Smoke trail system for fast movement
- Touch support for mobile
