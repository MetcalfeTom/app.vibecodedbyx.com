# Pirate Parrot

## log
- 2026-01-07: Converted from Neon Fox Playground to pirate theme
  - Fox → Colorful parrot with animated wings
  - Yarn balls → Gold doubloons with skull emblems
  - Neon platforms → Floating barrels and wooden planks
  - Ship deck as ground platform
  - Ocean night background with moon and stars
  - Pirata One / IM Fell English SC fonts

## features
- Play as a ship's parrot collecting gold doubloons
- Flapping flight mechanics (not just jumping)
- Floating barrels and wooden planks as platforms
- Physics-based coins you can knock around
- Animated parrot with wing flapping and head bob
- Ocean waves, moon, and starry night sky
- Touch controls for mobile

## gameplay
- Arrow keys / WASD to fly
- Space to flap wings
- Click/tap coins to knock them around
- Collect doubloons for points
- Land on barrels and planks

## design
- Colorful scarlet macaw style parrot (red, blue, gold)
- Gold doubloons with skull symbols
- Wooden barrels with metal bands
- Ship deck planks as ground
- Ocean night atmosphere with glowing moon

## technical
- Canvas 2D rendering
- Flap-based flight (can flap while airborne)
- Platform collision detection
- Coin physics with bouncing
- Screen wrapping
- Touch and keyboard input
