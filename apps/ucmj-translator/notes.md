# UCMJ Translator

## log
- 2026-01-04: Initial creation - UCMJ translation tool with 6 languages

## features
- 12 key UCMJ articles with full translations
- 6 languages: English, Spanish, German, French, Korean, Arabic
- Search/filter functionality
- Expandable article cards
- Copy to clipboard for translations
- Professional military-themed UI
- Legal disclaimer included
- Mobile responsive

## articles included
- Article 2: Persons Subject to the UCMJ
- Article 15: Non-Judicial Punishment
- Article 31: Rights Warning (Self-Incrimination)
- Article 32: Preliminary Hearing
- Article 86: Absence Without Leave (AWOL)
- Article 89: Disrespect Toward Superior Officer
- Article 90: Willfully Disobeying Superior Officer
- Article 92: Failure to Obey Order or Regulation
- Article 107: False Official Statements
- Article 120: Rape and Sexual Assault
- Article 128: Assault
- Article 134: General Article
