# celery-smash · notes

## log
- 2026-05-12: Shipped v1. Per chat: "simple game where you click falling celery stalks before they reach the bottom of the screen." Canvas-based playfield with DPR-aware sizing (max 40rem tall, 72vh on most viewports). **Celery** = procedurally drawn cartoon stalks: trapezoid body with vertical linear gradient from `hsl(hue, 55%, 65%)` light top to `hsl(hue, 70%, 28%)` dark bottom (hue jittered 90–112 so each stalk is a slightly different green), 3 vertical grooves at -0.2/0/+0.2 width, white highlight stripe down the left, dark outline 1.5px. Leaves at top: 3–5 deterministic positions on a -π/2-centered arc, each leaf is a radial-gradient ellipse with a single vein stroke. Drop shadow ellipse projected to floor based on distance (alpha decays past 700px). Per-stalk RNG: 56–94px tall, 14–19px wide, 70–110 u/s base velocity (plus level scaling), ±0.4 initial spin, sine-wobble x-drift via `Math.sin(wob) * dt * wobAmp` (8–24 amp) for character. **Physics**: gravity 220→720 u/s² (×1.12 per level, capped), simple integration `vy += g*dt; y += vy*dt`. Stalks pinned within [16, W-16] so they don't slide off; if `y - h/2 > floorY` → splice + `loseLife()`. **Hit test**: rotated-rect — transform pointer into stalk local space via inverse `cos(-angle)/sin(-angle)` rotation, check `|lx| < w/2+14 && |ly| < h/2+12` for generous tap zone. Miss-click on empty space resets the combo immediately. **Smash**: 10–16 chunk particles (first 3 are leaves ~ ellipse, rest are squares) fired in radial directions with `90–310 u/s` velocity + upward `-80` impulse, gravity `480`, rotation, bounce off floor at 25% retention, 0.6–1.1s life. Floating "+10 ×2" score text rises in Lilita One with paper-stroke outline, magenta when combo ≥3. **Combo**: builds per consecutive smash, resets on miss-click OR missed-floor OR 1.6s without smash. Multipliers: ≥3 → 1.5×, ≥5 → 2×, ≥8 → 3×. Best combo tracked, shown on game-over. **Levels**: every 18 smashes triggers `state.level += 1`, spawn rate `×1.22` (capped 4.2/s), gravity `×1.12` (capped 720). Big "LV N" banner appears mid-screen for 1.4s, 4-note triangle arpeggio plays (G/C/E/G — 392/523/659/784Hz). Lv 3+ has 18% chance of double-spawn 120ms after primary. **Lives**: 3 hearts as `.lives-pip` red circles in the top-right HUD cell. Miss = -1 life + red MISS toast at floor + persistent green floor splat (3.5s decay). 0 lives → game over overlay, save best score to `localStorage['celery-smash-best-v1']`. **Audio** (Web Audio synth, deduped 30ms): smash = bandpass-noise burst (1700+Hz, Q=1.6, 90ms exp decay) + 190→58Hz sine sub thud + 620Hz triangle leafy chirp. Miss = 260→78Hz sawtooth descent through 1.4kHz lowpass. Level-up = G-C-E-G triangle arp. Game over = G-Eb-B-G descending sawtooth through 900Hz lowpass. **HUD**: green-tagged stat cells (Score in gold Lilita One, Lv in lime, Lives as red pips), aria-live on score, COMBO×N banner centered just below HUD when ≥2. **Aesthetic**: cream-paper bg with multi-angle linear-noise overlay at multiply blend, green grass-tinted stage (gradient #d7e9b0 → #84a84e), dotted grass texture, bottom hazard tape stripe in red+ink diagonals. Title "celery·smash" in Bungee 4xl with stacked green drop-shadows (light-green → leaf → dark-stalk) plus a red "smash" outline. Fraunces italic tagline "tap the stalks. *save the floor.*" FARM TO FACE · LOT №12 stamp top-left. Panel buttons: Pause/Restart/Sound toggle. Overlays: intro + game-over cards with paper bg + thick ink border + 8px drop-shadow offset, big-num final score in Hot 3.6rem Lilita One with stalk-light shadow. **Keys**: Space pause, R restart, M mute. **Accessibility**: rem units throughout, ≥4.5:1 contrast, role=application on canvas with summary aria-label, role=status+aria-live on score, role=dialog+aria-modal on overlays with aria-hidden toggling, ≥44px touch targets, focus-visible orange outline, prefers-reduced-motion kills transitions. **Mobile**: pointerdown unifies mouse + touch, no double-tap zoom (touch-action: none), HUD cells flex-wrap. Pollinations OG (909 seed). Fonts: Bungee + Lilita One + Fraunces italic + Space Mono.

## issues
- Hit test is rotated-rect with +14/+12 padding — slightly forgiving on touch but still tighter than a finger pad. Could expand to padding-with-circle or just make padW = w*1.5 if mobile users complain.
- Combo resets on miss-click on empty canvas — punishes spam-clickers. Intentional but might frustrate. Could toggle to "miss-click is free" if requested.
- Stalks bounce off the wall pins (x clamped to [16, W-16]) which can make them slide along the edge for a beat. Looks fine.
- Sound dedupe (30ms) means simultaneous smashes during a chain only play one crunch — keeps the compressor from clipping but loses some intensity at high combo.
- Particles only do floor-bounce check, not stalk-on-stalk. Anvils… er, celery don't interact with each other.
- Level cap is effectively 24 (gravity hits 720 and spawn 4.2 around then) — beyond that it's the same difficulty plateau. Good enough for a 1-minute game.
