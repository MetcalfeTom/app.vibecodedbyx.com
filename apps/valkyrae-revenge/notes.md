# Valkyrae's Revenge

## log
- 2026-01-07: Initial creation - high-stakes arena survival game
  - Valkyrie warrior with wings, spear, and golden armor
  - Wave-based enemy spawns (draugr, berserker, wraith, jotun)
  - Combo system with multipliers
  - Soul collection for scoring
  - Power-ups (health, speed, damage)
  - Norse aesthetic with runes

## features
- Valkyrie protagonist with animated wings
- Spear attack with knockback
- 4 enemy types with different behaviors
- Wave-based progression
- Boss enemies every 5 waves
- Combo system with score multipliers
- Soul drops from enemies
- Power-up drops (health/speed/damage)
- Blood splatter effects
- Health bar and invincibility frames

## controls
- WASD / Arrow keys: Move
- SPACE / Click: Attack with spear
- Mouse: Aim direction
- Mobile: Touch buttons

## enemy types
- Draugr: Basic undead, balanced stats
- Berserker: Fast and strong, medium health
- Wraith: Very fast, low health, moderate damage
- Jotun: Boss enemy, slow but devastating

## design
- Norse mythology aesthetic
- Gold and dark blue palette
- Uncial Antiqua / Cinzel fonts
- Rune circle arena
- Blood splat persistence
- Particle effects

## technical
- Canvas 2D rendering
- Entity-component style enemies
- Collision detection
- Knockback physics
- Combo timer system
- Wave spawning system
