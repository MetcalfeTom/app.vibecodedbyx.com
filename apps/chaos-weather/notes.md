# Chaos Weather

Satirical weather app where pianos, anvils, cats, and fish fall from a neon sky with lightning and wind effects.

## log
- 2026-04-11: Initial build. Canvas-based falling object simulation with 5 modes (pianos, anvils, cats, fish, chaos/all). Neon gradient sky shifts hue over time, random lightning bolts with flash effect, horizontal wind streaks. Each object type hand-drawn on canvas (piano with keys, anvil with ACME text, cat with surprised face, fish with scales). Impact effects: debris particles + expanding shockwave ring. Scrolling news ticker with absurd weather alerts. Forecast card with rising temperature counter. Stats bar tracking objects fallen, impacts, chaos level. Bungee Shade + Overpass Mono typography.

## features
- 5 weather modes: pianos, anvils, cats, fish, chaos (all at once)
- Hand-drawn canvas objects with detail (piano keys, cat expressions, fish scales)
- Neon gradient sky that shifts hue continuously
- Random lightning bolts with screen flash
- Wind streaks effect
- Ground impact: debris particles + shockwave ring
- Scrolling news ticker with absurd weather alerts
- Live stats: objects fallen, impacts, escalating chaos level
- Temperature rises over time
- Object cap (80) to prevent slowdown
