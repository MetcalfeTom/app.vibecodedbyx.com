## features
- 3D forest clearing scene
- Fox den mound with glowing entrance
- Stylized fox with:
  - Orange body, white tail tip
  - Glowing eye reflections
  - Breathing animation
- 12 neon trees with:
  - Glowing bark veins (cyan/green)
  - Translucent canopy layers
  - Floating glow particles
  - Point lights
- 200 glowing grass blades
  - Swaying animation
  - Multiple colors
- 8 bioluminescent mushrooms
  - Various colors (magenta, cyan, green, orange)
  - Pulsing glow effect
  - White spots on caps
- 50 fireflies
  - Floating paths
  - Blinking glow
  - Various colors
- Orbit camera (drag to rotate, scroll to zoom)
- Auto-rotate when idle
- Touch controls for mobile

## design
