# Prophetic Balance

Walk on water by pressing rhythm keys in time with the beat. Lose your rhythm, lose your faith, and sink into the deep.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. 4-key rhythm system drawn from 7 possible keys (A/S/D/F/J/K/L), keys shuffle every 30 steps. Beat timing grades (Perfect/Great/Good/Late) with faith gain/loss per grade. Faith bar decays over time, faster at higher BPM. BPM starts at 72, increases 0.5 per step up to 160. Sinking animation when faith drops below 20%. Figure with robe, halo (brightness tied to faith), arm sway synced to BPM. Water rendering: wave lines, caustic light, ambient ripples, moon with reflection. Footstep ripples on successful steps. Spiritual blessing messages every 15 steps. Beat visualization with scrolling dot. Touch buttons for mobile. Best distance in localStorage. Cormorant Garamond + DM Mono typography, deep ocean night aesthetic.

## features
- 4-key rhythm system from 7-key pool
- Beat timing grades (Perfect/Great/Good/Late)
- Faith bar with natural decay
- BPM increases with distance (72→160)
- Key pattern shuffles every 30 steps
- Sinking animation on low faith
- Robed figure with golden halo tied to faith
- Arm sway synced to BPM
- Water waves, caustics, moon reflection
- Footstep ripples
- Spiritual blessing messages at milestones
- Beat dot visualizer
- Touch controls for mobile
- Best distance persistence (localStorage)
