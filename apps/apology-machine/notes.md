# The Apology Machine

It called you fat. It is deeply, profoundly, theatrically sorry. Every visit generates a unique, overly dramatic apology letter.

## log
- 2026-04-12: Initial build. Procedural apology letter generator. 6 paragraph sections assembled from randomized pools: 10 salutations, 8 openings, 4 incident descriptions, 5 excuse denials, 6 body praises, 6 suffering descriptions, 5 promises, 8 closings, 10 P.S. lines. "Escalate Remorse" button adds extra paragraphs of escalating guilt (5 variants). Apology counter in localStorage tracks total apologies. Formal letter layout on cream paper with border, watermark "SORRY", rotating stamp badge. Random teardrop stains (div circles). Escalation increases tear count and adds compound-guilt paragraphs. Crimson Pro + JetBrains Mono typography, cream/parchment editorial aesthetic.

## features
- Procedurally generated dramatic apology letters
- 10 salutations, 8 openings, 4 incidents, 5 excuse denials
- 6 body praises, 6 suffering paragraphs, 5 promises
- 8 closings, 10 P.S. lines
- "Escalate Remorse" button for extra guilt paragraphs
- Persistent apology counter (localStorage)
- Formal letter layout on cream paper
- "SORRY" watermark, rotating stamp badge
- Random teardrop stain effects
- Stamp text changes with escalation level
- Scroll-to-top on regenerate
