# 3AM Thoughts

## log
- 2026-07-05: v1 — confessional void journal where text melts. Set your wake time, then type thoughts into a textarea. Hit Enter to release words into the void — they float upward briefly then melt downward with increasing gravity + vertical stretch (meltStretch up to 2.2×). Words track frequency: used 3+ times = trembling with ×N counter, used 5+ times = shatter into individual characters that explode outward with gravity. Drip particles spawn from melting words (teardrop-shaped, hue-matched). Sleep deprivation meter tracks typing speed, typo rate (backspace/3), ramble length, and session time — weighted 35/25/25/15%. Regret timer counts down to your alarm with rotating passive-aggressive judgments ("this was a mistake", "why are you like this", "your alarm will show no mercy"). Background color shifts based on real clock time: midnight blue → sickly fluorescent green-tinge toward 4-6AM. Ambient floating dust motes. Web Audio: sine sweep on thought submit, bandpass noise on word shatter, triangle drip sounds. Nothing is ever saved — all dissolves. Instrument Serif italic + Newsreader + Azeret Mono. Deep indigo/purple/warn-coral palette.
