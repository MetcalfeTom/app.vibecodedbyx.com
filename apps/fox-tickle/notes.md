# Fox Tickle

## log
- 2026-01-16: Initial creation
  - Adorable fox character with animated fluffy tail
  - Falling grapes that tickle the fox on contact
  - Mouse/touch to tickle the fox directly
  - Giggle meter fills up leading to laugh explosion
  - Fluffy particle effects from the tail
  - Laugh bubbles float up when tickled
  - Grape splash effects
  - Sound effects (giggles and pops)
  - Score tracking for grapes caught and tickles
  - Baloo 2 + Grandstander fonts
  - Feather cursor

## features
- Canvas-based fox rendering with gradient shading
- Animated fluffy tail that wags faster when tickled
- Falling grape clusters with rotation
- Catch grapes by moving cursor over them
- Tickle the fox by hovering over it
- Giggle meter fills to 100% for laugh explosion
- Fluffy fur particles
- Blush effect when tickled
- Happy/laughing expressions
- Web Audio API sound synthesis

## controls
- Mouse/Touch: Move over fox to tickle
- Move over grapes to catch them
- Grapes that hit the fox also tickle it
