SOCRATES
I know only that I know nothing… about who has my chopstick.

Each philosopher needs both chopsticks — left and right — to eat, and picks up the left one first. When all five grab their left stick at the same moment, nobody can ever get a right one: that's a deadlock, and this table does nothing to prevent it. Click any philosopher to make them put their stick down and break the cycle — congratulations, you are now the operating system.
