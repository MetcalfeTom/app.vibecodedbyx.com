# mochi-cat

## log
- 2026-05-01: Created. **uwu cat clicker — kawaii pink-neon clicker game with a chibi pink cat that reacts to taps and evolves through 6 visual stages.** Single-file vanilla JS. **Core loop**: click the cat → gain ♡ pets (1 per click base, scaled by upgrades + multipliers) → spend ♡ on upgrades → cat evolves visually as lifetime ♡ crosses thresholds. **Cat sprite is fully procedural canvas** (no images): pink chibi body w/ radial gradient highlight, separate head circle, 2 triangular ears w/ inner pink, sparkle eyes (white sclera + pink iris + 2 white sparkles), uwu mouth (CSS bezier `w` shape), pink cheek blush, paws w/ pink toe beans, wagging tail (sin-wave), squash-bounce scale on click. **Cat reactions per click**: bounce 1→0.86 decay, blush bumps to 1, mouth opens (with tongue), happy face for 420ms (eyes squint to `^^` smile arcs), tail wags faster, blink suppressed. **Blink** every 4–8s, ~120ms keyframe with `eyeH` lerp from 12→3. **Evolution stages by lifetime ♡**: 0 = kitten, 1k = bowtie cutie (pink bow drawn on left ear w/ knot dot), 10k = sparkle puff (3 animated 4-point amber sparkles around face), 100k = crown princess (5-point amber crown w/ pink jewels stroked above head), 1M = angel neko (golden halo ellipse + small white wings flanking body), 10M = cosmic mochi (3 mint-cyan rotating orbital ellipses around the cat). **Upgrades** (10 total — chat specifically requested cozy passives): 🐾 PAW POWER (+1 click ♡/lvl, base 15), 🛋️ COZY PILLOW (+1 ♡/sec, base 40 — soft napping), 🧶 YARN BALL (+1 ♡/sec, base 90 — playful kicks), 🥛 WARM MILK (+3 ♡/sec, base 280 — cozy bowl), 🍣 SUSHI (+5 ♡/sec, base 600 — happy belly), 💋 KISSU (×1.05 click multi, base 1.5k grow 1.5), ✨ STARDUST (+25 ♡/sec, base 7k — sparkle dreams), 🌸 SAKURA NAP (+60 ♡/sec, base 24k — blossom dreams), 👑 PRINCESS (×1.15 global multi, base 60k grow 2.0), 👻 NEKO SPIRIT (+150 ♡/sec, base 180k — ghost purrs). All exponential cost scaling `base × grow^lvl`. **Click value formula**: `(1 + paw_lvl) × 1.05^kissu × 1.15^princess × (mega ? 5 : 1)`. **CPS formula**: `(pillow + yarn + 3·milk + 5·sushi + 25·stardust + 60·sakura + 150·spirit) × 1.15^princess × (mega ? 5 : 1)`. **MEGA UWU MODE**: every 50 clicks accumulates a charge (capped at 5). When you have ≥1 charge AND no active mega, the next click consumes a charge and starts an 8s burst that 5×s both click value AND cps + sets a remaining-time gold ring at the bottom of the canvas + flashes a screen-center "MEGA UWU ✨" banner in Bagel Fat One that pops in 0.6→1.15→1 with rotation. **Visual reactions** in stage coords: floating `+N ♡` text in candy white that floats up + scale-rotates + fades over 1.2s; expanding pink ripple from tap point; 25%/click chance of a bonus floating word ("nya~", "uwu", "♡", "mrrr", "purr", "✨") spawning ~80ms after the score pop. **Scene aesthetic**: deep indigo→violet→hot-pink vertical gradient bg + 2 layered fixed `body::before/::after` sparkle pseudo-elements with 7-stop radial-gradients tile-panning vertically over 50s/90s with reverse direction for parallax depth. Glassy cards w/ backdrop-filter blur(8px), 2px hot-pink border, 28px radius, pink box-shadow + inset pink glow. **Typography**: Bagel Fat One (chunky kawaii display) for title + counter + reactions, Zen Maru Gothic (rounded Japanese gothic) for body + buttons, Fredoka (rounded geometric) for taglines + meta, DotGothic16 (pixel) for stat lines + footer + mega-mode timer. **Title** "mochi cat · uwu clicker · ♡ ♡ ♡ ·" w/ pink/hot-pink layered text-shadows + 3px offset 3D, rotated -2deg with the small subtitle rotated +2deg for that hand-drawn lean. **Audio** (Web Audio synth, ♪ toggle bottom-right): pet click = 660Hz triangle pluck + 1.4× harmonic 25ms after, mega click = 4-note ascending bell arpeggio (880/1175/1568/1976), buy = 540→810Hz two-tone confirmation, mega trigger = 5-note major-pentatonic chord arp (392/494/587/698/880), error = 180Hz sine. **Persistence**: full state via `localStorage['mochi-cat-v1']` JSON — pets/lifetime/clicks/upgrade levels/mute/megaCharges. Saves on every 12 clicks + every upgrade buy + every 10s tick (so passive idle gains aren't lost on close). **Controls**: tap or click cat (mouse + touchstart), Space/Enter pet, ↻ reset wipes localStorage with confirm. **HUD**: top-right pill shows lifetime ♡ + current stage emoji + name; main panel shows huge Bagel Fat One pet counter w/ bump animation on increase + tagline "pets · ♡" + live "+N ♡/click · M/sec idle" rate line in DotGothic16. Mobile responsive: panel stacks below cat at ≤880px, canvas auto-DPR via `Math.min(2, devicePixelRatio)`, touch-action manipulation on cat for instant click feedback. Pollinations OG.

## issues
- Click value scales exponentially via Princess (×1.15/lvl) AND Kissu (×1.05/lvl). With both maxed (10 + 20) you'd hit (1.15^10 × 1.05^20) ≈ 4.05× × 2.65× ≈ 10.7× click multiplier — combined with PAW L60 (+60 base) that's 651 ♡/click. Probably a bit too multiplicative — could rebalance Kissu down.
- Mega UWU charges accumulate even while mega is already active, but the consume-on-next-click path requires `!wasMega` so they queue rather than overlap. Acceptable, players probably won't notice the queueing nuance.
- Cat sprite is drawn fresh every rAF frame — even when idle. Could skip redraws when nothing has changed (no bounce/blush/mouth/blink active) to save mobile battery. Cosmetic perf only.
- Lifetime ♡ drives evolution but spending pets doesn't lower it (intentional). However, ♡/click and ♡/sec rate counters update from current `clickValue()` / `cps()` — if Princess is bought, both jump immediately even before any new clicks. Expected behavior, just noting.
- Save format `mochi-cat-v1` doesn't preserve lastSaveT, so passive earnings while the tab was closed are NOT credited (only the open-tab passive ticker accumulates). Could add an offline-progress catch-up on load.
