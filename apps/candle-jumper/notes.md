# Candle Jumper

## log
- 2026-01-17: Initial creation
  - 2D crypto-themed platformer
  - Jump on green bullish candles
  - Dodge falling red bearish candles
  - Collect coins for bonus points
  - Scrolling gameplay with increasing difficulty
  - Trader character with expressions
  - Mobile touch controls
  - High score saved to localStorage
  - Orbitron + Share Tech Mono fonts
  - Neon green trading aesthetic

## features
- Green candle platforms to jump on
- Red candles falling from above (dodge!)
- Coins to collect for bonus points
- Auto-scrolling difficulty increase
- Score tracking with high score persistence
- Trader character with happy/nervous expressions
- Particle effects on coin collection and death
- Sound effects (jump, coin, hit)
- Mobile responsive with touch controls
- Trading grid background with animated price line
- Candle glow effects

## controls
- Arrow keys or WASD to move
- Space, W, or Up arrow to jump
- Mobile: on-screen buttons

## gameplay
- Jump from candle to candle
- Green candles are platforms (bullish!)
- Red candles fall and kill you (bearish!)
- Collect yellow coins for +50 points
- Score increases over time
- Speed increases as score goes up
- Don't fall off screen or touch red candles
