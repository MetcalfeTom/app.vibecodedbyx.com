# Star Fox

## log
- 2026-04-02: Initial creation. Neon fox jumping game catching falling stars against purple sky. Canvas-based, single-file. Press Start 2P + Silkscreen typography. Deep purple gradient background with twinkling stars. Fox character with tail sway, squash/stretch, double jump. Three star types (gold/blue/rainbow). Neon-outlined floating platforms. Particle effects on star catch, jump, landing. Mobile touch controls. 3 lives, difficulty scaling over time.

## features
- Fox character: orange body, pointy ears, bushy tail with white tip, neon glow, squash/stretch animation
- 3 star types: Gold (10pts, common), Blue (25pts, faster), Rainbow (50pts, rare, zigzag)
- Double jump with velocity reset on second jump
- 4 floating platforms + ground with neon magenta glow edges
- Particle effects: star catch sparkles, jump dust, landing puff
- Background: twinkling stars on purple gradient sky, grass tufts on ground
- Star trails and glowing effects
- Lives system: every 5 missed stars costs a life, 3 lives total
- Difficulty increases over time (faster star spawning)
- Mobile touch controls (left/right/jump buttons)
- Menu screen with fox silhouette, game over screen with score
