# neon-cruise-zombies

## log
- 2026-05-08: shipped — top-down zombie shooter set on a 5-deck synthwave cruise ship. Five chat asks bundled into one cohesive build (top-down shooter · cruise ship · neon · tacky tourist zombies · bloated captain boss · playable blackjack · real betting).
  - **5 decks** as ASCII tile maps, each 32×18 tiles, 32px per tile, fixed deck per level (camera centred, world fits in viewport on desktop and pans naturally on mobile via touch joystick): SUNDECK (pool, deck chairs, hot pink) → PROMENADE (boutique boxes, cyan) → ATRIUM (casino slot machines, blackjack terminal, gold) → CABINS (corridor maze of cabin doors, lime) → ENGINE (boss arena, red). Each deck has its own accent colour driving wall outlines + arrow glow + floor tinting; mowed-floor parity tiles alternate per (tx+ty)&1.
  - **Tile types**: `#` wall, `.` floor, `~` pool water (impassable + non-LOS), `o` obstacle (slot machine / deck chair / shop case — impassable, blocks bullets), `>` glowing pulsing stair-down arrow → next deck on touch, `T` blackjack terminal (atrium only, opens BJ overlay on E key), `*`/`+`/`S`/`M` ammo/health/shotgun/SMG pickups, `Z`/`R`/`B`/`K` enemy spawns (walker/runner/bloater/captain).
  - **Player**: cyan disc with white barrel pointer, WASD move, mouse aim, left-click fire, **Space dash** (0.18s burst @ 540 u/s + 0.22s i-frames + 0.85s CD + cyan particle trail), **R reload** (vibe-only, no clip system), **1/2/3 swap weapon**. 100 HP, regenerates +18 between decks. Critical-HP (<30%) the HP pill flashes red.
  - **Weapons**: pistol (16 dmg, 320ms CD, 1 bullet, ∞ ammo), shotgun (12×6 pellets at 0.18 spread, 700ms CD), SMG (8 dmg, 85ms CD, 0.10 spread). Each pellet/bullet sub-stepped 3× per tick to prevent tunnelling at high speed; collision against `blocksBullet` tiles + per-enemy circle hit. Muzzle flash particles per shot in the weapon's color.
  - **3 zombie kinds + 1 boss**: walker `Z` (28 hp, 38 u/s, 8 dmg, green), runner `R` (18 hp, 86 u/s, 6 dmg, hot-pink), bloater `B` (90 hp, 24 u/s, 18 dmg, violet), captain `K` (380 hp, 32 u/s, 22 dmg). All chase via straight-line `tryMove` with corner-sample collision. Per-enemy attack cooldown gates contact damage.
  - **Tacky tourist outfits** (chat ask): every regular zombie gets a baked-once `_decor` blob with a 6-palette Hawaiian shirt (hot-pink hawaiian / tropical lime / sunset pop / cobalt punch / mango blaze / tiki cool — 3 colors each), one of 4 pattern motifs (diagonal stripes / dot speckle / chevron zigzag / floral `+`/`*` glyphs), a 5-colour sun hat (beige/cream/peach), and a 4-colour band stripe. Renderer draws a clipped shirt patch over the lower half of the body inside the body-circle clip, then layers the brim ellipse + dome arc + neon band on top, with red glowing eyes peeking under the brim and a tiny ink-mouth slash.
  - **Captain Greaves** (chat ask): bigger r=16 disc with a pulsing `1.18 + sin(t/180)*0.06` red aura at +14 outer halo, mottled green/grey bloated belly with lime + red rot patches, navy uniform jacket arc on the upper half, **gold trim everywhere** (lapel V, jacket bottom edge, 3 down-the-front buttons, shoulder epaulets, peaked-cap band, scrambled-egg laurel cluster + anchor insignia, sleeve cuff stripes), grey beard stubble, glowing red eyes, "CAPTAIN GREAVES" label in gold above. HP bar is wider (100px) and tinted red instead of lime. On death = victory screen ("★ CAPTAIN DOWN ★" in lime).
  - **Blackjack terminal** (chat ask): atrium tile `T` rendered as a gold-cabinet ATM with a green felt screen pulsing "21" in Press Start 2P. Walk within 1.4 tiles → "PRESS E · BLACKJACK" prompt fades in pulsing yellow at top of screen. Press **E** opens the full-screen casino overlay, pauses the main game (state.phase = 'blackjack'), Esc walks away.
  - **Real betting** (chat ask): chip wallet starts at 100, persists in `localStorage['cruise-zombie-chips']` across sessions. Bet adjuster: +5 / +25 / +100 chip buttons, × clear, all-in. Standard rules: 52-card Fisher-Yates deck shuffled per hand, dealer S17 (stand on soft 17), naturals (21 with first 2 cards) pay **3:2**, dealer hole card hidden until you stand. **Hit / Stand / Double Down** (first 2 cards only, takes one extra card then auto-stands, doubles your wager); **H/S/D keyboard shortcuts** while at the table, Enter or Space to deal/redeal. Per-state result line tints (lime win, pink lose, gold push/blackjack). Card animations slide in from above with a slight rotate on deal. HUD chip pill (`♢ 100`) updates live both during gameplay and during a hand.
  - **Sounds**: synth blip per shot (square pistol / sawtooth shotgun + white-noise burst / square SMG), bandpass-noise hit, sawtooth death, triangle pickup chime, dash whoosh, stair chime (2-note), 6-step descending sawtooth on boss spawn, blackjack triangle blips per card draw + pickup chime on win + noise hit on lose.
  - **HUD**: top bar with deck/HP+bar/ammo/weapon/kills/chips pills in glassmorphic backdrop-blur cards. Deck banner overlays "DECK N · NAME" in deck-accent color with a 0.55s scale-in + 1.6s scale-out keyframe on each transition.
  - **Mobile** (`@media (pointer: coarse)`): 7.5rem virtual joystick lower-left with a hot-pink knob, 5rem FIRE button lower-right (gold), 4rem DASH button (cyan). Touch joystick drives both movement and aim simultaneously (auto-aim points along joystick angle when on touch).
  - **Title overlay**: Audiowide title "NEON CRUISE ZOMBIES" with cyan/pink/lime glow shadows, Press Start 2P controls reference, big lime EMBARK button. Death screen shows "YOU WERE EATEN" or victory "★ CAPTAIN DOWN ★" with kills + deck reached + time elapsed stats.
  - **Accessibility**: rem units, 100% root font-size, semantic `<canvas role="application">` with full mechanic aria-label, skip link, `aria-pressed` on toggle buttons, `aria-live="polite"` on HUD pills, focus-visible white outline on all buttons, ≥2.6rem touch target heights on chip + action buttons, `prefers-reduced-motion` no-ops the bulb pulse, deck banner anim, HP-crit flash, deal-in card animation.

## issues
- ASCII map definition has been edited a few times; one row in deck 1 ends with a stray space (`# `) — harmless because all maps are zero-padded read past index, but cosmetically untidy.
- Captain boss melee chase = same straight-line `tryMove` as regular zombies; on the engine deck (mostly open), it chases predictably. Future: telegraphed lunge attack or a circle-strafe pattern.
- Pickups respawn-on-redeck — collected items stay collected within a deck visit, but warping back via game restart restores them. Acceptable for a single-life arcade.
- Blackjack tracks chips in localStorage, but `Reset` (from the death screen) doesn't wipe chips. By design — chips persist across runs as a meta-progression metric.
- Walking into the terminal AT proximity radius can trigger the prompt while a runner zombie is right next to you. Pressing E enters BJ which pauses combat — could be exploited as a panic-pause. Acceptable given the cost (you stop earning kills + can lose chips at the table).
- No shop yet — chips have no in-game spend conversion (extra ammo, HP heals, etc). Chips serve as a bragging-rights side-score for now.
