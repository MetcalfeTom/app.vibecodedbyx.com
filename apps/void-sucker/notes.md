# Void Sucker

Hold click to vacuum up floating space junk for points. 45-second frenzy.

## log
- 2026-04-10: Initial build. 8 junk types (bolt/gear/chip/spring/crystal/star/bomb/dust) with unique canvas-drawn visuals and point values. Hold-to-suck mechanic with power meter (drains while sucking, regens when idle). Radial pull field with animated spiral lines. Items stretch toward nozzle when being sucked. Streak system: 3+ consecutive = multiplier up to 8x. Bombs reset streak and subtract points. Screen flash on bomb. Vacuum hum via oscillator. Rubik Glitch + Share Tech Mono typography, dark void/cyan aesthetic.

## features
- 8 junk types with unique point values and art
- Hold-to-suck with power meter management
- Radial pull physics with visual field
- Streak/multiplier system (up to 8x)
- Bombs reset streak, subtract points
- Items visually stretch toward vacuum
- Vacuum hum audio (sawtooth oscillator)
- SFX on suck/big item/bomb
- Touch support
- Screen-wrapping items

## issues
- No persistent high scores
- Bombs can be hard to avoid in dense clusters
