# Soul Judge

A cursed digital pet that judges your life choices and music taste.

## log
- 2026-04-10: Initial build. Canvas-rendered cursed orb pet with mood-reactive eyes/mouth (5 moods: bored, disgusted, amused, furious, tolerant). Float animation, particle aura, blink cycle. 4 actions: Feed (food roasting), Music (taste judging), Confess (life choice mocking), Pet (reluctant acceptance). Keyword matching for specific roasts (pizza, ramen, pop, metal, Taylor, Drake, etc) with fallback generic lines. Contempt meter, judgment counter. WebAudio SFX. Pirata One + Vollkorn typography, dark purple cursed aesthetic.

## features
- Canvas pet with mood-reactive expressions (5 eye/mouth styles)
- Float animation with particle aura
- 4 interaction types with text input
- Keyword-matched roasts for foods, music genres, life topics
- 70+ unique judgment lines
- Contempt and judgment counters
- Petting reduces contempt slightly
- Idle judgments when not interacting
- WebAudio SFX per action type
- Mobile responsive

## issues
- No persistence (contempt resets on refresh)
- Limited keyword matching
